AMMO TYPES
----------
  Light  — pistols, SMGs (Ruger, Glizzy, UZI, Tec-9, HV Express)
  Medium — rifles, shotguns (AR-14, M16, Sawed Off, Cruiser 500, Draco)
  Heavy  — explosives, special (RPG, Decimator)


KEY ITEM DEFINITION FIELDS
---------------------------
  gun_class        "small" = sidearm only, "medium"/"large" = weapon slot only
  base_damage      (min, max) tuple — random roll per shot
  gun_range        max Chebyshev distance for targeting
  ammo_type        "light", "medium", or "heavy"
  mag_size         rounds before reload needed
  reload_speed     energy cost to reload (0 = free action)
  firing_modes     dict with "accurate" and "fast" sub-dicts, each has "hit" (%) and "energy" (cost)
  aoe_type         "target" (single), "cone" (spread), "line" (piercing), "circle" (explosive)
  cone_angle       degrees for cone AOE (30 = narrow, 90 = wide)
  ammo_per_shot    (min, max) rounds consumed per fire action (cone guns)
  projectiles      number of pellets for cone/spread guns
  consecutive_bonus  +N damage per consecutive hit on same target
  jam_chance       % chance to jam per shot (0 = never jams)
  jam_clear_cost   energy cost to clear a jam
  mag_size_options list of possible mag sizes (random on spawn)
  reload_per_floor max reloads allowed per floor
  grants_ability   ability ID granted while gun is equipped
