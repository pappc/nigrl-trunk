================================================================================
NIGRL SPELL / ABILITY COMPENDIUM — SUPPLEMENT III
ELEMENTAL & ARCANE SPELLS
================================================================================

This supplement designs 48 spells organized by element/school. These are the
flashy damage spells — fireballs, lightning, ice storms, arcane force — that
make dedicated caster builds feel powerful and distinct from physical builds.

The core design innovation of this supplement is the ELEMENT COMBO SYSTEM: spells
leave elemental conditions on targets (Wet, Burning/ignite, Chill, Shocked,
Brittle, Arcane Mark, Earthbound, Shadowed, Luminous, Poisoned, Windswept) that
interact with subsequent spells for amplified effects. The full chart is at the
bottom of this document.

EXISTING effects leveraged here:
  ignite   — Burning, 1 dmg/stack/turn, stacks. Strips Chill. Wet extinguishes.
  chill    — Slows energy gain by 10 per stack. Strips on Ignite apply.
  shocked  — +15% incoming melee damage per stack (max 5). Stacks on reapply.
  wet      — Extinguishes Ignite on apply. Tags enemy as soaked.
  slow     — Reduces energy ratio. duration and ratio params.
  stun     — Skip AI turn, zero energy gain.
  confuse  — Randomized movement.
  dot      — Generic damage over time.
  fear     — Flee from source. 50% break on damage.

NEW effects introduced (see Implementation Notes per entry):
  brittle  — Physical hits deal +50% damage. 1 charge: consumed by any phys hit.
  frozen   — Full stun + brittle flag. Duration 3-5t. Removed by fire/heat.
  arcane_mark — Next spell targeting this entity deals +BKS flat bonus damage.
  earthbound  — -50% movement energy (ratio 0.5), -2 defense.
  shadowed    — Cannot be targeted by SINGLE_LOS abilities (they mis-aim).
             Cleared by any light/holy effect. (NPC-targetable version of invis.)
  luminous    — +25% incoming damage from all sources. Cleared by Shadowed.
  poisoned    — 2 dmg/turn, defense reduced by 1 per 5 turns (max -3 reduction).
  windswept   — Pushed 1 tile away from caster per turn, auto if open tile.
             Cannot stack; refreshes duration.

PRIMARY STAT SCALING for this school: Book-Smarts (BKS).
  - Base spell damage = stated formula + floor(BKS / 4) flat bonus
  - All spells with "BKS scaling" mean: add floor(BKS/4) to the damage roll
  - At BKS 8 (minimum): +2 bonus. At BKS 12: +3. At BKS 20 (skilled): +5.
  - This is intentionally modest — caster builds want BKS for SP generation
    AND spell damage, making every BKS point doubly valuable.

NOTATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  TARGET:  SELF | ADJACENT | ADJ_TILE | SINGLE_LOS | LINE | AOE_CIRCLE
  CHARGE:  INF (infinite, cooldown-gated) | /FL (per floor) | TOTAL (permanent
           uses) | ONCE | FL_ONLY (per floor, not saved on descent)
  SOURCE:  which skill tree, perk level, or item grants this ability
  STAT:    which player stat scales the damage/effect
  COMBO:   element condition applied OR element conditions that trigger a bonus

  Tier labels: LOW (floor 1-2 accessible), MID (floor 3-4), HIGH (floor 5+)

E-F-01  LIGHTER FLUID  [LOW TIER]
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  NIGRL Name:  "Lighter Fluid" — you pull a little aerosol can and hiss fire
  Source:   Blackkk Magic L1 activated perk  OR  item "BIC Lighter" (single use)
  Target:   SINGLE_LOS  (range 5)
  Charge:   INF (cooldown 6 turns)
  Stat:     Book-Smarts
  Combo:    APPLIES: ignite (3 stacks, 5t)
            REACTS: if target already has ignite, adds 2 stacks instead of 3
                    (refueling the fire accelerates the burn)

  Mechanic:
    Fire a ranged ignite bolt. Deals 3 + floor(BKS/4) immediate fire damage,
    then applies ignite (3 stacks, 5 turns) to the target.
    If the target already has the ignite effect active, instead add 2 stacks to
    the existing effect and refresh the duration to max(current, 5). Message:
    "Lighter Fluid feeds the flames! (+2 stacks)"
    If target has Wet: wet is stripped, ignite fails to apply. Message:
    "The water douses your lighter fluid!"
    If target has Chill/frozen: chill is stripped, ignite applies normally.

  Why:      The lowest rung of fire offense. Good enough on its own; rewarding
            when you stack with a second fire source (existing firebolt, ignite_spell,
            Jungle Boyz strain). Creates the foundation of ignite-stacking builds.
  Flavor:   "Aerosol + flame = simple math. You didn't need chemistry class
             for this one."
  Notes:    execute(engine, tx, ty): resolve to monster at (tx,ty); apply
            immediate damage via entity.take_damage(dmg); then apply_effect(
            entity, engine, "ignite", duration=5, stacks=3) which triggers the
            existing IgniteEffect.on_reapply path if already present.
            ABILITY_REGISTRY entry: TargetType.SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS, ChargeType.INFINITE,
            max_charges=None, cooldown=6.

E-F-02  BACKDRAFT  [MID TIER]
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  NIGRL Name:  "Backdraft" — a sudden pressure blast of superheated air
  Source:   Blackkk Magic L3 activated perk  OR  item "Fire Extinguisher" (ironic
            — turns out those things can explode if you heat the canister first)
  Target:   AOE_CIRCLE  (radius 3, centered on target tile)
  Charge:   /FL 2
  Stat:     Book-Smarts
  
  Combo:    APPLIES: ignite (2 stacks, 5t) to all entities in radius
            REACTS: if any entity in radius already has 3+ ignite stacks,
                    trigger FLASHOVER (see below)

  Mechanic:
    All living entities in AOE radius (including player if present) take
    4 + floor(BKS/4) fire damage and receive ignite (2 stacks, 5t).
    Ignite interactions: Wet entities have wet stripped; ignite fails on them.
    Chill/frozen entities: chill stripped, ignite applies.

    FLASHOVER: If any entity in the radius enters the tick with 3 or more ignite
    stacks, those entities instead take an additional 6 flat fire damage from
    the pressure wave igniting the concentrated heat. Message: "Flashover! The
    accumulated heat explodes!"
    Player is NOT immune to Flashover — catching yourself in a flaming room is
    your problem.

  Why:      The AOE fire tool. Forces ignite-stack building as a prelude: light
            a target or two with Lighter Fluid or firebolt, then Backdraft the
            room for a Flashover spike. High floor count (2/floor) means
            selective use — you can't spam it.
  Flavor:   "You crack the seal on something pressurized and hot. The room
             decides it's not a room anymore; it's an oven."
  Notes:    execute(engine, tx, ty): collect all entities in Chebyshev radius 3
            of (tx,ty). Deal damage + apply ignite. Post-damage, check each
            entity's ignite stacks and add bonus damage if >=3. Message logged
            per Flashover entity.
            AOE_CIRCLE radius=3 in AbilityDef.

E-F-03  NAPALM STRIKE  [HIGH TIER]
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  NIGRL Name:  "Napalm Strike" — classic. You call it in. It arrives.
  Source:   Blackkk Magic L5 activated perk  OR  Pyromania L4 activated perk
  Target:   AOE_CIRCLE  (radius 2, but leaves PERSISTENT fire terrain)
  Charge:   /FL 1
  Stat:     Book-Smarts + Strength (STR adds ignite stacks)
  Combo:    APPLIES: ignite (3 + floor(STR/3) stacks, 6t)
            PLACES: fire hazard entities on all tiles in radius
            REACTS: if target tile has existing fire entities, stack ignite +2

  Mechanic:
    Immediately deal 6 + floor(BKS/4) fire damage to all entities in radius 2
    and apply ignite (3 + floor(STR/3) stacks, 6 turns). Then call
    create_fire(x, y) for every open floor tile in the radius, creating fire
    hazard entities that persist until the floor resets. Entities that step
    on these tiles receive IgniteEffect per the existing fire hazard system.

    The persistent fire terrain is the unique feature. This changes the map —
    you can use it defensively (make a room impassable) or offensively (herd
    enemies into it). Enemies with meander AI will blindly walk through fire.

    At STR 8: +2 stacks. At STR 12: +4 stacks. Maximum 9 initial stacks via this
    formula (STR 18, hypothetical), but standard cap of 9999 applies overall.

  Why:      A capstone-tier ability that permanently alters the floor. The fire
            terrain created by create_fire() uses the EXACT same hazard system
            already implemented. This costs 1/floor, so placement matters.
  Flavor:   "You didn't invent fire. But you did just add a lot more of it."
  Notes:    Requires hazards.create_fire(x,y) imported in abilities.py (or
            executed via engine reference). After damage+ignite, iterate over
            radius tiles, skip walls and entity-blocked tiles, call create_fire.
            Cross-reference: existing place_fire ability places ONE fire tile;
            napalm_strike places ALL tiles in radius.

E-F-04  PHOENIX COAT  [MID TIER]
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  NIGRL Name:  "Phoenix Coat" — this hoodie is literally on fire. You planned
               this.
  Source:   Pyromania L3 activated perk
  Target:   SELF
  Charge:   /FL 2
  Stat:     Tolerance (TOL — enduring fire)
  Combo:    APPLIES: ignite (2 stacks) to YOURSELF  + PhoenixCoatEffect buff

  Mechanic:
    Apply PhoenixCoatEffect to the player (10 turns). While active:
    - Any entity that deals melee damage to you receives ignite (2 stacks, 4t).
    - You gain immunity to your OWN ignite damage (the ignite is on you but does
      not tick against you for the duration — only ticks against others via
      their damage reflection).
    - You take +50% damage from Wet effects (water damages a burning coat badly).

    When PhoenixCoatEffect expires: strip any ignite stacks off the player.
    Message on apply: "Your coat ignites. Anyone who touches you will know."

    The "you are on fire" aspect is cosmetic — it's the melee reflect that
    matters. High-TOL builds can afford the +50% wet damage. Enemies with ranged
    AI are unaffected (they don't melee). Best against meander/passive_until_hit
    AI types who will whale on you.

  Why:      Completely inverts the defensive paradigm. You WANT to be hit. It
            punishes face-tanking enemies while still requiring you to manage
            the wet vulnerability. Synergizes with Liquid Courage (A-01 from
            Compendium I) which already wants you to stand in melee.
  Flavor:   "You could dodge. Or you could just let them figure out what happens
             when they punch someone who's on fire."
  Notes:    PhoenixCoatEffect: new buff subclass. apply() → apply_effect self
            ignite. on_player_melee_hit() hook applied in REVERSE: engine needs
            to call a defender hook when player is hit by monster. Check if
            engine.player has phoenix_coat; if yes, apply_effect(attacker,
            engine, "ignite", stacks=2, duration=4).
            New hook needed: modify_incoming_damage could serve double-duty if
            attacker reference is passed; alternatively add a new
            on_player_takes_melee_damage hook to Effect base class.

E-I-01  COLD SHOULDER  [LOW TIER]
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  NIGRL Name:  "Cold Shoulder" — attitude so icy it physically manifests
  Source:   Blackkk Magic L1 alt  OR  item "Bag of Ice" (consumable, /FL 2)
  Target:   SINGLE_LOS  (range 6)
  Charge:   INF (cooldown 5 turns)
  Stat:     Book-Smarts
  Combo:    APPLIES: chill (1 stack, 6t)
            REACTS: if target already has chill, apply +1 additional stack
                    instead (Chill.on_reapply handles this already — just fire
                    apply_effect twice conceptually, or pass stacks=existing+1)

  Mechanic:
    Fire a slow projectile at one target. Deals 2 + floor(BKS/4) cold damage
    immediately. Applies chill (1 stack, 6 turns) — each stack reduces the
    target's energy gain by 10/turn. At 3 stacks, a base-speed enemy (100
    energy/turn) gains only 70 energy/turn — effectively 30% slower.

    If target has ignite active: ignite is stripped (existing chill/ignite
    interaction), deal +3 bonus cold damage as the "steam explosion" from
    rapid temperature change. Message: "The sudden cold flash-quenches the fire!
    (+3 damage)"

  Why:      Entry-level frost spell that enables the chill-stack gameplan.
            Simple, safe, and honest. The ignite-strip bonus is a consolation
            prize for bad sequencing or a tool against Wet enemies where you
            can't start with fire.
  Flavor:   "Literally. A cold shoulder. You just pointed a cold shoulder at
             someone and it worked."
  Notes:    Uses existing ChillEffect, which already has on_reapply stacking.
            Immediate damage is direct — entity.take_damage(dmg). Then
            apply_effect(entity, engine, "chill", duration=6, stacks=1).
            If entity had ignite: bonus_dmg=3, entity.take_damage(bonus_dmg),
            message logged.

E-I-02  PERMAFROST  [MID TIER]
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  NIGRL Name:  "Permafrost" — ground freezes under their feet
  Source:   Blackkk Magic L3 alt  OR  item "Dry Ice Bomb" (crafted consumable)
  Target:   ADJACENT_TILE  (fires in a direction; freezes a tile)
  Charge:   /FL 3
  Stat:     Book-Smarts
  Combo:    APPLIES: chill (2 stacks, 8t) + earthbound (6t) to entity on tile
            PLACES: frost terrain flag on tile (flavor, no mechanical effect)

  Mechanic:
    Target an adjacent tile (any non-wall). Any living entity on that tile
    takes 3 + floor(BKS/4) cold damage and receives:
      - chill (2 stacks, 8 turns)
      - earthbound (6 turns): -50% movement energy, -2 defense

    EarthboundEffect: new subclass. modify_energy_gain: return amount * 0.5 if
    used for movement actions (movement costs energy in some implementations;
    if not, just apply slow ratio=0.5 which already exists). Defense: apply()
    saves entity.defense, reduces by 2; expire() restores. on_reapply: refresh
    duration only.

    If no entity is on the tile: spell still fires but has no effect on entities
    (terrain flag set cosmetically). Silent success.

    PERMAFROST TERRAIN: set a dungeon tile property frozen=True on that tile.
    Any entity that enters the tile mid-floor movement gets 1 stack of chill
    for free. Tile thaws after 20 turns or if a fire AOE hits it. This terrain
    interaction is the "medium complexity" feature — cosmetically tracked via
    dungeon.frozen_tiles: set of (x,y) tuples.

  Why:      Battlefield control. ADJACENT_TILE means you're placing it right
            next to you — useful for blocking a corridor approach, slowing an
            enemy that's about to be in range. The earthbound debuff pairs with
            subsequent lightning (shocked) or fire (ignite) for tri-element
            combos.
  Flavor:   "You reach down and tell the floor it's winter now."
  Notes:    ADJACENT_TILE targeting already exists in the system. execute
            resolves entity at (tx,ty) if any, applies effects. Permafrost
            terrain: dungeon.frozen_tiles attribute (new); engine._run_energy_loop
            or movement handler checks if entity tile is in frozen_tiles set,
            applies free chill stack.

E-I-03  ABSOLUTE ZERO  [HIGH TIER]
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  NIGRL Name:  "Absolute Zero" — temperature limit approaching
  Source:   Blackkk Magic L5 alt  OR  Item "Liquid Nitrogen Canister" (TOTAL 3)
  Target:   SINGLE_LOS  (range 8)
  Charge:   /FL 1
  Stat:     Book-Smarts
  Combo:    APPLIES: frozen (full freeze — new effect, see below)
            REACTS: if target has 3+ chill stacks before cast, freeze duration
                    doubled (6t instead of 3t) — reward for chill pre-stacking

  Mechanic:
    A piercing beam of absolute cold. Deals 8 + floor(BKS/4) cold damage.
    Then strips ALL chill stacks and applies frozen (3 turns, or 6 turns if
    target had 3+ chill stacks when hit).

    FrozenEffect (new):
      - Combines full stun (zero energy, skip AI turn — same as StunEffect)
      - Sets a flag entity.brittle = True: the NEXT physical hit against this
        entity while frozen deals +50% bonus damage (the shatter mechanic)
      - If ignite is applied to a frozen entity: frozen is instantly removed
        (heat thaws ice), ignite applies at +3 stacks bonus (the rapid phase
        change creates explosive steam), message: "SHATTERED BY FIRE!"
      - When frozen expires normally: remove brittle flag, message "thaws"

    The freeze is limited to 3 turns (6 with pre-stack) because the shatter
    timing matters — you need a melee ally, a physical ability, or a damage
    source ready. Solo caster: use Absolute Zero, then immediately melee the
    frozen target for the shatter bonus.

  Why:      The payoff for the entire chill-stacking gameplan. Pre-stack cold,
            freeze for massive shatter, or chain into fire for a steam burst.
            The 6-turn extended freeze is genuinely powerful — nearly 3 enemy
            turns completely locked — and requires investment (you spent
            2 prior chill turns).
  Flavor:   "Water vapor in the air turns to snow. The target turns to ice.
             You figure out the next step."
  Notes:    FrozenEffect: new subclass. before_turn returns True (skip AI).
            modify_energy_gain returns 0. on apply: set entity.brittle = True.
            on expire: entity.brittle = False. Need brittle field on Entity
            (default False). In combat.py damage resolution: if entity.brittle,
            deal * 1.5 additional damage and set entity.brittle = False with
            message "SHATTER!"

E-I-04  SLEET STORM  [MID TIER]
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  NIGRL Name:  "Sleet Storm" — you make it miserable outside for everyone
               inside
  Source:   item "Big Gulp (Slushee)" — consuming grants Sleet Storm /FL 1
  Target:   AOE_CIRCLE  (radius 4 centered on player)
  Charge:   /FL 1 (granted by item — consumed on use)
  Stat:     Tolerance (how long you've been drinking frozen drinks)
  Combo:    APPLIES: chill (1 stack, 5t) to all entities in radius
            REACTS: if target already has Wet, deal +4 cold damage and apply
                    +1 additional chill stack (wet surfaces freeze faster)

  Mechanic:
    Radial ice storm centered on the player. Hits ALL entities in radius 4
    (including the player — you are in the storm too). Each entity takes
    1 + floor(TOL/6) cold damage and receives chill (1 stack, 5 turns).
    Player also takes the chill — this is a calculated risk.

    The Sleet Storm does not discriminate: you, your enemies, everyone in the
    room slows down. On a floor full of faster-than-you enemies, this is often
    a net positive (you lose 10 energy/tick; a meander enemy that was already
    gaining 100 now gains 90, still faster, but relatively your mobility gap
    shrinks). Against a room full of the same-speed enemies, it's a wash on
    speed and just deals light AOE damage.

    If any entity in radius has Wet: +4 cold damage, +1 extra chill stack for
    that entity specifically. Perfect setup: use a water ability first, then
    Sleet Storm.

  Why:      The chaos pick. Self-included AOE is the cost. Forces risk/reward
            calculation every time — are you in a position where universal slow
            helps you more than it hurts? Pairs with mobility effects that let
            you act while enemies crawl. Item source (Big Gulp) makes it a
            one-time consumable reward rather than a permanent skill investment.
  Flavor:   "You didn't mean to hit yourself with the sleet storm. You just
             didn't care enough to step out of it first."
  Notes:    execute(engine): collect all entities in radius 4 of player,
            including engine.player. Apply damage and chill to each.
            Big Gulp item: FOOD_DEFS entry, multi-turn eat (2 turns), on
            completion grants ability "sleet_storm" as a /FL 1 instance.
            Or: use_effect {"ability": "sleet_storm"} on item in ITEM_DEFS.

E-X-03  PRISM STRIKE  [HIGH TIER]
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  NIGRL Name:  "Prism Strike" — hits with all elements at once
  Source:   Blackkk Magic capstone cross-school unlock (requires having used
            4+ different element spells at least once this run — tracked via
            engine.elements_used: set of str)
  Target:   SINGLE_LOS  (range 6)
  Charge:   /FL 1
  Stat:     Book-Smarts
  Combo:    APPLIES: all element conditions simultaneously to target
            REACTS: for each element condition already on target, add
                    +floor(BKS/6) bonus damage per existing condition

  Mechanic:
    A convergence of every element. Deals 5 + floor(BKS/4) BASE damage, then
    applies ALL of the following simultaneously:
    - ignite (2 stacks, 4t)
    - chill (1 stack, 4t) — triggers the fire+ice interaction: chill is
      immediately stripped by ignite application. Net: just ignite survives.
      Unless the chill was pre-existing before ignite (then both applied then
      ignite strips chill — same result, no exception needed).
    - shocked (1 stack, 4t)
    - earthbound (3t)
    - poisoned (3t)
    - luminous (3t)
    - arcane_mark (3t)

    PRE-EXISTING BONUS: For each element condition the target already had BEFORE
    Prism Strike hit (counted before applying the new conditions): add
    +floor(BKS/6) bonus damage per condition. At BKS 12: +2 per pre-existing.
    If target had ignite+shocked+poisoned before Prism Strike: +6 bonus damage.

    The ignite+chill interaction resolves after both are applied: chill is
    stripped by ignite, so net result is the target has ignite but not chill.
    Shocked, earthbound, poisoned, luminous, arcane_mark all persist.

    Chill note: if target had pre-existing chill, they're now getting ignite
    which strips it. But for the PRE-EXISTING BONUS count, chill was present
    BEFORE the strike, so it counted toward the bonus even though ignite removed
    it. Snapshot the pre-existing conditions before applying anything.

  Why:      The reward for a fully diversified elemental build. You've invested
            in fire, ice, lightning, earth, poison, shadow, light — now Prism
            Strike pays dividends based on all the conditions you pre-applied.
            The capstone unlock (4+ element spells used this run) means it's
            only available to committed multi-element players.
  Flavor:   "All of it. At once. You figured out how to do that."
  Notes:    engine.elements_used: set tracking which element types have been
            used (add to execute functions per element). Unlock check in
            _can_use_ability or as prerequisite in AbilityDef. Snapshot pre-
            existing conditions before applying: count = sum(1 for eff in
            entity.status_effects if eff.id in ELEMENT_CONDITION_IDS). Apply
            all conditions. Bonus = count * (BKS // 6). Final damage =
            base + bonus.

INTERACTION KEY:

STEAM*      Wet + Fire: Wet is stripped, ignite applies. +3 bonus cold damage
            if applied in reverse (see Cold Shoulder). If simultaneous on
            same target: create a steam cloud (2-tile radius, Wet applied to
            all entities in radius — water vapor).

FREEZE**    Wet + Ice: Wet is stripped. Apply frozen effect (see Absolute Zero
            rules) at half duration (1.5t rounds to 2t). Quick freeze.

SURGE***    Wet + Lightning: Wet stripped, deal DOUBLE lightning damage, apply
            shocked at +2 stacks. The definitive Wet combo (already in
            Static Slap mechanics; generalized here).

Mud†        Wet + Earth: Wet stripped. Target receives Earthbound for double
            duration. "Mudslide" — they're stuck in the muck.

QUENCH‡     Burning + Ice: Burning stripped, no ignite re-applies. +3 bonus cold
            damage (rapid thermal change). See Cold Shoulder.

MARK        Any + Arcane: Arcane Mark is consumed, adding +BKS flat damage to
            this hit. Works for fire, ice, lightning, earth, shadow, holy,
            poison, wind — any element.

Scorch‡‡   Burning + Fire: additional ignite stacks (2+) added. Existing
            IgniteEffect.on_reapply already handles this.

Permafrost§ Chill + Earth: if Permafrost (E-I-02) is placed on a chilled target's
            tile, the Chill duration is extended by 4 turns and they become
            Earthbound immediately (no separate cast needed).

SHATTER     Frozen + Physical hit: entity.brittle consumed, +50% physical
            damage. Frozen also stripped. Works for Gravel Blast, Rockslide,
            Seismic Stomp, melee, guns.

CRACK       Frozen + Lightning: lightning damage applies, frozen strips.
            Target receives +1 shocked stack from the electrical current
            through the ice.

RUBBLE      Frozen + Earth: Rockslide hitting a frozen target consumes brittle
            for double damage AND scatters rubble to adjacent tiles (apply 1
            chill stack to entities on adjacent tiles from the cold flying debris).

BOIL††     Poisoned + Fire: fire ignite stacks cause the poison to boil,
            dealing the poison DOT at 2x rate for 3 turns, then expire.

CONDUIT     Poisoned + Lightning: poison is conductive; shocked application
            gets +1 extra stack if target has poisoned.

SLAM        Earthbound + Earth: second earth hit on earthbound target gets
            the knockback (Seismic Stomp's mechanic generalized). Any earth
            ability hitting an earthbound target triggers pushback.

Consume+    Arcane Mark + any element: mark is consumed for +BKS bonus damage
            on that hit, then re-applied (Arcane Bolt rhythmic cycle).

ECLIPSE     Luminous + Shadow: both strip each other on contact. If target
            has luminous and you apply a shadow effect, luminous is removed
            (no stacking). Vice versa. Whichever was applied last wins.

PURGE       Shadowed + Holy: holy magic strips shadowed. Smite vs. a shadowed
            target strips shadow AND deals +8 purge damage (already in Smite
            mechanic).

INFERNO     Windswept + Fire (Hurricane Round): hurricane fans ignite stacks
            +3. Flying burning targets become miniature fire tornadoes.

LIFT        Earthbound + Holy: sacred ground or Mend applied to an earthbound
            entity strips the earthbound (holiness lifts the stone drag).

DRAIN       Poisoned + Shadow: Soul Drain on a poisoned target heals at 1.5x
            rate (poison weakens the spirit's defenses).

CORRODE     Earthbound + Poison: Acid Spit on an earthbound target deals double
            the AcidCorrodedEffect defense shred (-2 defense instead of -1).

HOLY FIRE   Burning + Holy: Smite against a burning target does NOT extinguish
            ignite; instead it amplifies the smite's base damage by the number
            of ignite stacks (floor(stacks/3) bonus, minimum +1).

THAW        Chill + Holy: Mend strips chill (the purge mechanic extends to cold
            via holy warmth).

DRIFT       Chill + Wind: windswept entity with chill moves in the wind
            direction if their tile is open — the chill doesn't prevent
            windswept movement.

SPORE       Poisoned + Wind: Gust against a poisoned target spreads the
            poisoned to all entities between the target's push path and
            their destination (slipstream).

ANCHOR      Earthbound + Shadow: Soul Drain on an earthbound target lasts 2
            extra turns (they can't flee the drain).

GALE        Wet + Wind: Gust against a Wet target pushes them 1 extra tile
            (the water reduces friction). If pushed into another entity,
            deal 2 collision damage to both.

Arc         Shocked + Wind: Gust against a shocked target transfers 1 shocked
            stack to any entity it passes through on the push path (electrical
            arc from the windswept charge).

Venom       Shadowed + Poison: Plague Bolt against a shadowed target's plague
            spread radius increases from 3 to 5 (concealed plague vector).

BALANCE CONSIDERATIONS
================================================================================

DAMAGE BENCHMARKS:
  Floor 1-2 enemies: 10-20 HP. Target: low-tier spells deal 3-5 damage before
  ignite stacks (DOT adds 3-5 more over 3 turns). Fine for pre-health enemies.
  Floor 3-4 enemies: 25-50 HP. Mid-tier spells deal 5-8 direct + conditions.
  Floor 5+ enemies: 50-100+ HP. High-tier spells deal 8-15 direct. Combos
  needed for efficiency.

BKS SCALING CALIBRATION:
  floor(BKS/4): yields +2 at BKS 8, +3 at BKS 12, +4 at BKS 16, +5 at BKS 20.
  floor(BKS/3): yields +2 at BKS 8, +4 at BKS 12, +5 at BKS 15.
  Elementals use /4 as the standard ratio.
  Arcane school uses /3 (slightly higher) to compensate for no element trick.

CHARGE ECONOMY:
  INF with cooldown: good for filler/basic attacks. 4-8 turn cooldowns.
  /FL 2-3: mid-tier workhorses. Guaranteed 2-3 uses per floor.
  /FL 1: high-value decisive moments.
  ONCE/TOTAL: capstone/emergency uses only.

POTENTIAL OVERPOWERED INTERACTIONS:
  - Absolute Zero (freeze 6t) + Rockslide (delay 1t) + brittle consume:
    possible but expensive — requires 2 /FL 1 abilities and 1 turn delay.
    Net result on a boss could be massive. Acceptable for high-investment play.
  - Smite + Luminous: 14+ damage floor at mid-BKS for a /FL 1 shot.
    Consider whether this trivializes bosses. Mitigation: Spotlight uses INF
    cooldown 5t; Smite /FL 1. Both cost spell slots and turns.
  - Umbral Tide + Hurricane Round (fear + windswept): enemies fleeing in
    random directions, also being pushed. Very chaotic. Acceptable — they
    can still deal damage if they walk into you.
  - Plague Bolt spread + crowded room: full room poisoned for free on one kill.
    At 2 dmg/turn for 6 turns = 12 damage per enemy. Not an instant kill;
    requires time. Acceptable.

SKILL TREE INTEGRATION:
  These spells are primarily sourced from Blackkk Magic tree, with cross-unlocks
  to Pyromania (fire), Negromancy (shadow), and item sources. The existing
  Blackkk Magic tree has no assigned perks at all levels — this compendium
  provides a thematic direction: multi-element arcane caster.

  Suggested Blackkk Magic progression:
    L1: Lighter Fluid OR Cold Shoulder OR Static Slap OR Acid Spit OR Gust
        (player picks one element path at L1)
    L2: Arcane Bolt + Life Tap (universal unlocks at L2)
    L3: Element-specific mid-tier (Backdraft/Permafrost/Ball Lightning/
        Toxic Cloud/Vacuum Burst/etc.) + Elemental Catalyst unlock
    L4: Kinetic Shield + Faraday Cage + element specialism cross-unlocks
    L5: Napalm Strike / Absolute Zero / Overload / Arcane Surge / Rockslide /
        Umbral Tide / Plague Bolt / Hurricane Round (pick one at L5)
        + Prism Strike (if 4+ elements used)
