WHITE POWER — 10 Branching Skill Tree Variations
==================================================
Design doc for the NIGRL roguelike (Crack Den / Meth Lab skill).
These are BRANCHING trees with player-choice topology — NOT linear L1-L3.
The player selects one branch at each split and cannot unlock the other.

White Power theme reference:
  - Swagger defense: int((effective_swagger - 8) / 2)
    At SWG 8=0 def, 10=+1, 12=+2, 14=+3, 16=+4, 18=+5
    So +4 SWG = +2 def; +8 SWG = +4 def; +12 SWG = +6 def
  - Player HP: 30 + CON*10. At CON 10: 130 HP
  - Temp HP absorbed BEFORE armor, armor before HP (Temp HP→Armor→HP)
  - Tox damage multiplier: 1.0 + (tox/100)^0.6
    At tox 25 = ~1.16x; tox 50 = ~1.26x; tox 100 = ~1.5x; tox 200 = ~2.0x
  - Tox resistance: % reduction to tox gained (100% = immune for that source)
  - Stat names: constitution, strength, street_smarts, book_smarts, tolerance, swagger
  - Energy: 100/tick base. +30 speed = ~23% faster action rate.
  - ChargeType options: INFINITE+cooldown, PER_FLOOR, TOTAL, ONCE, FLOOR_ONLY

XP source for White Power: resisting toxicity (each point resisted = XP),
killing enemies as a toxicity-immune entity, purging debuffs/tox from self.

Current linear reference (L1-L3):
  L1: Bleached    — +4 SWG, +20% tox resistance
  L2: White Out   — 3/floor: gain 25 tox, +8 SWG/-25% dmg dealt for 50 turns
  L3: Pure        — +4 SWG, double XP from tox resisted

Cross-tree synergies to leverage:
  - Chemical Warfare: tox as resource; Whitewash ability (implied); tox-damage builds
  - Meth-Head: meth as parallel resource; speed stacking
  - Drinking: Alco-Seltzer (remove 50 tox + floor-wide tox immunity); White Gonster (debuff purge)
  - L Farming: Swagger synergy (both boost SWG); Armor Up interaction
  - Mutation: rad/tox dual resistance options
  - Alcoholism: Stash Finder provides frequent consumable supply for tox management

Shape glossary:
  Y-split (L3)        — L1, L2 trunk; branch at L3: L3a/L4a vs L3b/L4b. 6 perks, 2 capstones.
  Diamond             — L1 shared; L2a/L2b split; L3a/L3b; L4 convergence; L5a/L5b final split.
  Triple Fork (L4)    — L1, L2, L3 trunk; split at L4 into L4a/L4b/L4c. 6 perks, 3 capstones.
  W-shape             — L1/L2 shared; L3/L4 dual branches; L5 convergence; L6a/L6b final split.
  Long Trunk Y        — L1-L4 shared trunk; late branch at L5 into L5a/L5b.
  Inverted Y          — TWO starting branches (L1a/L1b); each with L2; converge at shared L3-L4.
  Ladder/Zigzag       — L1 shared; L2 choose A/B; L3 shared; L4 choose A/B; L5 shared capstone.
  Trident             — L1-L2 shared; L3a/L3b/L3c split; L3a+L3b reconverge at L5; L3c stays solo.
  Double Diamond      — L1 shared; L2 split; L3 convergence; L4 split; L5 capstone.
  Asymmetric Y        — L1-L2 shared; split at L3: Branch A = L3a only; Branch B = L3b+L4b+L5b.

New effects needed (all 10 variations combined):
  WhiteOutEffect (existing/L2 trunk in V1), SterileFieldEffect, BlindingWhiteEffect,
  PorcelainSkinEffect, ScourEffect, TempleEffect, DivineShieldEffect,
  AbsolutePurityEffect, ImmaculateEffect, ImmuneWindowEffect

New ability IDs (all 10 variations combined):
  sterilize_field, bleach_bomb, pristine_shout, cleansing_wave, purge_self,
  alabaster_skin, chalk_line, ivory_tower, void_purity, lye_bath,
  immaculate_conception, scour_b, whitewash_aura, divine_armor, bleach_the_earth


==========================================================================
VARIATION 1: "THE MONUMENT" — Shield vs. Strike
==========================================================================
Shape: Y-split (L3)
Fantasy: Shared trunk stacks raw Swagger to absurd levels. Split between
  Branch A — pure stone-wall defense (temp HP shields, damage reduction)
  Branch B — converting Swagger defense into lethal counterforce

TREE DIAGRAM:

    L1: Bleached
          |
    L2: Ivory Skin
         / \
        /   \
  [BRANCH A]  [BRANCH B]
  L3a: Porcelain  L3b: Gleaming Edge
       |                  |
  L4a: Untouchable   L4b: Blinding White
   (capstone)          (capstone)

TRUNK:

L1 — BLEACHED (stat + passive)
  Perk type: stat + passive
  Stat bonus: swagger +4
  Passive: +25% toxicity resistance.
  Desc: "First coat. You're cleaner than them already."
  Balance: Matches existing L1. Starting point for all Variation 1 builds.

L2 — IVORY SKIN (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: At the start of each floor, gain Temp HP equal to your Swagger
          stat (e.g., SWG 12 = 12 Temp HP). This Temp HP persists until
          consumed and does not regenerate mid-floor until a new floor is
          reached. Temp HP counts toward the Temp HP → Armor → HP
          absorption order.
  Desc: "Walk in wearing armor nobody can see."
  Balance: At base SWG ~12 after L1, grants 12 Temp HP/floor. Scales
          strongly with Swagger investment. Does not stack across floors.

BRANCH A — The Wall

L3a — PORCELAIN (passive)
  Perk type: stat + passive
  Stat bonus: swagger +4, constitution +2
  Effect: Each point of Swagger above 12 grants +1 to your Ivory Skin
          Temp HP pool (additive with the base = SWG amount). Additionally,
          whenever you take melee damage equal to or greater than your
          Swagger, reduce the NEXT incoming hit by 3 (flat, once, resets
          after triggering). Basically a reactive armor layer.
  Desc: "They chip but they can't break through."
  Balance: At SWG 16+: 16 base + (16-12)=4 bonus = 20 Temp HP/floor.
          Reactive -3 hit softens spike damage.

L4a — UNTOUCHABLE (activated + passive) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: activated + passive
  Ability: alabaster_skin
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (2/floor)
  Target type: SELF
  Effect: 2/floor. Activate to enter an Untouchable state for 8 turns.
          While active: incoming melee damage is reduced by your
          swagger_defence value a SECOND time (i.e., applied twice).
          Also passive: if you end a floor having taken no more than 5
          total damage (after all reductions), gain +2 permanent Swagger.
          This permanent gain has no cap — it accumulates run-wide.
  Desc: "2/floor. Eight turns where the math doesn't add up in their
         favor. Keep it clean."
  Balance: Double-swagger_defence on a SWG 16 build = -8 per hit instead
          of -4. Against floor 3 enemies hitting for 12, that's 4 damage
          per hit. The permanent SWG gain is a long-term run reward for
          cautious, defensive play.
  Synergy: L Farming's Armor Up (35% block chance) + Untouchable's
          double swagger_defence = nearly unhittable 8-turn windows.

BRANCH B — The Blade

L3b — GLEAMING EDGE (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Your melee attacks deal bonus damage equal to half your
          swagger_defence value (rounded down). For example, SWG 14 =
          swagger_defence 3, so +1 melee damage. SWG 18 = +2. SWG 24 = +4.
          Additionally, crits refresh your Ivory Skin Temp HP by 3 points.
  Desc: "Polish cuts just as well."
  Balance: At accessible SWG levels (14-18), +1 to +2 melee bonus.
          Not dramatic alone but grows with further SWG investment. Crit
          synergy makes Street Smarts hybrid builds interesting.

L4b — BLINDING WHITE (activated) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: activated
  Ability: blinding_white
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (3/floor)
  Target type: AOE_CIRCLE (radius 3, centered on player)
  Effect: 3/floor. Flash a burst of blinding light. All enemies in radius 3
          are stunned for 2 turns. For 15 turns after activation, your
          Gleaming Edge melee bonus is TRIPLED (still based on
          swagger_defence; at SWG 18 that's +6 extra melee damage per hit).
  Desc: "3/floor. They never see the second hit coming."
  Balance: 2-turn stun in AOE-3 is strong crowd control. The tripled
          Gleaming Edge bonus is roughly +4-6 extra per hit for 15 turns.
          Costs: no stat bonus, relies on prior Swagger investment.
  Synergy: Street Smarts crit chance (STS*3%) — high SWG build can spare
          some STS for crit to proc Ivory Skin Temp HP regeneration on L3b.


==========================================================================
VARIATION 2: "THE CONFESSOR" — Purge vs. Accumulate
==========================================================================
Shape: Diamond (L2 split → L4 convergence → L5 final split)
Fantasy: Two philosophies of purity. A-path: cleanse everything, never
  hold tox, gain power from being clean. B-path: accumulate tox then
  purge it in one explosive moment for massive temp power.

TREE DIAGRAM:

       L1: White Out (revised)
            /    \
           /      \
     L2a: Spotless  L2b: Loaded Barrel
          |              |
    L3a: Clean Slate  L3b: Pressure Cooker
           \            /
            \          /
        L4: Absolution (shared convergence)
            /    \
           /      \
   L5a: Grace   L5b: Eruption
  (capstone)    (capstone)

TRUNK:

L1 — WHITE OUT (REVISED) (activated + stat)
  Perk type: activated + stat
  Stat bonus: swagger +4
  Ability: white_out_r1
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (3/floor)
  Target type: SELF
  Effect: 3/floor. Remove all current toxicity from yourself. Gain Temp HP
          equal to (tox removed / 5) (e.g., 50 tox removed = 10 Temp HP).
          For 20 turns, gain +30% toxicity resistance.
  Desc: "Rinse. Three times a floor, whether you need it or not."
  Note: This reimagines White Out from "gain tox" to "purge tox for shields."
  Balance: Against ambient Meth Lab tox, this is a strong early-game survival
          tool. Temp HP generation scales with how toxic you let yourself get.

BRANCH A — The Clean Path

L2a — SPOTLESS (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: While you have 0 toxicity: all incoming melee damage is reduced
          by 2 (flat). Additionally, kills while at 0 tox grant +2 White
          Power XP (in addition to normal sources). Your White Out
          Temp HP from purging at 0 tox is still 0 — this path rewards
          staying clean, not the purge itself.
  Desc: "Zero tox. Zero tolerance for their nonsense."
  Balance: -2 flat DR while clean is equivalent to 1 extra Swagger defense.
          Incentivizes tox-avoidance over tox-accumulation.

L3a — CLEAN SLATE (activated + passive)
  Perk type: activated + passive
  Ability: clean_slate
  Charge type: INFINITE (40t cooldown)
  Target type: SELF
  Effect: 40t cooldown. Instantly reduce toxicity to 0. For the next 30
          turns after cleaning, any tox you would gain is instead converted
          to Temp HP at a rate of 1 Temp HP per 8 tox avoided (rounded
          down, min 1). This makes you temporarily "immune" while banking
          the would-be damage as a shield.
          Passive: If you are at 0 tox when you kill an enemy that was
          above 50 tox, that kill grants +1 TOL permanently (max +3 total).
  Desc: "40t. Wipe the slate. Anything they throw at you goes somewhere
         better."
  Balance: The 40t cooldown means roughly 1 use per room fight. Perm +TOL
          on "clean kills" makes floor-clearing at 0 tox deeply satisfying.

BRANCH B — The Pressure Path

L2b — LOADED BARREL (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Each point of tox you are carrying above 10 grants +0.5 melee
          damage (rounded down; cap: +15 bonus at 40 tox). This is a flat
          pre-armour bonus additive with weapon power. You take the White
          Power class fantasy sideways — tox is ammunition here.
  Desc: "Fill up first. Then shoot."
  Balance: At 40 tox the cap kicks in for +15 melee — substantial on
          floor 2-3. The penalty: tox damage multiplier at 40 tox = ~1.22x
          incoming. Net risk/reward is meaningful.

L3b — PRESSURE COOKER (passive + activated)
  Perk type: passive + activated
  Ability: pressure_release
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (2/floor)
  Target type: SELF
  Effect: Passive: Tox above 50 grants an additional +5 flat melee damage
          (stacks with Loaded Barrel). Above 75 tox: +3 speed per 25 tox
          (so at 100 tox: +9 speed on top of other bonuses).
          Activated (2/floor): Vent all toxicity. Deal damage to one
          adjacent enemy equal to (tox vented / 2) as a pure toxic slam,
          then reduce to 0 tox. Gain 15 Temp HP from the purge.
  Desc: "Let it build. Let it build. NOW vent it."
  Balance: At 100 tox this is a 50-damage purge hit — exceptional for
          mid-game. But getting to 100 tox while taking 1.5x incoming
          damage is genuinely risky.

CONVERGENCE:

L4 — ABSOLUTION (stat + passive)
  Perk type: stat
  Stat bonus: swagger +4, tolerance +2
  Effect: After any White Out or purge-type activation (White Out, Clean
          Slate, Pressure Release), you gain a 10-turn Absolution window:
          +10% dodge chance and enemies targeting you have -1 accuracy tier
          (they miss more). This applies after both Branch A and Branch B
          purge mechanics, unifying the paths.
  Desc: "Either way, the moment after cleansing — they can't touch you."
  Balance: Dodge chance stacks with existing dodge sources. The -1 accuracy
          tier is conceptual (enemy hit roll is penalised). The convergence
          rewards ANY purge action, making both branches feel the node.

FINAL SPLIT:

L5a — GRACE (passive + stat) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: passive + stat
  Stat bonus: swagger +4
  Effect: While the Absolution 10-turn window is active, your damage
          output increases by 30% (multiplicative after all additive
          bonuses, same pattern as Two Down Ring). Additionally, each time
          you kill an enemy during an Absolution window, extend the window
          by 3 turns (no cap). A clean run with frequent purges can keep
          Absolution active almost permanently.
  Desc: "The clean ones move faster. Hit harder. Stay clean."
  Balance: Requires timing purges before fights. In Meth Lab's ambient
          tox, White Out's 3/floor means 3 Absolution windows + kill
          extensions. A 10+ kill streak inside one window is plausible.
  Synergy: Drinking tree's Slow Metabolism doubles active drink durations —
          if Absolution were a drink buff, it would extend. (Not the same
          system, but Alco-Seltzer can trigger White Out / purge.)

L5b — ERUPTION (activated) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: activated
  Ability: eruption_wp
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (1/floor)
  Target type: AOE_CIRCLE (radius 4, centered on player)
  Effect: 1/floor. Vent ALL your toxicity in a single explosive wave.
          Every enemy in radius 4 takes damage equal to (tox vented / 3,
          minimum 5). Enemies within radius 2 are also stunned for 3 turns.
          After the eruption, you gain 20 Temp HP and 8 Swagger for 30
          turns (+4 swagger_defence). Path B's philosophy: accumulate to
          the brink, then detonate everything.
  Desc: "1/floor. Everything you saved up, all at once. Out there."
  Balance: At 80 tox this deals 26 AOE damage to all enemies in radius 4.
          Paired with Pressure Cooker's buildup mechanics, this is a room-
          clearing nova. The stun in radius 2 ensures safety after firing.
  Synergy: Chemical Warfare's Whitewash mechanic (half tox as shields):
          these two cross-tree capstones both weaponize tox purge but in
          different directions — Eruption fires outward, Whitewash inward.


==========================================================================
VARIATION 3: "THE BAPTISM" — Offense vs. Resistance
==========================================================================
Shape: Triple Fork (L4)
Fantasy: Long shared trunk builds a fortress of Swagger and tox immunity.
  At L4, three distinct capstones represent three extremes of purity:
  A — absolute shield (tank everything),
  B — tox immunity as offensive weapon (apply tox freely, immune to own),
  C — swagger-to-offense conversion (melee is now defense)

TREE DIAGRAM:

    L1: Bleached
          |
    L2: Pristine Body
          |
    L3: Scoured
         /|\
        / | \
       /  |  \
  L4a:    L4b:    L4c:
  White   Plague  Swagger
  Marble  Carrier Strike
(capstone)(capstone)(capstone)

TRUNK:

L1 — BLEACHED (stat + passive)
  Perk type: stat + passive
  Stat bonus: swagger +4
  Passive: +20% toxicity resistance.
  Desc: "First layer. Set the foundation."

L2 — PRISTINE BODY (stat + passive)
  Perk type: stat + passive
  Stat bonus: swagger +3, tolerance +2
  Passive: +20% additional toxicity resistance (total 40% from L1+L2).
          Also: debuff durations are reduced by 1 turn on you (minimum 1).
          Counts as a "debuff reduction" passive, applies after application.
  Desc: "Two coats. The slow rot can't find a way in."
  Balance: 40% tox resistance is meaningful in Meth Lab ambient tox.
          Debuff duration -1 is a minor quality-of-life for all zones.

L3 — SCOURED (activated + stat)
  Perk type: activated + stat
  Stat bonus: constitution +3
  Ability: scour_b
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (3/floor)
  Target type: SELF
  Effect: 3/floor. Remove ALL toxicity and ALL debuffs from yourself
          simultaneously. Gain 1 Temp HP per debuff stack removed (min 1
          per debuff, counting each individual debuff as a unit). For
          example: fear (1 stack) + 2 ignite stacks + 30 tox removed =
          3 Temp HP gained + the tox Temp HP from purge at L1's rate.
          This is the capstone lead-in — it gives all three branches a
          strong pre-capstone cleaning tool.
  Desc: "3/floor. Everything off. Start fresh."
  Balance: Each scour gives 3-8 Temp HP typically. 3/floor is generous —
          this is a defensive anchor for the whole tree.

TRIPLE FORK:

L4a — WHITE MARBLE (passive) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: stat + passive
  Stat bonus: swagger +6, constitution +2
  Effect: Permanent: Your armor value no longer degrades when you take hits
          (it still absorbs damage — it just refills after each hit,
          functionally making armor a permanent per-hit DR). Note: armor
          on the floor already refills between floors; this makes it
          refill per-HIT (permanent per-combat regeneration).
          Additionally: if your current Swagger is 18 or higher, all
          incoming toxicity is reduced by 50% before resistance is applied
          (a multiplicative pre-resistance reduction).
  Desc: "At 18 Swagger, tox bounces. Armor doesn't chip. You just... last."
  Balance: Permanent armor regeneration is powerful but not damage-
          reducing in a nova scenario. At SWG 18 (accessible: L1+L2+L4
          gives +13 SWG, starts at ~8, reaches ~21): the 50% pre-resist
          tox reduction combined with 40% resistance = taking 30% of tox
          gains. Very tanky in Meth Lab.
  Synergy: L Farming's Built Different (+4 SWG, +2 CON) easily pushes
          to SWG 18+ threshold.

L4b — PLAGUE CARRIER (passive) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: You become immune to the toxicity damage multiplier (your own
          tox still shows on your HUD but deals 0% additional incoming
          damage to you). Enemies you hit apply 4 toxicity per melee hit
          (on-hit effect, passive — no additional input required).
          Additionally: enemy tox-based skills (e.g., Covid-26's acid
          spit) are reflected: you take 0 tox but the enemy gains 10 tox
          instead.
  Desc: "You carry it. It eats them. You don't feel a thing."
  Balance: Tox-immunity + on-hit tox application is the fantasy of being
          a clean vessel full of poison. On floor 4+ with 8+ melee hits
          per fight, every enemy will be moderately toxic by end of fight.
          Cross-tree synergy with Chemical Warfare's Incubation Period
          (+15% dmg to 3-consecutive-tox enemies) is exceptional.

L4c — SWAGGER STRIKE (passive) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Your swagger_defence value is added as flat damage to every
          melee attack (in addition to its normal defensive calculation).
          At SWG 20 = swagger_defence 6, so every hit deals +6 bonus
          damage. This is applied pre-armor on the enemy (additive with
          weapon power). Additionally: killing an enemy while your Swagger
          is above 16 grants +1 Swagger for the rest of the floor
          (stacks up to +8 per floor, like Sniping's Dead Eye).
  Desc: "Defense becomes offense. The same number doing two jobs."
  Balance: Starting swagger_defence for this build at capstone: ~6 (SWG
          20). +6 melee damage is roughly 30-50% of a floor-3-4 weapon's
          base damage. The per-kill SWG stacking means late-floor fights
          snowball aggressively.
  Synergy: Drive-By tree's +3 SWG +3 STS provides a cheap 1-perk jump.
          Sniping's Dead Eye stacks with the Swagger Strike kill bonus.

VARIATION 4: "THE WHITEWASH" — Offense vs. Recovery
==========================================================================
Shape: W-shape (L1/L2 shared trunk; L3a/L4a and L3b/L4b branches;
       L5 shared convergence; L6a/L6b final capstone split)
Fantasy: Tox-as-resource economy. Accumulate tox deliberately, convert
  it strategically. Two branch philosophies reconverge into a powerful
  shared node before the final fork between offense and recovery.

TREE DIAGRAM:

  L1: Bleached
        |
  L2: White Out (original)
      /       \
     /         \
 L3a: Chalk    L3b: Ivory Tower
 Line               |
     |          L4b: Fortify
 L4a: Lye Bath      |
      \             /
       \           /
      L5: Purification Ritual (convergence)
           /          \
          /            \
   L6a: Immaculate   L6b: Void Purity
       (capstone)     (capstone)

TRUNK:

L1 — BLEACHED (stat + passive)
  Perk type: stat + passive
  Stat bonus: swagger +4
  Passive: +20% toxicity resistance.

L2 — WHITE OUT (activated + stat)
  Perk type: activated + stat
  Stat bonus: swagger +2
  Ability: white_out_v4
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (3/floor)
  Target type: SELF
  Effect: 3/floor. Gain 25 toxicity on yourself. For 50 turns: +8 Swagger,
          -25% damage dealt. This is the ORIGINAL White Out as trunk,
          preserved exactly. It creates a Swagger surplus at the cost of
          reduced offense — thematic for the diverging W branches.
  Desc: "3/floor. Take the hit. The math gets interesting after."

BRANCH A — The Offensive Tox Path

L3a — CHALK LINE (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: While White Out is active (tox-boosted SWG state), the -25%
          damage dealt penalty is halved to -12.5% (rounded: -12%). This
          makes White Out a genuine offense tool, not a pure tank stance.
          Additionally: melee hits during White Out apply 3 toxicity to
          the target (the tox "bleeds" outward from you).
  Desc: "Half the penalty. All the chalk."
  Balance: Transforms White Out from a pure tank cooldown into a hybrid
          offense/defense buff. The tox-bleed makes enemies easier for
          Chemical Warfare cross-tree synergy.

L4a — LYE BATH (activated) — BRANCH CAPSTONE
  Perk type: activated
  Ability: lye_bath
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (2/floor)
  Target type: SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS
  Effect: 2/floor. Hurl a splash of caustic lye at a visible enemy.
          Deals 4 + (your current tox / 6) damage (min 4, no armor
          penetration but bypasses 50% of enemy tox resistance). The
          target also gains 20 toxicity. If this kill the target, your
          current tox is reduced by 30 (the satisfying "use the poison
          to kill and flush it").
  Desc: "2/floor. Throw what's inside you at them. Both of you come out
         cleaner than before. Well. One of you."
  Balance: At White Out's 25 tox: 4+(25/6)=8 damage. At 75 tox from
          multi-stack: 4+(75/6)=16 damage + 20 enemy tox. On kill, -30 tox
          self-reduction is useful tox management.

BRANCH B — The Tank Path

L3b — IVORY TOWER (stat + passive)
  Perk type: stat + passive
  Stat bonus: swagger +4
  Effect: White Out's -25% damage dealt penalty is converted to a +10%
          damage TAKEN reduction instead (replaces the penalty entirely).
          This path converts White Out's downside into a defense upside.
          Also: +1 armor per 25 Swagger above 8 (so SWG 16 = +1 armor,
          SWG 24 = +2, etc.).
  Desc: "Flip the penalty. Build the tower."
  Balance: +10% DR during White Out is significant on a high-Swagger tank.
          Armor per SWG is slow scaling but permanent.

L4b — FORTIFY (passive + stat)
  Perk type: stat + passive
  Stat bonus: constitution +3, swagger +2
  Effect: While White Out is active, incoming toxicity gains are reduced
          by 75% (from all sources). This means using White Out to gain
          25 tox actually locks you near that level for the duration —
          you can't accumulate further. Good: the 25 tox stays manageable.
          Bad: You can't leverage Lye Bath's tox scaling. Branch B
          commits to tank identity, Branch A to tox offense.
  Desc: "White Out locks in 25 tox. Nothing more. Tower holds."

CONVERGENCE:

L5 — PURIFICATION RITUAL (activated)
  Perk type: activated
  Ability: purification_ritual
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (1/floor)
  Target type: SELF
  Effect: 1/floor. Consume all current toxicity. Gain Temp HP equal to
          (tox consumed / 4). Gain +1 White Power XP per 10 tox consumed.
          For 20 turns: +6 Swagger, +20% toxicity resistance (stacks with
          existing). This convergence node rewards both branches — Branch A
          may have 60-80 tox to consume (large Temp HP burst), Branch B
          will have ~25 (smaller burst but they're tankier already).
  Desc: "1/floor. Burn it all. Come out the other side harder."
  Balance: Branch A at 75 tox: 18 Temp HP + 7 XP + buffs. Branch B at 25
          tox: 6 Temp HP + 2 XP + buffs. Both get value but A gets more
          from the purge, B gets more from raw tank stats.

FINAL SPLIT:

L6a — IMMACULATE (passive) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Immediately after Purification Ritual fires, you enter an
          Immaculate state for 30 turns. While Immaculate: immune to ALL
          toxicity gain, all debuffs expire 2 turns faster per tick (not
          just applied duration — they literally decay faster), and kills
          grant +3 White Power XP. If you remain Immaculate until it
          expires naturally (not cut short), gain a permanent +2 Swagger
          and +1 Constitution (once per floor, up to +6 SWG and +3 CON
          total per run).
  Desc: "After the ritual, thirty turns of nothing touching you. Nothing."
  Balance: Permanent SWG/CON accumulation is a run-length power ramp.
          Perm +6 SWG over a 4-floor run = +3 swagger_defence. Very strong.

L6b — VOID PURITY (passive) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: activated + passive
  Ability: void_purity
  Charge type: TOTAL (3 uses per run)
  Target type: SELF

          -25% damage penalty remains (you're still paying the full cost
          — this branch IS the investment; the payoff comes later). Also:
          +30% toxicity resistance (total 55% with L1).
  Desc: "First layer. More Swagger. Still hurts but the foundation is set."
  Balance: +12 Swagger during White Out = swagger_defence of roughly 7
          at SWG 22. That's -7 per incoming hit, on a 3-activation-per-floor
          basis. Defensive, but the damage penalty is a real burden.

L4b — SECOND COAT (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: The -25% damage dealt penalty from White Out is now halved to
          -12.5% (effectively -12%). Additionally: when White Out expires
          naturally (runs to 0 turns remaining, not purged early), gain
          5 Temp HP. This rewards playing White Out to its full 50-turn
          duration rather than using cleanses.
  Desc: "Second layer. The cost gets bearable. Let it run out, not away."
  Balance: At this point: White Out gives +12 SWG, -12% damage, +5 Temp HP
          on expiry. Per floor: 3 activations = up to +15 Temp HP if all
          run to completion. Significant.

L5b — FINAL COAT (passive + activated) — LONG CAPSTONE
  Perk type: activated + passive
  Ability: final_coat
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (1/floor, additional charge per White Out allowed
               to expire naturally this floor — so up to 3/floor if all three
               White Out windows run to completion)
  Target type: AOE_CIRCLE (radius 3)
  Effect: 1/floor base (up to 3/floor with expiry bonuses). Activate to
          unleash a wave of bleaching force. All enemies in radius 3 take
          6 + (your current Swagger / 4, rounded down) damage and their
          attack power is reduced by 3 for 10 turns (debuffed by purity).
          Your tox is purged. Your White Out cooldown resets immediately
          after Final Coat activates.
          Passive: White Out's -12% damage penalty is further reduced to
          -6% (total after L4b). At this point: White Out costs effectively
          nothing in offense terms.
  Desc: "1-3/floor. Let the coats build up. Then the blast. Swing right
         after. The penalty is down to rounding error."
  Balance: With all three White Out activations running to completion:
          3 Temp HP windfall + 3 Final Coat charges. At SWG 24: each
          Final Coat hit = 6 + 6 = 12 AOE damage + -3 enemy attack for 10t.
          Three room-clears in a floor. The -6% damage penalty is negligible.
  Synergy: L Farming's Unfazed (SWG stacks on taking damage) — White Out's
          extra SWG stacks with any Unfazed-sourced SWG for a massive
          swagger_defence during the White Out window.

FINAL SPLIT:

L5a — ALABASTER SHIELD (activated + passive) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: activated + passive
  Ability: alabaster_shield
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (2/floor)
  Target type: SELF
  Effect: 2/floor. Activate to erect the Alabaster Shield for 15 turns.
          While active: you are immune to ALL incoming damage and effects
          (complete invulnerability window). However: you cannot deal
          damage or use abilities during the Shield (you can move and
          use items). When the Shield expires, gain Temp HP equal to
          (damage that WOULD have hit you during the Shield, tracked but
          nullified). This Temp HP lasts until consumed (cross-floor).
          Passive: White Out now grants +12 Swagger (instead of +8) for
          free (no other changes needed, overrides the +8).
  Desc: "2/floor. Fifteen turns where they're just hitting air. You
         add up what they almost did. Then you start again."
  Balance: Full 15-turn invulnerability is the most powerful defensive
          cooldown in the game. The no-damage constraint is real — pure
          survival/repositioning use case. Temp HP from blocked damage
          can be enormous (vs. a mob at floor 4: 5 enemies hitting 12/turn
          for 15 turns = 900 potential = large Temp HP pool). Balanced by
          the 2/floor cap and the no-attack constraint.
  Synergy: Jaywalking's Dash (Dash 3 tiles instantly) works during the
          Shield window — reposition safely during immunity.

L5b — WRATHBONE SLAM (activated + passive) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: activated + passive
  Ability: wrathbone_slam
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (3/floor)
  Target type: AOE_CIRCLE (radius 2, player-centered)
  Effect: 3/floor. Slam the ground. All enemies in radius 2 take damage
          equal to your FULL Swagger stat (not swagger_defence — the whole
          number, e.g., SWG 22 = 22 damage, ignoring armor). All enemies
          hit are knocked back 1 tile (pushed away from player). For 15
          turns after Wrathbone Slam, your melee damage is increased by
          swagger_defence (the defensive value, additive with attack power).
          Passive: White Out now lasts 75 turns instead of 50 and grants
          +12 Swagger instead of +8 (same upgrade as Alabaster Shield but
          applied to the offensive capstone too — both L5 choices improve
          White Out as part of the long-trunk payoff).
  Desc: "3/floor. Every point of Swagger is a point of damage. Radius two.
         Watch them scatter. Then finish it up close."
  Balance: At SWG 22: 22 AOE damage, armor-ignoring, radius 2. In a 4-enemy
          cluster this is 88 damage total — likely killing 1-2 floor-4
          enemies outright. The knockback prevents flanking. The melee bonus
          afterward (swagger_defence = ~7 at SWG 22) runs for 15 turns and
          makes each hit substantially stronger.
  Synergy: Slashing's Whirlwind + Wrathbone Slam: Slam knocks enemies back,
          then Whirlwind (attack all adjacent) catches the ones still in
          range. Stabbing's Windfury (extra hit chance) during the
          swagger_defence melee bonus window for potentially +7 on each
          extra hit.
