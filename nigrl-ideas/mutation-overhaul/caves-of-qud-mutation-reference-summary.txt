CAVES OF QUD MUTATION REFERENCE - SUMMARY
==========================================

Full reference document: nigrl-ideas/caves-of-qud-mutation-reference.txt

WHAT IT COVERS:
- 24 Physical Mutations with mechanics, scaling, costs, cooldowns
- 24 Mental Mutations with mechanics, scaling, costs, cooldowns
- Morphotypes (Chimera, Esper), Physical Defects, Mental Defects
- 6 key design patterns extracted for NIGRL adaptation
- 5 concrete mutation categories proposed for NIGRL (Combat, Mobility,
  Defense, Utility, Defect) with 20+ specific mutation concepts

KEY DESIGN PATTERNS:
1. Equipment Slot Tradeoffs (Carapace blocks body armor but gives DR)
2. New Gameplay Verbs (every mutation = new thing you can DO)
3. Environment Interaction (fire/ice/acid/electricity interact with terrain)
4. Scaling Beyond Numbers (level thresholds unlock new capabilities)
5. Power-Has-A-Cost (Esper Glimmer attracts assassins)
6. Defects as Meaningful Choices (not just stat debuffs)

MOST NOTABLE MUTATIONS FOR NIGRL INSPIRATION:
- Carapace: Iconic tradeoff (+DR, blocks equipment slot)
- Burrowing Claws: Terrain destruction (dig through walls)
- Domination: Possess enemy bodies (play as them)
- Precognition: Snapshot/revert game state (undo button)
- Temporal Fugue: Create AI-controlled copies of yourself
- Force Wall: Create terrain barriers tactically
- Mass Mind: Power scales with number of nearby creatures
- Corrosive Gas: Physics-based gas that corrodes equipment
- Electrical Generation: Chain lightning through water/metal

PROPOSED NIGRL CATEGORIES:
A. Combat (Acid Spit, Toxic Nova, Bone Spurs, Adrenal Surge)
B. Mobility (Phase Shift, Long Legs, Gecko Grip, Tunnel Digger)
C. Defense (Chitinous Shell, Rubber Skin, Kill-Regen, Adrenaline Glands)
D. Utility (Echolocation, Pheromone Cloud, Radiation Sense, Toxic Metabolism)
E. Defects (Bioluminescent, Brittle Bones, Hemophilia, Paranoid, Unstable)

IMPLEMENTATION: Use Effect subclasses + AbilityDef grants. Existing tier
system (weak/strong/huge) maps to mutation power level.
