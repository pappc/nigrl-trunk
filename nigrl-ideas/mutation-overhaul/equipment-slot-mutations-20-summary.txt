EQUIPMENT SLOT MUTATIONS — SUMMARY
====================================
20 mutations that REPLACE, BLOCK, or CRIPPLE equipment slots with biological
alternatives. Covers all 7 slots: weapon, sidearm, hat, neck, rings, feet, outfit.

Three patterns: REPLACEMENT (mutation IS the gear), BLOCKING (slot locked),
CRIPPLING (slot works but poorly). High-mutation builds trade gear for biology.

WEAK GOOD (26-30):
  26. Bone Blade — replaces weapon with scaling bone weapon (+1 power per 2-3 mutations)
  27. Suction Cups — enhances all rings +1-3 stat bonus, adds 6th ring slot at L2
  28. Padded Soles — replaces feet with silent movement + grease/fire immunity
  29. Prehensile Tail — adds auto-use consumable slot, triggers at low HP
  30. Iron Crown — replaces hat with DR + confuse immunity + L3 confuse aura

WEAK BAD (26-30):
  26. Fused Trigger Finger — cripples sidearm (fire delay, accuracy loss, jams)
  27. Swollen Knuckles — rings hurt you (1-2 dmg per ring per 7-10t), reduced bonuses
  28. Exposed Vertebrae — cripples/blocks outfit, behind-attacks deal +2-6 bonus dmg
  29. Corroded Crown — blocks hat, 20-40% head hits deal +3-7 bonus damage + stun
  30. Brittle Soles — replaces feet with fragile plates, stress fractures on long sprints

STRONG GOOD (26-30):
  26. Organic Holster — enhances sidearm (instant reload, ammo reserve), blocks outfit
  27. Living Armor — replaces outfit with DR that grows per kill (+1 DR per 3-5 kills)
  28. Phantom Limb — adds ghost weapon (+4-8 armor-piercing bonus damage), costs 2 ring slots
  29. Grafted Mask — replaces hat with fear aura (30-50% fear on LOS) + Roar ability
  30. Neural Jack — enhances neck item bonuses +50-100%, auto-examine enemies on hit

STRONG BAD (26-30):
  26. Atrophied Arms — cripples weapon (-3-7 power) AND sidearm (-20-50% accuracy)
  27. Parasitic Caul — blocks hat, parasite feeds -2-4 HP per 10-15t, drops trash items
  28. Overgrown Digits — blocks ALL ring slots + cripples gun; melee slower but +2-4 dmg
  29. Exoskeletal Lock — locks outfit+feet, +5 DR but no diagonals, -20-40 speed
  30. Necrotic Holster — sidearm degrades (gun loses damage over time), ammo spoils on reload

SLOT COVERAGE: weapon(3), sidearm(5), hat(4), neck(1), rings(4), feet(3), outfit(4)
