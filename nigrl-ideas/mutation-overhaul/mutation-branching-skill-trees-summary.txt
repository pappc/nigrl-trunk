MUTATION — Branching Skill Tree Variations Summary
===================================================
Full doc: nigrl-ideas/mutation-branching-skill-trees.txt
10 variations, all 10 branching shapes used (each exactly once).

=== QUICK REFERENCE ===

V1  FREAKSHOW         Y-split (L3)       Mutation farming; quantity over quality OR selective breeding
V2  CHERNOBYL TANK    Diamond            Rad-as-armor; +defense per 50 rad; Branch A=safer, B=rad→HP
V3  SPLICE AND DICE   Triple Fork        Mutation control; tier selection; 3 capstones: Combat/Balance/Splice
V4  VOLATILE EVO      W-shape            Rad berserker; mutation=speed burst; crash damage=power
V5  BODY HORROR       Long Trunk Y       Mutation stacking; mutation_count buffs everything retroactively
V6  FALLOUT PROPHET   Inverted Y         Rad spreader; two origins (passive aura vs. active burst); enemy mutations
V7  SCAR TISSUE       Ladder/Zigzag      Adaptive defense; getting hit = DR; death save capstone
V8  YELLOWCAKE PROTO  Trident            Forced tier manipulation; Strong minimum; three-way fork (control/power/XP)
V9  THE EXPERIMENT    Double Diamond     Streak-based RNG manipulation; one shared capstone
V10 DARWIN AWARD      Asymmetric Y       Dual ambition; short path=fast melee scaler; long path=Perfect Organism

=== NEW ENGINE STATE (all variations) ===
mutation_count (run-wide), mutation_reroll_available (per-floor), genome_locked_stat (per-floor),
rad_berserker_stacks (per-floor), adaptive_dr_bonus (run-wide), freak_speed_stacks (per-floor),
last_mutation_was_good (run-wide), mutation_streak (run-wide), irradiate_splash_radius (int),
contamination_cloud_active (bool), forced_strong_next/forced_huge_next (bool),
mutation_xp_multiplier (float), scar_tissue_hits (run-wide), genome_splice_used (bool),
forced_evolution_charges (per-floor)

=== NEW PLAYERSTATS FIELDS ===
rad_gain_multiplier_bonus (float), mutation_good_base_bonus (float),
mutation_trigger_multiplier (float), mutation_tier_skip_weak (bool),
adaptive_dr (int), mutation_melee_bonus (int), rad_to_power_ratio (float)

=== NEW EFFECTS ===
BerserkerSurgeEffect (+6 melee, +20 speed, 8t), GlowingEffect (aura, irradiates adjacent/tick),
ContaminationCloud (3-tile aura toggle), AdaptiveShieldEffect (temp HP on floor entry),
PrimordialRageEffect (+12 melee, -50% def, 15t), SplicedEffect (cosmetic feedback)

=== NEW ABILITIES ===
splice_reroll (FLOOR_ONLY 1), rad_burst (INFINITE cd 8t, ADJACENT), rad_pulse (PER_FLOOR 2, AOE_CIRCLE),
inoculate_self (FLOOR_ONLY 1), genome_lock (FLOOR_ONLY 1), primal_scream (PER_FLOOR 1, AOE visible),
contaminate_aura (INFINITE toggle), irradiate_surge (INFINITE cd 12t), forced_evolution (PER_FLOOR 1),
absorb_rad (INFINITE cd 15t), genome_splice (ONCE per run)

=== CAPSTONE ROSTER (15 capstones across 10 trees) ===
Full Freak (V1A)         — +melee per 5 mutations, 6x trigger rate
Apex Specimen (V1B)      — doubled good mutation bonuses, bad penalties -1
Meltdown (V2A)           — dump all rad → AOE damage + PrimordialRage
Rad Absorption (V2B)     — rad→HP conversion, no HP rad damage, +3 CON
Combat Genome (V3A)      — +1 melee per mutation (multiplicative)
Perfect Balance (V3B)    — bad Weak/Strong = only SP reward, no stat loss
One True Splice (V3C)    — ONCE/run: perfect chosen mutation + Huge bonuses
Speed God (V4A)          — speed buff permanent all floor, move cost 50
Explosion Hazard (V4B)   — crash = AOE irrad explosion, stacks never decay
Mass Accumulate (V5A)    — per-tier melee+HP bonus every mutation
Deep Memory (V5B)        — retroactive % buff from all past mutations
Living Reactor (V6)      — permanent contamination aura + enemy rad detonation
Immortal Body (V7)       — scar_tissue HP/DR scaling + death save
Huge Only (V8A solo)     — every mutation is Huge, tripled Good Huge bonuses
Twin Towers (V8A+B conv) — converged trident: all bonuses combined + BKS rad damage
XP Harvest (V8C solo)    — 3x mutation XP + 10% overflow to other skill trees
Genome Complete (V9)     — Amplify Good + Negate Bad + no bad Huge + genome_splice
Darwin Award (V10A)      — +2 melee per mutation_count (retroactive), 3x trigger rate
Perfect Organism (V10B)  — Strong→Huge effects, bad mutations negated, genome_splice + 2x forced_evol

=== RECOMMENDED FOR IMPLEMENTATION ===
V1 Freakshow, V4 Volatile Evolution, V7 Scar Tissue, V10 Darwin Award
(diverse playstyles: farming / berserker / tank / dual-ambition)
Most synergy-rich: V6 Fallout Prophet (deepens Glow Up tree relationship)
Most mechanically novel: V3 Splice and Dice (mutation tier selection UI)
