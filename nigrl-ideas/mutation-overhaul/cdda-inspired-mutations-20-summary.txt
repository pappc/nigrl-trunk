CDDA-INSPIRED MUTATION EXPANSION — SUMMARY
============================================
20 new mutation candidates (5 per category) to extend the 80-mutation overhaul.
Inspired by Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead. Explores themes NOT in existing 80:
terrain creation, summoning, sound, stealth, item fusion, enemy manipulation,
conditional triggers, resource transformation, probability manipulation.

WEAK GOOD (21-25):
  21. Slime Trail — leave slime tiles that trip/tox enemies; you're immune
  22. Echolocation Pulse — reveal entire floor briefly + disorient nearby enemies
  23. Sticky Fingers — bonus loot on melee kills + gold pickup bonus + item duplication
  24. Chameleon Skin — stand still to become invisible; ambush strike +50-100% dmg
  25. Probability Flux — +5-15% to ALL % rolls (yours AND enemy's); L3 25% death save

WEAK BAD (21-25):
  21. Volatile Anatomy — random stat swap each floor (-2 one stat, +1 another)
  22. Resonant Bones — taking hits alerts all enemies in radius; they get speed boost
  23. Magnetic Blood — +15-35% enemy ranged accuracy vs you; speed penalty from metal items
  24. Neural Leakage — consecutive same-ability use deals -25-75% reduced effect
  25. Unstable Mass — big hits push you randomly; wall impact damage; hazard risk

STRONG GOOD (21-25):
  21. Flesh Puppeteer — mind-control an enemy to fight for you; blocks weapon slot
  22. Mimic Flesh — absorb enemy special attacks, apply them on your melee hits
  23. Split Consciousness — 2 actions per turn for 10-15t, 1-2x/floor; blocks hat
  24. Web Spinner — place invisible web traps that root enemies; up to 15 webs
  25. Adaptive Metabolism — build immunity to repeated debuffs within a floor; blocks feet

STRONG BAD (21-25):
  21. Echo Chamber Skull — 3-7 self-damage on ability/gun/item use; blocks hat
  22. Sympathetic Pain — 15-25% of damage you deal returns as self-damage; blocks neck
  23. Entropic Aura — weapons degrade with use; rings/neck items decay per floor
  24. Midas Curse — 20-40% chance items transmute to gold on pickup
  25. Split Identity — every 20-30 turns, "other self" takes 1 random harmful action

KEY SYNERGIES: Slime+Pheromone, Web+Chameleon, Puppet+Split, Mimic+Leech
KEY ANTI-SYNERGIES: Chameleon+Lopsided, Sympathetic+Feral, Midas+Iron Gut
