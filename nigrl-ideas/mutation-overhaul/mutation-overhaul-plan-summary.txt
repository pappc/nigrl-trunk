SUMMARY: mutation-overhaul-plan.txt (EXPANDED — 80 Mutations + Upgrade System)
==============================================================================

CONCEPT: Overhaul player radiation mutations from stat buffs/debuffs to
mechanically unique identities. Every mutation changes HOW you play. Inspired
by Caves of Qud. Urban, gritty, dark-humor flavor throughout.

VERSION 2 CHANGES FROM ORIGINAL:
  - Expanded from 36 to 80 mutations (20 per category × 4 categories)
  - Added full upgrade system: re-rolling a mutation you already have upgrades it
  - Light Sensitivity (Strong Bad) removed; replaced by new Strong Bad mutations
  - 10 new touch spell mutations (Touch of Frost/Fire/Shock + 7 more)
  - Tox/Rad interaction mutations (Irradiated Blood, Toxic Veins, Fission Heart, etc.)
  - More proc-based effects, fewer passive auras
  - All original 36 mutations carried forward with upgrade paths added

TIER CHANGE: Remove "huge" tier. Two tiers only:
  Weak  — threshold 75+ rad, costs 50 rad
  Strong — threshold 150+ rad (bumped from 125), costs 125 rad

POLARITY: 67% bad / 33% good base rate. Favorable Odds + Five Loco still push ~50%.

MUTATION COUNT: 20 weak good, 20 weak bad, 20 strong good, 20 strong bad = 80 total.

UPGRADE SYSTEM:
  engine.mutation_levels: dict[str, int] — tracks level per mutation (1=base).
  Re-rolling a mutation you have: upgrades to next level instead of duplicate.
  Good mutations get stronger. Bad mutations get worse.
  Upgrades are mechanically interesting when possible; stat-up fallback for simple ones.
  Typical cap: L3 for most. Necrotic Fists and Accelerated Aging self-escalate.
  Shedding a mutation removes it and resets its level to 0.
  Triple Helix can cascade: two same-mutation rolls in one event = jump 2 levels.
  Scarred Tissue fires on bad mutation upgrades too (counts as bad-mutation event).

--- REVERSAL (SHED) ---
Shedding resets mutation_levels[id] to 0 (full removal).
Reversal logic must account for current level (e.g., Hardened Dermis at L3 reverses 6 DR).
New reversal types: "grant_ability", "flag", "speed", "fov_bonus", "power_penalty",
"slot_block", "compound", "ability_and_flag", "stat_and_flag".
Triple Helix can cascade upgrades; Shed must fully unwind all levels at once.

--- UPGRADE SYSTEM FIELDS ---
Engine: mutation_levels: dict[str, int] — RUN-WIDE; key=mutation_id, value=level.
  Serialized as JSON object in save_system.py.
Self-escalating mutations (Necrotic Fists, Accelerated Aging, Degenerating Nervous):
  Not updated via re-roll; only one level (L1); escalate through their own counter logic.

--- BALANCE NOTES ---
Bioluminescence (Weak Bad) may punch above weak tier — monitor in playtesting.
Second Stomach + speed strains: ×3 speed intensity could be overpowered — cap flag noted.
Fission Heart (L3) creates self-mutation cascades — intentionally volatile high-risk.
Degenerating Nervous System hard-counters ability-heavy builds (Nuclear Research, Blackkk Magic).
Accelerated Aging caps at 25% base max HP to prevent truly unplayable scenarios.
Sensory Overload (safe direction per floor) adds interesting movement puzzles in corridors.
Muscle Memory Override L3 counterattacks cannot miss — specific synergy exception for Twitchy Nerves.
