# XP / Skill Point Gain Skill Concepts

Three takes on a new skill focused on XP and skill point acceleration, fitting the ghetto/black culture theme of NIGRL.

---

## Take 1: "Hustlin"

The grind never stops. You learn from the come-up itself.

| Level | Name | Type | Effect |
|-------|------|------|--------|
| L1 | **On My Grind** | Passive | Killing an enemy grants potential XP to a random skill you haven't maxed. Spreads knowledge across the board — the hustle teaches you everything. |
| L2 | **Stat Up!** | Stat | +3 Book Smarts |
| L3 | **Flip It** | Passive | When you pick up cash, gain skill points equal to 5% of the amount. Money is education — every dollar picked up feeds your growth. |

**XP source**: Earned passively from picking up any item off the ground. The hustler learns by collecting.

---

## Take 2: "Schoolin"

Nobody taught you — you taught yourself.

| Level | Name | Type | Effect |
|-------|------|------|--------|
| L1 | **Self Taught** | Passive | The first time you use each skill's action per floor (first smoke, first steal, first stab kill, etc.), that action gives 3x potential XP. Rewards doing a little of everything each floor. |
| L2 | **Stat Up!** | Stat | +2 Book Smarts, +2 Street Smarts |
| L3 | **Dropout** | Passive | Skill point cost to convert potential XP into real XP is reduced by 20%. Everything you've learned sticks better — less waste. |

**XP source**: Earned from leveling up other skills. Every level-up in anything gives Schoolin potential XP.

---

## Take 3: "Schemin"

Playing the long game. You don't just learn — you exploit what you know.

| Level | Name | Type | Effect |
|-------|------|------|--------|
| L1 | **The Long Game** | Passive | At the start of each floor, gain skill points equal to your total skill levels combined. The more you know, the more you earn just by showing up. |
| L2 | **Stat Up!** | Stat | +3 Book Smarts |
| L3 | **Inside Man** | Passive | When an enemy dies, 10% chance to gain 1 charge of a random ability you own. Your scheming pays off in unexpected ways. |

**XP source**: Earned from descending floors. Longer runs = more Schemin XP.

---

## Strategic Tension (all takes)

Do you invest early in this skill for compound gains, or rush a combat skill to survive? The hustler's dilemma — long money vs short money.
