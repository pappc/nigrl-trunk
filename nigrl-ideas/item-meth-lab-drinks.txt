NIGRL DESIGN DOC — 10 NEW METH LAB ALCOHOLIC DRINKS
======================================================
Author: Game Design Ideator
Date: 2026-03-15
File: nigrl-ideas/item-meth-lab-drinks.txt

All drinks follow the established pattern:
  - item_id → ITEM_DEFS entry with "type": "alcohol", "drink_id": <id>
  - effect logic lives in xp_progression._handle_alcohol (elif branch)
  - Effect class(es) live in effects.py with @register decorator
  - All award Alcoholism (primary) + Drinking (secondary) XP
  - All cost 1 hangover stack unless noted
  - Most stack (separate Effect instances), duration refreshes to max on reapply
  - char: "!" for all (standard drink character)
  - zones: ["meth_lab"] for all

Balance anchors (reference from existing drinks):
  - Duration: 50t short, 100t standard, floor-duration long
  - Stat buff magnitude: +5 is significant (Dead Shot Daiquiri), +2 is modest
  - Perm armor grant: +3 (Steel Reserve / standard), +5 (Platinum Reserve / premium)
  - HP heal: 50% max HP is the big heal (Steel Reserve / Platinum Reserve tier)
  - Tox removal: -50 (Alco-Seltzer tier)
  - Gun synergy: Dead Shot Daiquiri (+5 STS, +5 dmg, 50% ammo return) is the baseline

DRINK 01 — RADIOACTIVE REMY
==============================================================================

ITEM_DEF entry:
  item_id:     "radioactive_remy"
  name:        "Radioactive Remy"
  char:        "!"
  color:       (120, 255, 60)      -- bright lime green, toxic glow
  category:    consumable
  value:       60
  primary_skill: Alcoholism
  secondary_skill: Drinking
  zones:       ["meth_lab"]
  use_verb:    "Drink"
  use_effect:  {"type": "alcohol", "drink_id": "radioactive_remy"}

CONCEPT:
A lime-green energy drink spiked with literal isotopes scooped off the lab floor.
The booze slows mutation processing but makes each mutation hit harder — you glow
brighter and mutate rarer, but when the cosmic dice land, they land weird.

EFFECT — RadioactiveRemyEffect:
  id:           "radioactive_remy"
  category:     buff
  duration:     floor-duration (clears on floor change, like Five Loco)
  stacks:       yes (separate instances, each with own floor duration)
  on_reapply:   add new stack instance (same pattern as FiveLocoEffect)

  Passive mechanics (active while any stack lives):
    1. Radiation gain rate: halved (each rad tick delivers half the normal amount).
       Implementation: modify_rad_gain hook multiplies by max(0.5^stacks, 0.125).
       At 1 stack: 50% rad gain. At 2: 25%. At 3+: cap at 12.5%.
    2. Mutation tier bias: while this effect is active, all mutation rolls have
       +1 tier bump. Weak mutations become strong; strong become huge.
       (engine._mutation_tier_bump += 1 per stack; cap at +2 total)
       This is independent of Five Loco's polarity bonus — they compound.
    3. On expire (floor change): consume effect, restore rad gain to normal.

HANGOVER: +1 stack

SYNERGIES:
  - Five Loco + Radioactive Remy: Five Loco doubles rad gain, Remy halves it back,
    net effect is normal rad gain — but both tier bump AND polarity bonus apply.
    Stacking both creates a "high-quality mutation factory" build.
  - Tol-high builds: Tol already slows rad gain; stacking Remy on top makes
    mutations extremely rare but always strong-to-huge tier.

ITEM DESCRIPTION LINES (for _drink_descs):
  "Lasts until floor change. Stacks."
  "Halves radiation gain per stack."
  "+1 mutation tier per stack (weak→strong→huge)."
  "+1 hangover stack."

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES:
  - New field on engine: _mutation_tier_bump: int = 0 (reset on floor change).
  - Modify effects.py RadioactiveRemyEffect: in apply(), do
    engine._mutation_tier_bump = min(2, engine._mutation_tier_bump + 1).
    In expire(), do engine._mutation_tier_bump = max(0, engine._mutation_tier_bump - 1).
  - mutations.py: when rolling mutation tier, add _mutation_tier_bump to threshold
    (e.g., if bump=1, a 50-rad mutation rolls as if at 125-rad tier).
  - modify_rad_gain hook: multiply by 0.5 per stack. Require the modify_rad_gain
    lifecycle hook from stat-scaling-strains-v2 to be implemented first.

DRINK 02 — LONG ISLAND ICED TEETH
==============================================================================

ITEM_DEF entry:
  item_id:     "long_island_iced_teeth"
  name:        "Long Island Iced Teeth"
  char:        "!"
  color:       (255, 220, 140)     -- pale amber/tea colored
  category:    consumable
  value:       60
  primary_skill: Alcoholism
  secondary_skill: Drinking
  zones:       ["meth_lab"]
  use_verb:    "Drink"
  use_effect:  {"type": "alcohol", "drink_id": "long_island_iced_teeth"}

CONCEPT:
Five kinds of booze poured into one cup with a splash of Coke. Has nothing to do
with Long Island — it's named after what happened to a dude's mouth after he tried
to fight a dog while drinking it. Melee-focused drink that turns the player into a
gutter brawler: every hit sends shockwaves of stacked debuffs into enemies.

EFFECT — LongIslandIcedTeethEffect:
  id:           "long_island_iced_teeth"
  category:     buff
  duration:     100 turns
  stacks:       yes (each stack is a separate instance; duration refreshes on reapply)
  on_reapply:   add new instance

  on_player_melee_hit hook (per stack):
    - 20% chance per stack: apply "stunned" (skip 1 turn) to the defender.
    - 30% chance per stack: apply "intimidated" (cannot move toward player, 5t)
      if not already intimidated. Duration resets on reapply.
    - These rolls are independent; both can trigger on the same hit.
    - Gouge (Stabbing L1) synergy: if the hit already gouges, stun from this
      drink extends the gouge debuff duration by +2 turns instead of re-applying stun.

  NOTE: Does NOT apply toxicity. Does NOT affect guns.
  No modify_incoming_damage hook — this is pure offense/CC utility.

HANGOVER: +1 stack

SYNERGIES:
  - Stabbing skill tree (Windfury L3): Windfury extra-hit also triggers the melee
    hook, so each Windfury proc has its own 20%/30% chance per stack.
  - White Gonster: debuff purge heals the player — Long Island Iced Teeth is
    deliberately making enemies debuffed for White Gonster to benefit from.
    Stacking these rewards aggressive play with a heal-on-stun-purge loop.
  - Virulent Vodka: both apply on melee hit. Stack both for tox + CC.

ITEM DESCRIPTION LINES:
  "100 turn buff. Stacks."
  "Each stack: 20% stun, 30% intimidate"
  "per melee hit."
  "+1 hangover stack."

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES:
  - "intimidated" effect was designed in L4/L5 perk doc (cannot move toward player,
    5t). If not yet implemented, see agent memory for its spec.
  - "stunned" (skip turn) already exists in effects.py.
  - LongIslandIcedTeethEffect.on_player_melee_hit loops over stacks count,
    each roll independent. Windfury calls the melee hit hook too — confirm
    engine._apply_virulent_vodka pattern is called for Windfury hits; same
    pattern applies here.
  - Gouge extension: in the stun branch, check if defender has "gouge" debuff;
    if yes, extend gouge duration by 2 instead of applying stun.

DRINK 03 — COLD FILTERED COPE
==============================================================================

ITEM_DEF entry:
  item_id:     "cold_filtered_cope"
  name:        "Cold Filtered Cope"
  char:        "!"
  color:       (180, 220, 255)     -- ice-blue, premium brand parody
  category:    consumable
  value:       60
  primary_skill: Alcoholism
  secondary_skill: Drinking
  zones:       ["meth_lab"]
  use_verb:    "Drink"
  use_effect:  {"type": "alcohol", "drink_id": "cold_filtered_cope"}

CONCEPT:
Triple-cold-filtered through three layers of paper towel. The name is on the label.
A purely defensive drink that converts incoming toxicity pressure into temporary
durability — the worse the environment gets, the more it pays off.

EFFECT — ColdFilteredCopeEffect:
  id:           "cold_filtered_cope"
  category:     buff
  duration:     100 turns
  stacks:       yes (separate instances)
  on_reapply:   add new instance

  modify_incoming_damage hook (per stack, evaluated against scaled tox damage):
    - While tox >= 100: +3 flat armor per stack (temporary, does not affect max armor).
      Implemented as: armor bonus = min(tox // 100, 5) * 3 * stacks.
      Cap: min(tox // 100, 5) means max tier is 5 (at 500 tox) for +15 armor/stack.
    - Concretely: at 100 tox/1 stack: +3 armor. 200 tox/1 stack: +6. 500 tox/1 stack: +15.
      At 2 stacks / 300 tox: +18 armor total.
    - This armor bonus is recalculated every turn in the before_turn hook,
      not stored as a permanent delta. Implemented as a DR-style flat reduction
      applied in modify_incoming_damage: damage -= armor_bonus, min 0.

  on expire: bonus naturally disappears (no cleanup needed since it's calculated
    dynamically, not stored).

HANGOVER: +1 stack

SYNERGIES:
  - Virulent Vodka: Virulent Vodka intentionally raises enemy tox. High-tox
    enemies hit for more (their tox multiplier). Cold Filtered Cope converts the
    player's own tox into armor — rewarding builds that accept tox as a resource.
  - Tox-offensive strains (Chemical Warfare, Synthesize): player intentionally
    stacking their own toxicity gets stronger defensively from this drink.
  - Platinum Reserve (armor tripling): the armor bonus from Cold Filtered Cope
    scales with whatever max armor is in effect. Stacking both means
    tox-buffed armor gets tripled. Very strong at high tox.

ITEM DESCRIPTION LINES:
  "100 turn buff. Stacks."
  "At 100 tox: +3 armor per stack."
  "Bonus scales with tox (max +15/stack)."
  "+1 hangover stack."

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES:
  - ColdFilteredCopeEffect.modify_incoming_damage: tier = min(entity.toxicity // 100, 5),
    bonus = tier * 3 * self.stacks, return max(0, damage - bonus).
  - Does NOT modify max_armor. Does NOT interact with armor restoration effects.
  - No new engine fields needed. Uses existing entity.toxicity attribute.

DRINK 04 — BUCKSHOT BOURBON
==============================================================================

ITEM_DEF entry:
  item_id:     "buckshot_bourbon"
  name:        "Buckshot Bourbon"
  char:        "!"
  color:       (180, 100, 40)      -- dark amber whiskey brown
  category:    consumable
  value:       60
  primary_skill: Alcoholism
  secondary_skill: Drinking
  zones:       ["meth_lab"]
  use_verb:    "Drink"
  use_effect:  {"type": "alcohol", "drink_id": "buckshot_bourbon"}

CONCEPT:
Distilled in a pressure cooker, poured through a bike pump, sold out of a duffel bag.
The booze hits like pellets — gun-focused drink that multiplies shots fired
at the cost of aim, turning the player's gun into a spray weapon regardless of fire mode.

EFFECT — BuckshotBourbonEffect:
  id:           "buckshot_bourbon"
  category:     buff
  duration:     50 turns
  stacks:       yes (separate instances)
  on_reapply:   add new instance, refresh duration to max(existing, 50)

  on_player_gun_fire hook (per stack):
    - Each shot fired spawns +1 additional pellet that targets a random entity
      within the cone/line of fire. Additional pellet uses a separate to-hit roll.
    - Additional pellet: 60% base hit chance (not modified by STS or accuracy),
      damage = 50% of base gun damage (rounded down), no crits.
    - Multiple stacks: 1 extra pellet per stack (2 stacks = 2 extra pellets).
    - All extra pellets respect the AOE Distribution Rule:
      no target hit by more than ceil(total_pellets / 2) times.
    - Compatible with all fire modes. Cone fire: extra pellets aim into cone.
      Single-target fire: extra pellets scatter to adjacent tiles (Chebyshev 1)
      of the primary target, targeting any entity there. If no adjacent entity:
      pellet misses into the wall harmlessly.

  NOTE: Extra pellets do NOT recover ammo (Dead Shot Daiquiri ammo recovery does
    not apply to bonus pellets). They consume no additional ammo.
    This keeps Dead Shot Daiquiri relevant as a distinct gun drink.

HANGOVER: +1 stack

SYNERGIES:
  - Dead Shot Daiquiri: does not double-recover ammo from pellets, but the +5 gun
    damage applies to the BASE shot only — extra pellets still get 50% of that base.
    Combining both for spray + ammo sustainability is a natural build.
  - UZI (cone weapon): extra pellets scatter into the cone for maximum carnage.
  - Mag Rat skill tree (FAST fire mode specialist): more shots fired total means
    more pellet procs per combat, amplifying burst damage significantly.
  - Accurate fire mode: the extra pellets intentionally have low accuracy,
    making ACCURATE + Buckshot Bourbon still spray-ish — a deliberate tradeoff.

ITEM DESCRIPTION LINES:
  "50 turn buff. Stacks."
  "Each shot: +1 pellet per stack."
  "Pellets: 60% hit, 50% dmg, no crit."
  "+1 hangover stack."

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES:
  - on_player_gun_fire hook: needs the on_player_gun_fire(entity, engine, shot_data)
    lifecycle hook from stat-scaling-strains-v2 design. shot_data dict includes
    num_shots, targets_in_range, fire_mode, gun_entity.
  - Extra pellets resolution: for each extra pellet, pick a random valid target
    from eligible set (targets in range), roll 60% hit, deal damage if hit.
  - Hit count tracking: track hits per target including extra pellets in combined
    hit_counts dict to enforce ceil(total_pellets/2) cap.
  - New helper: effects.py buckshot_bourbon_active(engine) -> int (returns total stacks).

DRINK 05 — STREET CRED SPRITZER
==============================================================================

ITEM_DEF entry:
  item_id:     "street_cred_spritzer"
  name:        "Street Cred Spritzer"
  char:        "!"
  color:       (255, 130, 200)     -- hot pink, clashes with everything
  category:    consumable
  value:       60
  primary_skill: Alcoholism
  secondary_skill: Drinking
  zones:       ["meth_lab"]
  use_verb:    "Drink"
  use_effect:  {"type": "alcohol", "drink_id": "street_cred_spritzer"}

CONCEPT:
Bubbly pink canned drink in a can that says "WINNERS ONLY" on it. Tastes like
fruit punch and ambition. Swagger-focused: the drink amplifies the Swagger stat's
combat effects beyond their normal ceiling, turning a high-SWG build into a
genuine combat powerhouse for a limited window.

EFFECT — StreetCredSpritzer (on drink, immediate + buff):
  Immediate:
    - +3 effective Swagger (temporary, via add_temporary_stat_bonus) for 100 turns.

  StreetCredSpritzer buff (id: "street_cred_spritzer"):
    id:           "street_cred_spritzer"
    category:     buff
    duration:     100 turns
    stacks:       yes

    Passive mechanics per stack:
      1. Swagger defense formula enhancement: while this buff is active,
         the formula changes from int((SWG - 8) / 2) to int((SWG - 8) / 1.5).
         (More defense per point of Swagger above 8.)
         At SWG 12 normally: +2 def. With buff: +2.67 → +2 (rounds down, same).
         At SWG 16 (with temp bonus + other bonuses): normally +4, with buff +5.
         At SWG 20 edge case: normally +6, with buff +8.
         Per stack: each additional stack adds another 0.5 to the divisor's
         effective reduction. Stack 2: divisor 1.0 (SWG - 8 raw). Stack 3: capped.
      2. Dodge chance: +5% flat per stack (additive with existing dodge).
      3. On expire: remove temporary stat bonus and formula change. Cap defense
         at pre-expire value to avoid sudden HP swing.

HANGOVER: +1 stack

SYNERGIES:
  - Swagger mutation strains (Hustle Hard / Known Associate / Cold Chain):
    Spritzer amplifies the SWG investment those strains demand.
  - Cold Chain strain (SWG/mutation): Cold Chain's cold_chain_aura debuffs enemies
    with shaken; Spritzer adds dodge on top for a kite-heavy style.
  - White Gonster (+5 Swagger): stacking Spritzer + White Gonster pushes SWG
    temporarily to SWG 18+ range, hitting very high defense values.
  - Crown (hat, +4 SWG): with Crown + Spritzer + White Gonster, SWG can hit 20+,
    reaching the capped formula benefit.

ITEM DESCRIPTION LINES:
  "100 turn buff. Stacks."
  "+3 Swagger (temp). Dodge +5%/stack."
  "Swagger defense formula enhanced."
  "+1 hangover stack."

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES:
  - Temporary Swagger bonus: use engine.player_stats.add_temporary_stat_bonus
    ("swagger", 3) on drink; expire() must subtract it back.
  - Modified defense formula: engine.py computes swagger_defence. Add a check:
    if player has street_cred_spritzer buff, use modified divisor.
    Helper: effects.py get_street_cred_spritzer_divisor(engine) -> float.
    Returns 1.5 - (0.5 * min(stacks - 1, 1)). (1.5 at 1 stack, 1.0 at 2+, cap.)
  - Dodge: add to engine.player_stats.dodge_chance directly in apply/expire hooks.
  - stat_bonus expire: StreetCredSpritzer must track how much swagger temp bonus
    it added (self._swagger_granted = 3) and subtract exactly that in expire().

DRINK 06 — LEAN CUISINE
==============================================================================

ITEM_DEF entry:
  item_id:     "lean_cuisine"
  name:        "Lean Cuisine"
  char:        "!"
  color:       (150, 80, 200)      -- deep purple, codeine color
  category:    consumable
  value:       60
  primary_skill: Alcoholism
  secondary_skill: Drinking
  zones:       ["meth_lab"]
  use_verb:    "Drink"
  use_effect:  {"type": "alcohol", "drink_id": "lean_cuisine"}

CONCEPT:
Promethazine and sprite over crushed ice in a styrofoam cup. Slows everything down
to a crawl — the player moves slower but every action becomes more powerful.
A deliberate "glass cannon with a limp" fantasy: trading speed for damage scaling.

EFFECT — LeanCuisineEffect:
  id:           "lean_cuisine"
  category:     buff (despite penalties — player chose it)
  duration:     100 turns
  stacks:       yes

  modify_energy_gain hook per stack:
    - -20 energy/tick per stack. (At 1 stack: 80 energy/tick. At 2: 60. At 3: 40.)
    - Floor: 30 energy/tick minimum (never fully immobilized).

  Combat buffs per stack:
    - +15% damage multiplier on ALL player damage (melee, gun, ability).
      Implemented in combat as a flat multiplier: damage = int(damage * (1 + 0.15 * stacks)).
    - +1 flat damage per stack (in addition to the multiplier).
    - Crit damage bonus: +0.25x crit multiplier per stack.
      (Base crit 2.0x. At 1 stack: 2.25x. At 2: 2.5x. At 3: 2.75x.)

  NOTE: -20 energy/tick per stack is harsh. At 3 stacks: 40 energy/tick vs
    enemies' 100, meaning enemies get 2.5 turns per 1 player turn.
    This is intentional. The damage output at 3 stacks compensates heavily.

HANGOVER: +1 stack

SYNERGIES:
  - Meth-Head Wire effect (+30 energy/tick): partially counteracts Lean Cuisine
    speed penalty. Wire + 1 Lean stack = 110 energy (normal speed + speed buff).
    Combining is valid but costs hangover stacks on both sides.
  - Street Smarts crit build: higher crit chance + elevated crit multiplier
    (2.75x at 3 stacks) creates massive burst potential on crits.
  - Gouge (Stabbing L1): stun immobilizes enemies so the slow player can
    catch up. Lean Cuisine rewards having reliable CC.
  - Dead Eye skill tree (ACCURATE fire mode): single precise shots benefit more
    from the per-hit multiplier than spray builds. Patience + punch.

ITEM DESCRIPTION LINES:
  "100 turn buff. Stacks."
  "-20 energy/tick per stack (min 30)."
  "+15% dmg, +1 flat dmg, +0.25 crit mult."
  "+1 hangover stack."

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES:
  - modify_energy_gain: existing hook in effects.py. Sum across all Lean stacks.
    engine enforces minimum 30 energy/tick.
  - Damage multiplier: combat.py resolve_attack (or equivalent). Check for
    LeanCuisineEffect on attacker; apply multiplier before defense subtraction.
    Helper: effects.py lean_cuisine_damage_mult(engine) -> float (1.0 if none,
    1.0 + 0.15 * stacks if active). Crit mult: effects.py lean_cuisine_crit_bonus.
  - Multiple stacks: combine into single calculation (stacks field on single
    effect instance). On reapply: existing.stacks += 1, reset duration to 100.

DRINK 07 — BARREL ROLL
==============================================================================

ITEM_DEF entry:
  item_id:     "barrel_roll"
  name:        "Barrel Roll"
  char:        "!"
  color:       (200, 160, 60)      -- warm oak barrel brown
  category:    consumable
  value:       60
  primary_skill: Alcoholism
  secondary_skill: Drinking
  zones:       ["meth_lab"]
  use_verb:    "Drink"
  use_effect:  {"type": "alcohol", "drink_id": "barrel_roll"}

CONCEPT:
Aged for 12 years in a barrel that also had brake fluid in it. Named for the
corkscrew roll you do when it hits. A mobility drink with the unique property of
converting movement into damage — every tile the player walks becomes a threat.

EFFECT — BarrelRollEffect (on drink, immediate + buff):
  Immediate:
    - +25 energy bonus (one-time speed spike, like a single-turn burst).

  BarrelRollEffect buff:
    id:           "barrel_roll"
    category:     buff
    duration:     50 turns
    stacks:       yes

    modify_movement hook (new or existing):
      - While active: player movement does NOT trigger enemy opportunity attacks.
        (If that system exists or is later added; currently a future-proof note.)

    on_player_move hook (fires after each player tile movement):
      - For each entity adjacent (Chebyshev 1) to the destination tile at the
        moment the player arrives: 30% chance per stack to apply "tripped"
        (a new debuff: skip 1 turn, applies like stun). No damage component.
      - "Tripped" is cosmetically distinct from "stunned" but mechanically
        identical (skip 1 turn, then removed). Log: "[Enemy] trips over you."
      - This fires ONCE per move action, targeting ALL adjacent enemies at
        destination (each rolls independently, per stack).

    +15 energy/tick per stack (persistent speed buff):
      Implemented via modify_energy_gain hook.
      At 1 stack: 115 energy/tick. At 2: 130.

HANGOVER: +1 stack

SYNERGIES:
  - Hit-and-Run AI behavior (player mirror): the player becomes a mobile threat
    that destabilizes enemy positioning. Pair with wander/kite movement style.
  - Wire effect (Meth-Head): Wire already boosts speed. Barrel Roll on top makes
    the player very fast and the tripping effect fires more often per combat.
  - Jaywalking skill tree (street mobility): intended synergy hook. Barrel Roll
    is designed to reward the Jaywalking investment with a damage/CC payoff.
  - FAST fire mode: moving fast + spraying on arrival creates "drive-by" fantasy.

ITEM DESCRIPTION LINES:
  "50 turn buff. Stacks. +25 energy now."
  "+15 energy/tick per stack."
  "30%/stack: trip adj. enemies on move."
  "+1 hangover stack."

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES:
  - "tripped" effect: alias for "stunned" with different log message, or a
    separate TrippedEffect class inheriting StunnedEffect with display_name
    override. Recommend separate class for cleaner log messages.
  - on_player_move hook: new lifecycle hook needed on Effect base class.
    engine._handle_move calls it after resolving the move. Passes destination
    (x, y) and the list of adjacent entities.
  - Adjacency check: Chebyshev distance 1 from destination tile, all entities
    (not just enemies — but only apply tripped to living monsters).
  - modify_energy_gain: +15 per stack, consistent with modify_energy_gain pattern.

DRINK 08 — FOUR FINGERS OF FENTANOL
==============================================================================

ITEM_DEF entry:
  item_id:     "four_fingers_of_fentanol"
  name:        "Four Fingers of Fentanol"
  char:        "!"
  color:       (240, 200, 255)     -- pale lavender, pharmaceutical
  category:    consumable
  value:       60
  primary_skill: Alcoholism
  secondary_skill: Drinking
  zones:       ["meth_lab"]
  use_verb:    "Drink"
  use_effect:  {"type": "alcohol", "drink_id": "four_fingers_of_fentanol"}

CONCEPT:
Technically a controlled substance diluted into grain alcohol. It sits on the counter
in a beaker labeled "DO NOT DRINK." Pain suppression is the pitch: the player stops
registering damage in the traditional sense, converting HP damage to a slower-
draining resource, then paying it back hard when the drink wears off.

EFFECT — FourFingersEffect:
  id:           "four_fingers_of_fentanol"
  category:     buff
  duration:     100 turns
  stacks:       no — only one instance allowed. Second drink while active extends
                duration by 50 turns (up to 200t max) and adds +1 to
                self._pending_debt per 50 HP healed by the extension.

  Mechanics:
    On apply:
      - Heal player to full HP. Record how much HP was healed as self._pending_debt
        (= amount healed). Minimum debt: 0 (already full HP → no debt).
      - 50% DR while buff is active (all incoming damage halved, after armor).
        Implemented via modify_incoming_damage: return max(1, damage // 2).
      - No tox/rad interaction.

    On expire:
      - Deal self._pending_debt as unblockable damage (ignores armor and DR).
        Ignores all effect hooks. Raw damage: engine.player.hp -= pending_debt.
      - If pending_debt would reduce HP to 0 or below: trigger game over
        (permadeath). This is announced one turn before expiry: at duration == 1,
        print a warning: "The Fentanol is wearing off — BRACE YOURSELF."
      - If player has died before expiry by other means: debt is cancelled (no
        double kill).

  On reapply (while buff is active):
    - Extend duration by 50t (cap 200t total).
    - Heal player to full HP again. Add healed amount to self._pending_debt.
    - Do NOT reset duration to 100. Only extend.

HANGOVER: +2 stacks (it's a lot)

SYNERGIES:
  - Steel Reserve / Platinum Reserve: healing before the debt hits can buy time.
    Drinking a Platinum Reserve right as Four Fingers expires doesn't negate the
    debt (unblockable) but may restore HP immediately after if the player survives.
  - White Gonster: cannot purge Four Fingers (it's categorized buff, not debuff).
    The debt is a design feature, not a status — it's stored on the effect object.
  - Virulent Vodka: building tox on enemies while taking 50% DR makes you more
    aggressive. The debt cliff is the tradeoff for the burst window.
  - High CON builds: more max HP means the debt at expiry is a smaller % of max,
    making the drink more survivable on beefy characters.
  - Permadeath tension: this drink is designed to create a ticking-clock scenario.
    The one-turn warning at duration == 1 is critical for player agency.

ITEM DESCRIPTION LINES:
  "100 turn buff (no stack). +50t per redose."
  "Heal to full HP. 50% DR while active."
  "On expire: take all skipped dmg at once."
  "+2 hangover stacks."

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES:
  - Single-instance rule: on reapply, check if player already has this effect.

EFFECT — ChromeHandleEffect:
  id:           "chrome_handle"
  category:     buff
  duration:     100 turns
  stacks:       yes

  On apply:
    - For each other active alcohol buff on the player (identified by checking all
      status_effects for entries in ALCOHOL_BUFF_IDS set): extend that buff's
      remaining duration by +30 turns (does not exceed that buff's max, if any).

       Helper: effects.py get_chrome_handle_amplifier(engine) -> float.
       Returns 1.0 + (0.25 * stacks). All compatible drink effects call this
       helper before computing their magnitude.

         - White Gonster: swagger bonus = 5 * amplifier (rounded)
         - Virulent Vodka: tox applied = max(dmg,10) * stacks * amplifier

         - Lean Cuisine: dmg mult = 1 + (0.15 * stacks * amplifier)
         - Street Cred Spritzer: dodge bonus = 5% * stacks * amplifier

    2. On player kill while Chrome Handle is active: +5 energy bonus (one-time
       burst per kill, not per stack — prevents energy stacking abuse).

    - Remove amplifier. Effects that were amplified recalculate naturally.
    - +2 CON remains (permanent).

    This is the drink that makes the "drunk build" a real identity.
  - Dead Shot Daiquiri + Chrome Handle: the gun build's ceiling goes up dramatically.
    At 2 Chrome stacks + 2 Daiquiri stacks: gun dmg = 5 * 2 * 1.5 = +15 flat,
    plus 60% ammo recovery per bullet.
  - Alcoholism skill tree: Im Drinkin Here / Throw Bottle / Last Call (L5 ability)

  - Purple Drank synergy: Purple Drank replays last drink. If last drink was

    This is intentionally powerful — Purple Drank is designed as a combo piece.
  - Blue Drank (double next drink effect): Blue Drank + Chrome Handle = 2 CON
    permanent + 60t extensions on all buffs + 2 Chrome Handle instances.
    Very strong — gatekept by Blue Drank's own cooldown mechanics.

  Barrel Roll adds speed back per stack.
  Stacking both: -20 energy (Lean) + 15 energy (Barrel) per stack each =
    net -5 energy/tick per combined stack pair. Slightly slow but with
    melee CC from Barrel Roll and dmg mult from Lean. Viable build.
