CHEMICAL WARFARE — 10 Branching Skill Tree Variations
=======================================================
Design doc for the NIGRL roguelike (Meth Lab zone skill).
These are BRANCHING trees with player-choice topology — NOT linear L1-L4.
The player selects one branch at each split and cannot unlock the other.

Toxicity system reference:
  - Player tox multiplier:  1 + (tox/200)^0.6    (200 tox = 2x damage taken)
  - Monster tox multiplier: 1 + (tox/50)^0.6     (50 tox = 2x, monsters far more sensitive)
  - tox_resistance: int on PlayerStats (additive with temporary_tox_resistance)
  - Tox hazard types: toxic_creep (~, green-yellow), acid_pool (~, bright green),
    venom_pool (~, mid-green)
  - ChargeType options: INFINITE+cooldown, PER_FLOOR, TOTAL, ONCE, FLOOR_ONLY
  - Stat names: constitution, strength, street_smarts, book_smarts, tolerance, swagger
  - Energy: 100/tick base. +30 speed = ~23% faster action rate.
  - Player HP: 30 + CON*10. Crit chance: street_smarts * 3%.

XP source for Chemical Warfare: dealing toxicity to enemies, kills on
high-tox enemies (30+), using tox abilities.

Shape glossary:
  Y-split (L3)     — L1, L2 shared trunk; branch at L3: L3a/L4a vs L3b/L4b. 6 perks, 2 capstones.
  Diamond          — L1 shared; L2a/L2b split; L3a/L3b branches; L4 shared convergence;
                     L5a/L5b final split. 8 perks, 2 capstones.
  Triple Fork (L4) — L1, L2, L3 shared trunk; split at L4 into L4a/L4b/L4c. 6 perks, 3 capstones.
  W-shape          — L1/L2 shared; L3a/L4a and L3b/L4b branches; L5 shared convergence;
                     L6a/L6b final split. 8 perks, 2 capstones.
  Long Trunk Y     — L1, L2, L3, L4 shared trunk; branch at L5 into L5a/L5b. 6 perks, 2 capstones.


==========================================================================
VARIATION 1: "THE SPREADER" — Contagion vs. Infection
==========================================================================
Shape: Y-split (L3)
Fantasy: The trunk builds up your tox delivery toolkit, then you choose:
  Branch A — mass contagion (splash, chain death explosions, room-wide spread)
  Branch B — deep infection (single-target DOT amplification, mark-and-execute)

TREE DIAGRAM:

    L1: Patient Zero
          |
    L2: Incubation Period
         / \
        /   \
  [BRANCH A]  [BRANCH B]
  L3a: Carrier  L3b: Inoculate
       |              |
  L4a: Outbreak  L4b: Hemorrhagic Fever
   (capstone)     (capstone)

TRUNK:

L1 — PATIENT ZERO (activated)
  Perk type: activated
  Ability: patient_zero_b
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (3/floor)
  Target type: SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS
  Effect: Mark a visible enemy with the Patient Zero status (15 turns).
          Each turn the marked enemy gains 4 toxicity and spreads 2 tox
          to every directly adjacent enemy. The mark is visible
          (a '~' overlay on the target). Grants +2 Chemical Warfare XP
          when applied.
  Desc: "Pick your vector. Everything downstream dies slower."

L2 — INCUBATION PERIOD (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Enemies that have been toxic for 3+ consecutive turns (tracked
          per entity via a counter) take 15% increased damage from
          melee and ranged attacks. The bonus appears as soon as the
          enemy reaches their third tox-tick without losing toxicity.
          Resets if the enemy drops below 1 tox for a full turn.
  Desc: "Give it time. Everything breaks down."

BRANCH A — Mass Contagion

L3a — CARRIER (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Patient Zero can now be active on 2 enemies simultaneously
          (up from 1). Additionally, when a Patient Zero-marked enemy
          dies, ALL enemies in a 3-tile radius gain 10 toxicity (a death
          burst). This burst can trigger further death bursts if those
          enemies die with 30+ tox (chain reaction).
  Desc: "One carrier becomes two. Two becomes the whole block."

L4a — OUTBREAK (activated) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: activated
  Ability: outbreak_b
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (1/floor)
  Target type: SELF (affects all visible enemies)
  Effect: 1/floor. Trigger a mass outbreak event: every visible enemy
          simultaneously gains tox equal to (their current tox + 8).
          Enemies with 0 current tox gain a flat 8. Enemies with 50+
          tox gain 58+. Combined with Incubation Period and the Carrier
          death burst, a well-stacked room can chain-die in 3 turns.
          Grants +10 Chemical Warfare XP per enemy that dies within 10
          turns of this activation.
  Desc: "1/floor. Flip the switch. Let the room finish itself."
  Balance: Against 6 enemies all at 0 tox, this is only +8 each —
          minimal. Against 6 enemies already at 30 tox, this is +38
          each — entering dangerous multiplier territory for those
          enemies. Rewards setup; punishes using it cold.

BRANCH B — Deep Infection

L3b — INOCULATE (activated)
  Perk type: activated
  Ability: inoculate
  Charge type: INFINITE (30t cooldown)
  Target type: SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS
  Effect: 30t cooldown. Inject a specific enemy with a deep-tissue
          toxin. For 20 turns, that enemy's toxicity CANNOT be reduced
          below its current value at time of injection (they can gain
          more but cannot lose it). All tox damage ticks against them
          are doubled. If they die while Inoculated, you gain a
          permanent +1 TOL stat boost (max +4 TOL total across
          all Inoculate kills).
  Desc: "Lock it in. There's no sweating this one out."

L4b — HEMORRHAGIC FEVER (passive) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: When a Patient Zero-marked or Inoculated enemy reaches 50+
          toxicity, they enter Hemorrhagic Fever: lose 8 HP per turn
          (direct damage, bypasses armor) for as long as they stay at
          50+ tox. This replaces the normal tox multiplier damage for
          affected enemies — the flat HP drain is often more lethal
          against high-armor targets. Additionally, any enemy that takes
          Hemorrhagic Fever damage within your melee range triggers a
          free +4 tox application on hit (no action cost).
  Desc: "50 tox and they start bleeding out. Nothing you can do."
  Balance: Against high-armor enemies this is strictly better than the
          normal tox multiplier. Against low-armor targets, the normal
          formula may be comparable. The choice of which enemies to mark
          matters. +4 tox free on adjacency keeps melee pressure relevant.

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES (V1):
  - incubation_period_counter: dict {entity_id: int} tracking consecutive
    tox turns, stored as engine.chem_incubation_counters
  - inoculate_floor_lock: dict {entity_id: tox_at_injection_time}
  - hemorrhagic_fever: new Effect subclass, applies when entity.toxicity >= 50
    while entity has "inoculated" or "patient_zero" status
  - Permanent +1 TOL from Inoculate kills: tracked via engine.chem_inoculate_kills,
    capped at 4; applied as permanent stat increase in player._base.tolerance

NEW ABILITY IDs (V1): patient_zero_b, outbreak_b, inoculate

VARIATION 2: "THE JUNKIE" — Crash Economy vs. Tox Tolerance
==========================================================================
Shape: Y-split (L3)
Fantasy: The trunk turns consumable use into a tox economy. Then choose:
  Branch A — Crash Economy (spend tox for burst power, accept the crash)
  Branch B — Tox Tolerance (carry maximum tox without dying, stack it forever)

TREE DIAGRAM:

    L1: Loaded
          |
    L2: The Market
         / \
        /   \
  [BRANCH A]  [BRANCH B]
  L3a: Spike   L3b: Metabolic Overload
       |              |
  L4a: Pump &  L4b: Overdrive
       Dump          (capstone)
   (capstone)

TRUNK:

L1 — LOADED (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Consuming any item (food, drink, strain, joint) grants +8
          toxicity. If you are already at 40+ tox when you consume, the
          granted tox is doubled to +16. This converts your consumable
          usage into tox generation — the more you use, the more toxic
          you become.
  Desc: "Everything you swallow goes in the same direction."

L2 — THE MARKET (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: { "tolerance": 2, "constitution": 2 }
  Killing an enemy while at 30+ personal toxicity grants +6 Chemical
  Warfare XP. At 60+ tox: grants +12 XP instead. This creates a flywheel
  incentive to stay toxic while dealing damage.
  Desc: "+2 TOL, +2 CON. There's a price for everything. You've learned
        to make the poison pay."

BRANCH A — Crash Economy

L3a — SPIKE (activated)
  Perk type: activated
  Ability: spike_tox
  Charge type: INFINITE (50t cooldown)
  Target type: SELF
  Effect: 50t cooldown. For the next 8 turns, your toxicity is treated as
          double for all outgoing scaling effects (Chemical Rage bonuses,
          Contact High aura, tox-based ability scaling) but you take NO
          additional incoming damage from the inflated value. After 8
          turns: crash. Your tox drops to 0 and you take damage equal to
          tox/15 (rounded down, min 5, max 18 at 270+ tox). Pairs with
          any tox-scaling ability for a massive burst window.
  Desc: "50t cd, 8-turn window. Double power, then the floor catches up."

L4a — PUMP AND DUMP (passive upgrade) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Spike now also boosts your attack speed: gain +25 energy/turn
          during the Spike window (roughly 20% faster actions). The crash
          damage cap is removed (no longer capped at 18 — real formula:
          tox/15, so 200 tox crash = ~13 damage, 400 tox crash = 26
          damage, still survivable with CON). Additionally: after the
          crash, you gain immunity to toxicity for 3 turns (your body
          flushes completely; tox sources add 0 during immunity window).
  Desc: "Spike goes harder. Crash goes harder. The immunity window after
        is the only reason this isn't suicide."
  Balance: Speed boost during spike = meaningful action advantage. Crash
          immunity = recovery window. The capstone rewards using Spike
          as a planned burst rather than panic defense.

BRANCH B — Tox Tolerance

L3b — METABOLIC OVERLOAD (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: { "tolerance": 3, "tox_resistance": -30 }
  Your personal tox cap is raised to 200 (normally softcapped at around
  100 where the multiplier becomes very painful). Above 100 tox, your
  melee damage gains a flat bonus equal to (tox - 100) / 10 (rounded
  down). At 150 tox: +5 melee. At 200 tox: +10 melee. The -30% tox
  resistance means you accumulate faster — by design.
  Desc: "+3 TOL, -30% tox resist. The cap is a suggestion. You've
        broken through it."

L4b — OVERDRIVE (passive) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: While at 80+ personal toxicity, gain a second attack every
          4th turn (a free bonus strike that uses the same equipped
          weapon, at full damage). The counter resets if you drop below
          80 tox. Additionally: the flat melee bonus from Metabolic
          Overload is now DOUBLED (so at 200 tox: +20 melee bonus
          instead of +10). The tox multiplier on incoming damage at
          200 tox = 1 + (200/200)^0.6 = 2.0 — you take double damage.
          Overdrive only works if you can stay alive at that level.
  Desc: "80+ tox: 1 in 4 swings is free. 200 tox: +20 damage and you
        die twice as fast. You do the math."
  Balance: Free attack every 4th turn = +25% melee output. This is
          strong but gated behind lethal self-damage multiplier. Synergy
          with White Power L4 (Whitewash) is the intended safety net —
          a cross-tree interaction reward.

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES (V2):
  - spike_tox_active: bool flag, 8-turn countdown, engine.chem_spike_active
  - spike_tox_double: read in combat.py and all tox-scaling passive checks
  - spike_crash_immune_turns: int countdown on player entity (3 turns after crash)
  - tox_cap_raised: if player has Metabolic Overload, tox damage formula check
    uses 200 as denominator instead of 200 (formula already uses 200, so this
    perk changes the DISPLAY cap and enables the melee bonus above 100)
  - overdrive_counter: engine.chem_overdrive_counter (int, 0-3, resets on attack)
  - overdrive_bonus_attack: triggered in combat.py after normal melee when
    overdrive_counter == 0 and player.toxicity >= 80

NEW ABILITY IDs (V2): spike_tox

VARIATION 3: "THE ALCHEMIST" — Terrain Chemist vs. Body Chemistry
==========================================================================
Shape: Diamond (L2→L5)
Fantasy: A shared trunk establishes BKS-scaling acid delivery. The split
explores two approaches to the acid formula before converging on Dissolution
(shared L4), then diverging into two distinct endgames.

TREE DIAGRAM:

        L1: Corrosive Spit
           /          \
          /            \
  L2a: Terrain         L2b: Acid Blood
       Chemist
       |                   |
  L3a: Acid Volley     L3b: Caustic Skin
            \              /
             \            /
          L4: Dissolution (shared)
              /           \
             /             \
  L5a: Total Meltdown   L5b: Living Reagent
       (capstone)         (capstone)

TRUNK:

L1 — CORROSIVE SPIT (activated)
  Perk type: activated
  Stat bonus: { "book_smarts": 2 }
  Ability: corrosive_spit_b
  Charge type: INFINITE (25t cooldown)
  Target type: SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS
  Effect: +2 Book-Smarts. 25t cooldown. Spit a corrosive glob at a
          visible enemy (range 6). Deals 3 + BKS/3 direct damage and
          applies 20 toxicity. If the target already has 25+ tox, deals
          the tox amount as bonus damage (a detonation: e.g. 50 tox =
          50 bonus damage). The detonation does NOT consume the toxicity.
  Desc: "+2 BKS. 25t cd. Spit first; ask questions once they're melting."

BRANCH SPLIT AT L2:

L2a — TERRAIN CHEMIST (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Your Corrosive Spit splash creates a small acid pool (1-tile
          radius) at the impact point lasting 12 turns (4 damage/turn,
          also applies 4 tox/turn). Acid pools created by you last 50%
          longer than normal. You are immune to your own acid pools.
  Desc: "Every spit leaves a puddle. Herd them into the aftermath."

L2b — ACID BLOOD (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: When YOU take melee damage, the attacker takes 5 + BKS/4
          acid splash damage (direct, bypasses armor) and gains 8
          toxicity. Stacks with other retaliatory effects. Works once
          per attack — not per damage point.
  Desc: "Hit you back? That was a mistake. You bleed wrong."

L3a — ACID VOLLEY (activated)
  Perk type: activated
  Ability: acid_volley_b
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (2/floor)
  Target type: AOE_CIRCLE (radius 2, any visible tile as center)
  Effect: 2/floor. Lob an acid grenade at any visible tile. Creates a
          3x3 acid pool (6 damage/turn, 25 turns). Enemies in the
          initial blast radius take 8 + BKS/2 damage and 25 toxicity.
          Synergizes with Terrain Chemist: the pool lasts 50% longer
          (37 turns) and you remain immune to it.
  Desc: "2/floor. Pick a corner, make it uninhabitable."

L3b — CAUSTIC SKIN (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: { "constitution": 2, "tox_resistance": 20 }
  +2 CON, +20% tox resistance. Your skin has become chemically reactive:
  any enemy that deals melee damage to you gains 12 toxicity (up from 8
  from Acid Blood — stacks with the Acid Blood damage; this is a
  separate tox application, so same attacker gets both). Additionally,
  your personal tox multiplier formula uses 250 as the denominator
  instead of 200 (effectively reducing tox damage to you by ~20% at
  high tox values).
  Desc: "+2 CON, +20% tox resist. You've become an environmental hazard."

SHARED CONVERGENCE NODE:

L4 — DISSOLUTION (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Stat bonus: { "book_smarts": 2 }
  Effect: +2 Book-Smarts (combining with L1's +2 = total +4 BKS from
          this tree). Enemies that die on an acid pool tile or toxic
          ground tile you created leave a permanent (floor_duration=True)
          toxic puddle at their death tile (6 tox/turn, indefinite
          duration). Corrosive Spit detonation threshold drops from 25
          tox to 15 tox. Any acid pool you control now also applies the
          Incubation Period effect (15% increased damage taken after 3
          tox-turns) even without the V1 Spreader perk.
  Desc: "+2 BKS. Death leaves a stain. Your stains leave more death."

FINAL SPLIT AT L5:

L5a — TOTAL MELTDOWN (activated) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: activated
  Ability: total_meltdown
  Charge type: TOTAL (2 uses per run)
  Target type: SELF (affects entire current floor)
  Effect: 2/run. Every acid pool and tox hazard on the current floor
          simultaneously releases a tox burst: 15-tile radius (effectively
          the whole floor), 20 tox to every enemy caught in at least one
          burst. Enemies hit by 3+ bursts (i.e. adjacent to 3+ hazards)
          take an additional 30 direct damage and are stunned for 3 turns.
          Your own pools are immune to you per Terrain Chemist. After
          activation, all hazard tiles are consumed (they dissolve),
          granting +5 Chemical Warfare XP per hazard consumed.
  Desc: "2/run. Press the button. Let them burn from the inside out."
  Balance: Requires setup (acid pools laid in advance). Maximum value
          against packed rooms where enemies cluster in terrain. Against
          single enemies in open space, only 1 burst = 20 tox, modest.

L5b — LIVING REAGENT (passive) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Your own body becomes a walking acid reaction. All tox you
          carry is converted into a permanent aura: enemies adjacent to
          you take acid damage each turn equal to your_tox/20 (rounded
          up, so at 0 tox = 0, at 100 tox = 5 damage/turn/adjacent
          enemy). This damage counts as acid for Dissolution corpse
          placement and Terrain Chemist pool creation rules. Additionally,
          Corrosive Spit no longer has a cooldown — it is replaced with
          a 3-charge, INFINITE recharge version (gains 1 charge per 15
          turns spent with 20+ personal tox).
  Desc: "You ARE the acid pool. Standing next to you is the mistake."
  Balance: Aura damage at 100 tox = 5/turn adjacent = meaningful chip
          against low-armor enemies. Against tanky enemies it supplements
          rather than replaces burst. Spit recharge gating on personal tox
          rewards keeping tox elevated — synergy with Caustic Skin path.

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES (V3):
  - Dissolution L4 "permanent puddle" uses floor_duration=True on Effect
  - acid_pool_owner flag on hazard entity for immunity checks
  - living_reagent_aura: checked each energy tick for adjacent enemies when
    player has this perk and toxicity > 0
  - corrosive_spit recharge: engine.chem_spit_charge_timer (int); check if
    player.toxicity >= 20 each turn; increment on 15-turn intervals; max 3
  - total_meltdown uses engine.all_hazards list; filters for acid_pool type

NEW ABILITY IDs (V3): corrosive_spit_b, acid_volley_b, total_meltdown

VARIATION 4: "THE MARTYR" — Shared Pain vs. Flagbearer
==========================================================================
Shape: Diamond (L2→L5)
Fantasy: Mutual suffering is the trunk theme. The split deepens personal
tox masochism vs. pure aura infection before converging on Condemned
(shared L4), then two distinct endgames: Plague Flag vs. Death Wish.

TREE DIAGRAM:

        L1: Shared Suffering
           /          \
          /            \
  L2a: Condemned       L2b: Sympathy Pains
       |                   |
  L3a: Martyrdom       L3b: Toxic Empathy
            \              /
             \            /
           L4: Broken Body (shared)
              /            \
             /              \
  L5a: Plague Flag      L5b: Death Wish
       (capstone)          (capstone)

TRUNK:

L1 — SHARED SUFFERING (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: When YOU gain toxicity from any source, all enemies in a
          3-tile radius gain 25% of the amount (rounded down, min 1).
          E.g. gain 20 tox: nearby enemies gain 5 each. Does not recurse
          from the reflected amount (no loops).
  Desc: "You go down, the neighborhood goes with you."

BRANCH SPLIT AT L2:

L2a — CONDEMNED (stat)
  Perk type: stat
  Effect: { "tolerance": 4, "tox_resistance": -25 }
  +4 Tolerance, -25% Toxicity Resistance. The resistance penalty is
  intentional — more tox flowing in means more flowing out to enemies
  via Shared Suffering. High risk, high contagion.
  Desc: "+4 TOL, -25% tox resist. The worse it gets for you, the worse
        it gets for everyone."

L2b — SYMPATHY PAINS (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: { "constitution": 2, "street_smarts": 2 }
  +2 CON, +2 STS. Shared Suffering's range increases from 3 tiles to
  5 tiles. Additionally, when an enemy GAINS toxicity for any reason
  (not just your Shared Suffering), you gain 10% of that amount as
  temporary HP (a shield, not real HP; maximum 30 temp HP at any time).
  Every enemy's suffering slightly sustains you.
  Desc: "+2 CON, +2 STS. 5-tile aura. Their tox feeds your shield."

L3a — MARTYRDOM (activated)
  Perk type: activated
  Ability: martyrdom_b
  Charge type: INFINITE (60t cooldown)
  Target type: SELF
  Effect: 60t cd. Voluntarily self-harm: lose 15% max HP (min 1 HP)
          and instantly gain 50 toxicity. Shared Suffering immediately
          fires from this gain, spreading ~12 tox to nearby enemies.
          If you are already at 100+ tox when used, also gain a 3-turn
          Condemned buff: +20% damage dealt, immune to stuns.
  Desc: "60t cd. Hurt yourself, hurt the room. The trade works out."

L3b — TOXIC EMPATHY (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Shared Suffering is upgraded: now transfers 40% of your gained
          tox to nearby enemies (up from 25%), and affects a 5-tile radius.
          Additionally, when any enemy in LOS dies at 30+ toxicity, you
          gain 4 HP (a modest recovery that rewards letting poison do the
          work). Can only trigger once per enemy per death.
  Desc: "40% transfer, 5-tile radius. They carry a piece of everything
        you suffer."

SHARED CONVERGENCE NODE:

L4 — BROKEN BODY (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Stat bonus: { "tolerance": 2, "constitution": 2 }
  Effect: +2 TOL, +2 CON. Your tox multiplier on OUTGOING melee damage
          is now active (same formula as incoming, applied to output):
          melee damage is multiplied by 1 + (your_tox/200)^0.6.
          At 100 tox: ~1.66x outgoing damage. At 200 tox: 2.0x outgoing.
          This stacks with your incoming multiplier — at 200 tox you
          both take and deal double damage. Also: you can no longer
          die from toxicity damage alone (if tox would reduce you to 0
          HP, it leaves you at 1 HP instead; you can still die from
          attacks). A mercy rule for the full Condemned build.
  Desc: "+2 TOL, +2 CON. Your tox powers your fists. You can't die
        to the poison. Only to what comes after."

FINAL SPLIT AT L5:

L5a — PLAGUE FLAG (activated) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: activated
  Ability: plague_flag_b
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (1/floor)
  Target type: SELF
  Effect: 1/floor. For 15 turns: Shared Suffering transfers at 90%
          (up from 25%/40% from earlier perks). Every point of tox you
          accumulate hammers everything in range. Additionally, your
          Broken Body outgoing melee multiplier is doubled during this
          window. At 200 personal tox during Plague Flag: outgoing
          melee is 4.0x (double the formula output). The incoming
          multiplier remains at 2.0x — you are NOT double-immune, you
          still take double damage. This is a symmetric nuclear escalation.
  Desc: "1/floor. 15 turns. 90% transfer. You're the epicenter."
  Balance: 200 tox during Plague Flag = 4x outgoing, 2x incoming.
          Requires prior turns of tox buildup. 15 turns is enough to
          clear a room if the momentum is already there.

L5b — DEATH WISH (passive) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: When your HP drops below 25% of max: gain 60 toxicity
          instantly (firing Shared Suffering) and your melee attacks
          apply 15 tox to targets for the rest of the floor (passive
          on-hit, no action). This can only trigger ONCE per floor.
          Additionally, while below 25% HP, the Broken Body mercy rule
          is extended: you cannot die from any damage source for 3 turns
          after Death Wish triggers (a very brief invulnerability
          converted into a last-stand). After 3 turns, normal dying
          resumes. Use the window.
  Desc: "One time per floor. Drop to 25%, become nuclear. 3-turn window.
        Make them count."
  Balance: The no-death window is only 3 turns. At 25% HP with 60
          injected tox, you're outputting maximized damage while the
          Shared Suffering hammers every adjacent enemy. But you're also
          at 1/4 HP with tox multiplier damage incoming. The window
          is the whole game.

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES (V4):
  - Broken Body outgoing multiplier: new check in combat.py player
    melee attack after base damage calculation — if player has perk,
    multiply by 1 + (player.toxicity/200)^0.6
  - broken_body_no_tox_death: flag read in tox_damage tick; if tox
    damage would reduce player to <= 0, leave at 1 HP
  - death_wish_triggered_this_floor: bool flag, prevents re-trigger
  - death_wish_invuln_turns: int countdown (3 turns)
  - plague_flag_active: engine.chem_plague_flag_active, 15-turn countdown
  - sympathy_pains_temp_hp: capped at 30, added to entity.temp_hp
    whenever any enemy gains tox (hook in add_toxicity call site)

NEW ABILITY IDs (V4): martyrdom_b, plague_flag_b

VARIATION 5: "THE BIOLOGIST" — Hazard Doctrine vs. Subject Study
==========================================================================
Shape: Diamond (L2→L5)
Fantasy: Two approaches to scientific toxicology split from a shared
knowledge base, converge on mastery of tox-multiplier mechanics, then
fork into either total terrain saturation or a single-subject kill lab.

TREE DIAGRAM:

        L1: Field Research
           /          \
          /            \
  L2a: Dead Zone       L2b: Exposed Subject
       |                   |
  L3a: Biohazard       L3b: Controlled
       Perimeter             Experiment
            \              /
             \            /
      L4: Tox Mastery (shared)
              /           \
             /             \
  L5a: Saturation        L5b: Kill Lab
       Protocol              (capstone)
       (capstone)

TRUNK:

L1 — FIELD RESEARCH (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Stat bonus: { "book_smarts": 1, "street_smarts": 1 }
  Effect: +1 BKS, +1 STS. You gain 3x Chemical Warfare XP for every
          enemy killed at 40+ toxicity (instead of the normal rate).
          This powerful XP multiplier rewards investing in tox delivery
          early and makes Chemical Warfare the fastest skill to level for
          dedicated builds.
  Desc: "+1 BKS, +1 STS. Data collection. The more toxic the kill,
        the more you learn."

  Perk type: passive

          potentially triggering more friendly fire. Requires patience

FINAL SPLIT AT L5:

L5a — EPIDEMIC (passive) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: For the rest of the run: any time any enemy takes tox damage
          (from any source — hazard, passive, ability, Patient Zero tick),
          there is a 35% chance a random OTHER visible enemy also gains
          6 tox (no line-of-sight required for the secondary target).
          This is a permanent passive — always on, no activation. Combined
          with Outbreak and Viral Load's death burst, this creates a
          permanent background tox propagation across the whole floor.
          Against 8+ enemies in a room, the 35% random bounce means
          roughly 2-3 enemies gain an extra 6 tox every turn from passive
          chain effects alone. Also grants: +5 Chemical Warfare XP per
          enemy that dies at 60+ toxicity (the bonus rewards stacking
          tox before converting it to kills).
  Desc: "Permanent: 35% chance any tox damage bounces to another
        visible enemy. The contagion runs itself."
  Balance: The 35% bounce is roughly 1 extra tox event per 3 damage
          events. In active combat with 5 enemies taking tox damage,
          that's ~1-2 extra tox applications per turn. Modest per tick,
          compound over a floor. Strongest against groups (8+ enemies),
          weaker vs. solo targets.

L5b — PLAGUE FLAG (activated) — CAPSTONE
  Perk type: activated
  Ability: plague_flag_lg
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (1/floor)
  Target type: SELF
  Effect: 1/floor. For 18 turns: your own toxicity-gaining events
          trigger a mass shared-suffering broadcast — every time YOU
          gain tox, all visible enemies gain 40% of that amount.
          Additionally, your melee damage during this window is
          multiplied by 1 + (your_tox/200)^0.6 (the same formula as
          incoming damage — your tox now powers your output too).
          During Plague Flag, Outbreak (if not already used) gains
          a bonus: its +tox-equals-current effect is doubled (enemies
          gain current_tox * 2 instead of current_tox + 10). Use the
          flag as the escalation closer: pre-build tox with Patient Zero
          and Incubation Period, fire Outbreak mid-flag, and convert the
          whole room's tox into a kill window.
  Desc: "1/floor, 18 turns. Your tox spreads at 40% to all visible
        enemies. Your tox powers your melee. Outbreak doubles if unused.
        The three systems together end rooms."
  Balance: The melee tox multiplier is the same formula combat.py
          already applies to incoming damage — zero new math, just
          applied to the output side. At 150 personal tox: ~1.87x melee
          output. The 40% shared suffering is meaningful but not
          broken: at 20 tox gain, enemies gain 8 each. The real power
          is the Outbreak doubling — used mid-flag with pre-built tox,
          this can push rooms of enemies to 80-100+ tox in one turn.
