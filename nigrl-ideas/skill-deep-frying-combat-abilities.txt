================================================================================
DEEP-FRYING SKILL TREE — COMBAT ABILITIES DESIGN
NIGRL Roguelike | Design Doc v1.0 | 2026-03-28
================================================================================

CONCEPT OVERVIEW
----------------
Deep-Frying is being reworked as the DPS-focused food skill, contrasting with
Munching's defensive/sustain role. Where Munching heals and buffs, Deep-Frying
weaponizes the kitchen — boiling oil as a ranged nuke, grease slicks as terrain
denial, and the deep fryer itself as a combat amplifier.

Primary stat: Constitution (CON)
Secondary stat: Tolerance (TOL)

This gives tanky CON-heavy characters a damage outlet. High CON = high HP AND
high ability damage — a "brawny chef" archetype that doesn't rely on STR (melee),
STS (crits), or BKS (spells).

KEY CALIBRATION NOTES
---------------------
- CON range in play: 6–12 base, plus ring/temp bonuses. Typical tanky build: 10.
- Fry Shot (L1): damage = max(1, CON + 2 - DEF). At CON 10 vs DEF 2: 10 dmg.
- Enemy HP floors 1-2: roughly 10-30 HP. Floor 3-4: 25-60 HP.
- Ignite ticks 1 dmg/stack/turn. Grease Fire reaction: stacks*2 burst + stacks*2
  added ignite. Greasy on enemies (debuff): makes them flammable (Grease Fire).
- "Greasy" on enemies uses the same effect but category="buff" isn't relevant to
  enemies since they have no dodge to grant; the fire synergy still fires.
  NEW: A "greasy_debuff" enemy-specific effect is proposed below that is clearly
  a debuff category and does NOT give dodge (see Ability 7 note). Alternatively,
  reuse existing "greasy" — the Grease Fire synergy already works against enemies.
- IMPORTANT: The existing GreasyEffect on enemies already triggers Grease Fire
  when Ignite is applied — all abilities that stack Greasy + Ignite on enemies
  will naturally combo without new code.

EXISTING TREE (for context)
-----------------------------
L1 — Fry Shot (activated):
  Hurl hot grease at target within 4 tiles.
  Dmg: max(1, CON + 2 - DEF). Applies 3 Greasy stacks. 15t cooldown.
  Also deals deep-frying XP = damage dealt.

L2 — Extra Greasy (passive):
  Fried food items restore +20% HP when eaten.

THE 10 NEW ABILITIES (L3–L10 CANDIDATES)
=========================================

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ABILITY 1: SPLATTER  (Recommended: L3)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Type: Activated (cooldown)
Target Type: AOE_CIRCLE (radius 1, centered on adjacent/near tile)
Flavor: You hurl the deep fryer basket forward in a wide arc, flinging scalding
        oil and half-cooked grease in a 3x3 splash. Kitchen warfare at its finest.

Mechanical Specification:
  - Player targets a tile within 4 tiles (SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS targeting, but AOE)
  - All entities in a 3x3 radius (Chebyshev distance 1) around target tile take:
      Dmg = max(1, CON - 2 - DEF)   [weaker than Fry Shot since it hits multiple]
  - All targets hit receive 2 Greasy stacks (duration 20)
  - Cooldown: 20 turns
  - CON scaling: base is CON - 2; at CON 8 = 6 dmg raw, CON 12 = 10 dmg raw

Implementation Notes:
  - Ability ID: "splatter"
  - target_type=TargetType.SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS + custom execute_at that gathers all
    entities within Chebyshev(1) of (tx, ty), deals damage to each, applies greasy
  - get_affected_tiles: returns all tiles in 3x3 around (tx,ty) for cursor highlight
  - Award Deep-Frying XP = total damage dealt across all targets

Balance:
  - Weaker per-hit than Fry Shot but hits 3-9 targets in a pack
  - Stacking Greasy on multiple enemies sets up chain Grease Fires if player has
    any Ignite source (Pyromancy cross-skill synergy, torch items, Hot Cheetos)
  - At CON 10: 8 dmg per target in the AOE; decent but not overwhelming
  - 20t cooldown = slower cadence than Fry Shot, but serves a different role

Synergies:
  - Every Greasy stack from Splatter is primed for Grease Fire if enemy gets Ignite
  - Pairs well with a torch, Bic Lighter, or Pyromancy cross-skill build
  - Deep Fryer Basket weapon's on_hit_ignite_if_greasy triggers on melee follow-up

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ABILITY 2: POP THE OIL  (Recommended: L3 or L4)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Type: Passive
Target Type: N/A (on_player_melee_hit hook)
Flavor: Every time your fist or weapon makes contact, you hear that satisfying
        POP of hot oil releasing. The enemy gets a face full.

Mechanical Specification:
  - Passive: on every successful player melee hit, 25% + (CON * 2)% chance to
    apply 1 Greasy stack to the target (duration 20).
  - Formula: chance = min(0.75, 0.25 + CON * 0.02)
    - CON 6:  37% chance
    - CON 8:  41% chance
    - CON 10: 45% chance
    - CON 12: 49% chance
    (Max 75% at very high effective CON from bonuses)
  - No limit on stacks applied; each melee hit rerolls

Implementation Notes:
  - Ability ID: "pop_the_oil"
  - is_passive=True; registered in ABILITY_REGISTRY
  - perk_type="activated" with {"ability": "pop_the_oil"} but is_passive=True
    so UI shows "(passive)" label
  - Requires a new effect subclass OR can be handled via a new passive hook in
    the skill unlock system (engine checks skill level on each melee hit, similar
    to how Smoking's "Pack a Day" is checked)
  - Recommended: add check in combat.py's melee resolution block, after
    "Grease on-hit chance (e.g. Deep Fryer Basket)" block:
      if engine.skills.get("Deep-Frying").level >= 3_or_4:
          chance = min(0.75, 0.25 + con * 0.02)
          if random.random() < chance:
              apply_effect(defender, engine, "greasy", ...)

Balance:
  - At CON 10, ~45% chance = roughly every other melee hit. Moderate grease
    buildup during prolonged fights.
  - Softens transition from "Fry Shot only" to having persistent grease on enemies
  - Combined with Fry Shot, enemy can quickly reach 4-5 Greasy stacks (Grease
    Fire reaction at 5 stacks = 10 burst dmg + 10 ignite stacks)
  - Pure passive — no action cost, no cooldown. Balanced by the probabilistic
    nature and the fact that CON only caps at 12 base.

Synergies:
  - Multiplies value of any Ignite source
  - Combos with Deep Fryer Basket weapon (also has 20% on-hit grease + ignite-if-
    greasy procs — two independent grease rolls per melee hit at L4+)
  - Stacks with Splatter — use Fry Shot, then Splatter, then melee follow-up

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ABILITY 3: DEEP FRY ZONE  (Recommended: L4)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Type: Activated (per-floor charges)
Target Type: ADJACENT_TILE (places a hazard on an adjacent tile)
Flavor: You splash a puddle of searing hot oil on the floor tiles. Anyone who
        walks through it is getting Greasy whether they like it or not.

Mechanical Specification:
  - Places an oil_slick hazard on a target adjacent tile (any non-wall tile)
  - Oil slick: hazard_type="oil_slick", duration=15 turns, char='~', color=(220,180,50)
  - Any entity that steps onto or through an oil_slick tile during movement:
      - Receives 2 Greasy stacks (duration 20)
      - Has 30% chance to lose balance (apply slow: -30% energy gain, 2 turns)
        [new effect: "slip" — see implementation notes]
  - The player does NOT trigger their own oil slick
  - 2 charges per floor

CON / TOL Scaling:
  - Duration: 10 + CON turns (CON 6=16t, CON 10=20t, CON 12=22t)
  - Greasy stacks applied: 2 + floor(TOL / 4) (TOL 6=3, TOL 8=4, TOL 12=5)
  - At TOL 10: 4 stacks. At TOL 12: 5 stacks. (Encourages TOL investment)

Implementation Notes:
  - Ability ID: "deep_fry_zone"
  - Requires new hazard_type="oil_slick" in hazards.py
  - In _run_energy_loop step 4 or in do_ai_turn movement processing: check if
    entity's new tile has an oil_slick hazard, apply greasy + slip
  - New "slip" effect: simple slow effect, category="debuff", -30% energy gain,
    2t duration. OR reuse existing slow effect if one exists.
  - The oil_slick entity: char='~', blocks_movement=False, hazard_duration field
    (counts down, entity removed when expired)
  - This follows the frost_patch pattern proposed elsewhere in memory

Balance:
  - 2 charges/floor = meaningful but not spammable
  - Strong in corridors (enemies must walk through). Weaker in open rooms.
  - Hazard persists 16-22 turns — enemy patrol paths will trigger it repeatedly
  - Combined with a fire source, the oil slick becomes a "lit oil puddle" zone
    (player applies Ignite to enemy entering, Grease Fire instantly triggers)

Synergies:
  - Best combo: place oil_slick, then kite enemy through it, then use Fry Shot
    to trigger Grease Fire (Greasy from slick + Ignite from Fry Shot)
  - Pyromancy L5 Wildfire: enemies burning on an oil slick spread fire naturally
  - Corridor chokepoint denial — place it in the only path, back up, let enemies
    come to you (fits the CON = tanky, patient playstyle)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ABILITY 4: PORK RIND ARMOR  (Recommended: L5)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Type: Activated (cooldown)
Target Type: SELF
Flavor: You inhale deeply and coat your skin in a thick layer of crystallized
        fried pork skin. It's disgusting. It also stops knives.

Mechanical Specification:
  - Grants temp HP equal to CON * 3
    - CON 6:  18 temp HP
    - CON 8:  24 temp HP
    - CON 10: 30 temp HP
    - CON 12: 36 temp HP
  - Additionally: while the temp HP is active, melee attackers that hit the
    player have a 40% chance to receive 1 Greasy stack (the grease "rubs off")
  - The temp HP functions as normal temp HP (entity.temp_hp field; absorbs before
    regular HP; damage absorption order: Temp HP → Armor → HP)
  - Temp HP lasts until depleted (no duration limit)
  - Cooldown: 25 turns

Implementation Notes:
  - Ability ID: "pork_rind_armor"
  - execute: add engine.player.temp_hp += con*3; set engine.ability_cooldowns["pork_rind_armor"] = 25
  - The "attacker gets greasy on hit" — requires a new player stat flag or a
    temporary buff effect: "pork_rind_active" that has an on_incoming_melee_hit
    hook (fires when player is hit by melee).
    New lifecycle hook needed: on_incoming_melee_hit(engine, attacker) — called
    in combat.py when an enemy successfully hits the player.
    OR: check engine.player_stats for a pork_rind_charges field that decrements
    as temp HP is consumed, then check in combat.py hit handler.
  - Simplest implementation: store engine.pork_rind_armor_hp = CON*3 separately
    from temp_hp; when that pool > 0, absorb hits and roll 40% grease on attacker.
    When pool reaches 0, flag clears. This avoids needing a new lifecycle hook.

Balance:
  - CON 10 = 30 temp HP = absorbs roughly 3-5 hits from floor 3-4 enemies
  - 25t cooldown keeps it from being permanent uptime
  - The "rub off grease" punishes melee-heavy enemies, setting up Grease Fire
    if player has any ignite source after
  - Synergizes with high CON — the same stat driving ability damage also drives
    this defensive layer. CON investment rewards are clear.
  - Does NOT scale with Tolerance on purpose (keeps CON as the primary stat)

Synergies:
  - Enemies that proc Grease Fire while attacking = they hurt themselves for
    applying the grease (Grease Fire triggers on their own greasy stacks when
    player fires Fry Shot at them afterward)
  - Pairs with Pop the Oil (L3-4): melee attacker gets greasy from armor rub-off,
    player counter-attacks and also procs Pop the Oil → double greasy application

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ABILITY 5: GREASE TRAP  (Recommended: L5 or L6)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Type: Activated (per-floor)
Target Type: SELF (instant, global)
Flavor: You've been cooking long enough to know: control the grease, control the
        fight. Every enemy on the floor gets a little taste of what you're working
        with.

Mechanical Specification:
  - Instantly applies 2 Greasy stacks to ALL visible enemies (within FOV)
  - Duration: 20 turns
  - Additionally: the next time any Greasy enemy takes damage from any source
    (not just fire), deal bonus damage equal to their current Greasy stack count
    [this is a one-time bonus per enemy that resets when Greasy is consumed by
    Grease Fire]
  - 1 charge per floor (extremely powerful, very limited)

CON Scaling:
  - Greasy stacks applied: 1 + floor(CON / 4)
    - CON 6:  2 stacks
    - CON 8:  3 stacks
    - CON 10: 3 stacks
    - CON 12: 4 stacks

Implementation Notes:
  - Ability ID: "grease_trap"
  - execute: iterate engine.dungeon.get_monsters(); if visible (in dungeon.visible),
    apply 1+CON//4 Greasy stacks to each
  - The "bonus damage on next hit" could be handled by a new "primed_grease" flag
    on each enemy entity OR by a brief "grease trap marked" effect (dur=30;
    modify_incoming_damage adds entity's current greasy stack count to damage once,
    then clears the flag).
  - Simplest: just apply the Greasy stacks without the bonus damage — even without
    it, mass-Greasy on all visible enemies is a powerful setup for Ignite combo.
    The bonus damage adds depth at cost of implementation complexity.

Balance:
  - 1 charge/floor makes it a "big moment" ability
  - On CON 10 with 4 visible enemies: 12 Greasy stacks distributed = significant
    Grease Fire potential if player has ignite source
  - Arguably the most powerful utility ability — hence 1 charge/floor
  - The per-enemy greasy stack count matters: enemies accumulate stacks over time
    from Fry Shot, Splatter, Pop the Oil — Grease Trap tops them all off

Synergies:
  - Immediate synergy with Fry Shot: Grease Trap → Fry Shot on nearest enemy →
    Grease Fire on that enemy → ignite spreads (Pyromania Wildfire if cross-build)
  - All enemies Greasy makes them slow on oil slick if placed nearby
  - Deep Fryer Basket weapon: already has 20% on-hit ignite-if-greasy — after
    Grease Trap, every weapon hit has 20% to auto-trigger Grease Fire

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ABILITY 6: BURNING LARD  (Recommended: L6)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Type: Activated (cooldown)
Target Type: LINE_FROM_PLAYER (fire in a direction)
Flavor: You pour a stream of burning lard from your fryer in a wide arc,
        leaving a river of fire that everything in its path has to cross.

Mechanical Specification:
  - Fire a 6-tile line of burning grease in a chosen direction
  - Every entity in the line takes: Dmg = max(1, CON + TOL - DEF)
    - CON 10 + TOL 8 vs DEF 2 = 16 dmg
    - CON 10 + TOL 10 vs DEF 2 = 18 dmg
    - CON 12 + TOL 12 vs DEF 2 = 22 dmg
  - Every entity in the line receives 3 Greasy stacks (duration 20)
  - Each tile the line passes through becomes an oil_slick hazard (duration 10)
    [leaves a burning trail on the floor using the oil_slick entity from Ability 3]
  - Cooldown: 25 turns

CON/TOL Scaling:
  - Damage uses BOTH CON + TOL (encourages balanced investment)
  - Cooldown reduction if TOL >= 10: 20 turns instead of 25

Implementation Notes:
  - Ability ID: "burning_lard"
  - target_type=TargetType.LINE_FROM_PLAYER
  - execute_at: trace the 6-tile ray; for each tile in ray:
      - If entity present: deal CON+TOL-DEF damage, apply 3 Greasy stacks
      - Place oil_slick hazard at that tile (or extend existing one)
  - If oil_slick hazard system from Ability 3 is implemented, reuse it here
  - If oil_slick not yet implemented: tiles just apply Greasy to any entity that
    steps on them later (store via a set engine.oil_slick_tiles with expiry ticks)
  - Award Deep-Frying XP = total damage dealt across all targets

Balance:
  - LINE_FROM_PLAYER means player must position well — corridor use is excellent
  - The trail of oil_slick persists for 10t = corridor denial after the initial hit
  - Damage is the highest raw single-target equivalent of any frying ability:
    CON+TOL scaling rewards builds that invest in both
  - At typical CON 10 + TOL 8: 16 dmg on a line — more than Fry Shot (10 dmg)
    but no defense pierce, can miss via line geometry

Synergies:
  - Immediately primes the line for Grease Fire if enemy survives and gets Ignite
  - Oil_slick trail = Burning Lard IS the floor denial + the damage ability
  - Cross-skill: Pyromancy Bic Lighter after Burning Lard = line full of Greasy
    enemies immediately hit with flame from a follow-up Ignite source
  - Rolling skill (smoke cloud) + Burning Lard trail = smoke + fire combo

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ABILITY 7: FULL RENDER  (Recommended: L7)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Type: Passive
Target Type: N/A (modifies Grease Fire synergy trigger)
Flavor: You've learned to render fat so completely that when your grease ignites,
        it doesn't just burst — it keeps burning. The fire never fully goes out.

Mechanical Specification:
  - PASSIVE: Modifies the Grease Fire reaction globally for any enemy the player
    causes to Grease Fire.
  - Without Full Render: Grease Fire = stacks*2 burst damage + stacks*2 ignite
    stacks added. Greasy consumed.
  - With Full Render active:
    - The burst damage becomes stacks * (2 + CON / 5) [rounds down]
      - CON 8:  stacks * 3
      - CON 10: stacks * 4
      - CON 12: stacks * 4 (4.4 rounds to 4)
    - Greasy is NOT fully consumed — half the stacks remain (floor(stacks/2)),
      so a second Grease Fire can trigger on the same enemy if they get more Ignite
    - The ignite stacks added = original stacks * 3 instead of * 2
  - Only affects enemy Grease Fires (not player self-grease-fire)

Implementation Notes:
  - Ability ID: "full_render"
  - is_passive=True
  - The _check_grease_fire_synergy() function in effects.py needs to check
    engine.skills.get("Deep-Frying").level >= 7 (or check a flag) to modify:
    - damage = greasy_stacks * (2 + con // 5)
    - bonus_stacks = greasy_stacks * 3
    - Only remove floor(greasy_stacks / 2) greasy stacks instead of all
  - Alternatively: set engine.player_stats.full_render_active = True as a
    PlayerStats bool flag, and check it in _check_grease_fire_synergy()
  - New PlayerStats field: full_render_active (bool, default False)

Balance:
  - Multiplicatively amplifies ALL previous abilities — every Greasy stack from
    L1-L6 now deals more on Grease Fire trigger
  - At CON 10: burst goes from stacks*2 to stacks*4. With 5 stacks: 20 burst dmg
    instead of 10. Significant.
  - The partial Greasy preservation is the truly dangerous part — enemies that
    survive a Grease Fire are still half-Greasy, ready for a second trigger
  - Appropriately late (L7) since it amplifies existing tools rather than adding new
  - Passive with no cost — this IS the payoff for investing deeply in the tree

Synergies:
  - Every ability in the tree (Fry Shot, Splatter, Pop the Oil, Grease Trap,
    Burning Lard) now has higher burst ceiling
  - Stacking Deep Fryer Basket weapon proc rate with Full Render = constant
    re-triggering Grease Fires on survivors
  - Anti-boss: boss enemies that survive the first Grease Fire are still primed
    for the second. Good against high-HP targets.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ABILITY 8: THERMITE BASTE  (Recommended: L7 or L8)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Type: Activated (total charges)
Target Type: ADJACENT (touch range)
Flavor: You apply a thick baste of pure alchemical grease directly to one enemy.
        Within moments, they are coated in something that is not merely flammable
        but actively wants to burn. Three uses. Use them wisely.

Mechanical Specification:
  - Instantly applies MAX Greasy stacks (10 stacks, duration 30) to an adjacent
    enemy — the maximum possible in one application
  - Also applies 3 Ignite stacks immediately (triggers Grease Fire instantly)
  - The Grease Fire from this is guaranteed; the player is applying both triggers
    at once intentionally
  - Total charges: 3 (rare, precious — this is a finisher or boss tool)
  - No cooldown beyond charge consumption

CON Scaling:
  - The immediate Ignite stacks = 1 + CON // 3
    - CON 6:  3 ignite stacks
    - CON 8:  3 ignite stacks
    - CON 9:  4 ignite stacks
    - CON 12: 5 ignite stacks
  - With Full Render (L7): Grease Fire burst = 10 * (2 + CON//5)
    - CON 10: 10 * 4 = 40 burst damage
    - CON 12: 10 * 4 = 40 burst damage + 5 ignite stacks ongoing

Implementation Notes:
  - Ability ID: "thermite_baste"
  - target_type=TargetType.ADJACENT (quick-select; auto-fires if 1 adjacent enemy)
  - execute_at: apply 10 Greasy stacks, then apply 1+CON//3 Ignite stacks
    (Grease Fire triggers automatically via _check_grease_fire_synergy on the
    ignite application — no special code needed)
  - charge_type=ChargeType.TOTAL, max_charges=3

Balance:
  - Only 3 total charges for the run — must be saved for dangerous situations
  - Without Full Render: 10*2 = 20 burst + 20 ignite stacks = massive but not
    infinite damage. Floor 4 boss likely survives.
  - With Full Render (CON 10): 40 burst + 40 ignite stacks. Against a 60 HP boss
    at full health, this is potentially 40 burst + ~40 more ignite ticks = 80+
    effective damage over time. Boss-killing territory if positioned correctly.
  - TOUCH RANGE: player must be adjacent. High risk for high reward. Permadeath
    implication: player is next to a dangerous boss while using this.

Synergies:
  - The absolute best Grease Fire trigger in the tree; everything else sets up,
    this finishes
  - Cross-skill: any CON buff (temporary or permanent) directly amplifies ignite
    stacks and (via Full Render) the burst multiplier
  - Can be used mid-floor to save a difficult fight: 3 charges = 3 emergencies

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ABILITY 9: THE LARD IS MY SHEPHERD  (Recommended: L9)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Type: Passive
Target Type: N/A (on-kill trigger)
Flavor: Every enemy that dies by grease and fire blesses you. Their fat renders
        into your body. You grow heavier, greasier, and harder to kill.

Mechanical Specification:
  - PASSIVE: When the player kills an enemy that dies while having the Greasy
    effect (at any stack count), the player gains:
    - Temp HP equal to half the dead enemy's Greasy stack count (min 1)
    - 1 Greasy stack on self (the player absorbs the enemy's grease)
      [player Greasy = +3% dodge per stack, already implemented]
    - Deep-Frying XP = 10 * greasy_stacks_on_kill
  - This creates a sustain loop: killing Greasy enemies feeds the player

CON Scaling:
  - Temp HP gained = max(1, floor(greasy_stacks / 2)) + floor(CON / 5)
    - CON 8:  +1 temp HP bonus per kill
    - CON 10: +2 temp HP bonus per kill
    - CON 12: +2 temp HP bonus per kill
  - At 5 greasy stacks + CON 10: floor(5/2) + 2 = 4 temp HP per kill

Implementation Notes:
  - Ability ID: "lard_is_my_shepherd"
  - is_passive=True
  - Check in the "entity_died" event handler or after kill confirmation in
    combat.py: check engine.skills.get("Deep-Frying").level >= 9; check if
    dead entity had Greasy stacks; award temp HP and player Greasy stack
  - New engine event: on kill with greasy condition — best added to the
    entity_died event bus handler in engine.py under "killer == engine.player"
  - Deep-Frying XP: call engine.skills.gain_potential_exp("Deep-Frying", 10*stacks)

Balance:
  - Modest heal per kill (2-5 temp HP typically)
  - Encourages the "grease everything, then kill" loop rather than greasing and
    running. Active engagement with the Greasy system.
  - Deep-Frying XP from kills accelerates later skill levels (L9-L10 XP curve
    is steep: 25,000 and 100,000 potential XP required)
  - Player building Greasy stacks on themselves (dodge) while also healing per kill
    creates a durable aggressive playstyle

Synergies:
  - Grease Trap (L5) + cleave/Splatter = multiple enemies Greasy = multiple kills
    each granting temp HP in one turn
  - Works passively during melee if Pop the Oil (L3-4) is applying grease on hit
  - Anti-attrition: allows longer dungeon floors without needing food, if you keep
    kills coming

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ABILITY 10: THE FORBIDDEN DEEP FRY  (Recommended: L10, Capstone)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Type: Activated (ONCE per run)
Target Type: SELF → explodes outward AOE
Flavor: You pour everything — every drop of oil, every ounce of grease, every
        bubbling vat of scalding fat you've ever cooked with — over yourself and
        the entire floor. It is terrifying. It is glorious. It is totally
        irreversible.

Mechanical Specification:
  - Targets SELF; immediately affects all entities on the entire current floor
  - Phase 1 (instant): All non-player entities on the floor receive:
    - Greasy stacks = 5 + floor(CON / 3)   (CON 10 = 8 stacks, CON 12 = 9 stacks)
    - Ignite stacks = floor(CON / 2)         (CON 10 = 5 stacks, CON 12 = 6 stacks)
    - Grease Fire triggers on ALL of them simultaneously:
        Burst = greasy_stacks * 2 (base) or greasy_stacks * (2 + CON//5) with Full Render
        + ignite stacks continue ticking
  - Phase 2 (player): Player also gains:
    - 3 Greasy stacks on self (+9% dodge)
    - Temp HP = CON * 5 (CON 10 = 50 temp HP; a full shield)
    - BUT player takes Ignite (3 stacks, 5 turns) — you're covered in grease too.
      The player ignite is a non-negotiable cost.
  - Phase 3 (environment): All floor tiles become oil_slick for 20 turns
    (every tile on the floor is slicked — walking costs are dangerous)
  - ONCE per run. No reset, no refund.

CON Scaling (summary):
  - Enemy Greasy stacks: 5 + CON//3  →  CON 6=7, CON 8=7, CON 10=8, CON 12=9
  - Enemy Ignite stacks: CON//2      →  CON 6=3, CON 8=4, CON 10=5, CON 12=6
  - With Full Render (L7): burst per enemy = 8*(2+2)=32 to 9*(2+2)=36 raw burst,
    BEFORE ignite ticks
  - Player temp HP: CON * 5          →  CON 6=30, CON 10=50, CON 12=60

Permadeath Consideration:
  - Player takes 3 ignite stacks = 3 dmg/turn for 5 turns = 15 damage total
  - At full HP (CON 10 = 130 HP) this is trivial; but if already wounded, this
    can be the killing blow
  - The entire floor becoming oil_slick means the player can ALSO slip if moving
  - This is intentional: the capstone is powerful but leaves the player in
    a vulnerable, burning, oil-covered mess. NIGRL humor.

Implementation Notes:
  - Ability ID: "forbidden_deep_fry"
  - target_type=TargetType.SELF
  - charge_type=ChargeType.ONCE
  - execute: iterate ALL entities in dungeon (monsters + player):
      For non-player: apply greasy stacks + ignite stacks (grease fire auto-triggers)
      For player: apply greasy (3 stacks), apply temp HP, apply ignite (3 stacks)
  - Place oil_slick hazards on ALL walkable non-wall floor tiles:
      iterate dungeon tiles; for each passable tile without existing hazard, spawn
      oil_slick entity (or set a per-tile flag engine.floor_oil_slick = True with
      expiry counter, to avoid creating thousands of hazard entities)
  - Best implementation for floor-wide oil: add engine.floor_oil_slick_turns counter
    (int, countdown from 20). In _run_energy_loop movement check: if counter > 0,
    treat every floor tile as oil_slick for movement resolution.
  - New engine state: floor_oil_slick_turns (int, default 0; countdown per turn)
  - Award massive Deep-Frying XP: sum of all damage dealt (significant XP for an
    already-maxed-tree player, but satisfying)

Balance:
  - Once per run — used at a pivotal moment (boss floor, overwhelming ambush,
    final floor)
  - Against a floor with 6 enemies at CON 10, no Full Render:
    8 greasy stacks each → 8*2=16 burst + 5 ignite stacks (5 dmg/turn for 5t)
    = 16+25 = 41 effective dmg per enemy. With 6 enemies = 246 effective damage.
  - With Full Render: 8*(4)=32 burst per enemy = 192 burst damage across 6 enemies
    in one action. Effectively ends the floor.
  - Cost: 15 self-damage from ignite + risk from oil-slick floor movement
  - This is the Munching tree equivalent of a "eat all your food at once" panic
    button, but offensive. The flavor and the numbers both deliver on the fantasy.

Synergies:
  - Full Render (L7) is the single biggest multiplier — getting to L10 means L7
    is already unlocked; every run that uses The Forbidden Deep Fry has Full Render
  - Thermite Baste (L8) with 3 charges: use 2 before Forbidden Deep Fry to weaken
    the boss, then Forbidden Deep Fry to clear the room
  - Smoking tree: if player is fire-immune at L3+ (Pyromania branch), the self-
    ignite from Forbidden Deep Fry is free damage with no cost. Major cross-skill

CROSS-SKILL SYNERGIES SUMMARY
================================
  - Pyromancy/Pyromania: BEST cross-skill. Any Ignite source (Bic Lighter, Breathe
    Fire, Wildfire spread) instantly triggers Grease Fire on Greasy enemies. A
    Pyromancy/Deep-Frying hybrid build is the "Chef on Fire" archetype.
  - Smoking (Fiery Fists if implemented): melee attacks apply ignite — combined
    with Pop the Oil's passive grease, every melee hit is a potential Grease Fire.
  - Munching: Fried food heals (Extra Greasy L2). Eating while fighting keeps HP
    up as Deep Fry Zone and Grease Trap burn the room down.
  - Stabbing (crits via STS): Thermite Baste as an opener to apply 10 Greasy,
    then stab the now-Greasy-and-ignited enemy for crit damage — total burst combo.
