WHITE POWER — 10 Branching Skill Tree Variations (Tox Inversion Edition)
========================================================================
Design doc for the NIGRL roguelike (Crack Den / Meth Lab skill).
Core mechanic: TOX INVERSION. These are BRANCHING trees with player-choice
topology. The player selects one branch at each split and cannot unlock the
other.

== DESIGN DIRECTION: TIN CAN TANK ==

White Power is the tank tree. The signature fantasy:
  - Extremely hard to kill (armor, Temp HP, DR, tox inversion)
  - Low damage output as a deliberate, identity-defining tradeoff
  - Swagger-focused: every 2 SWG above 8 = +1 flat defense
  - Tox inversion as core mechanic: absorbing the Meth Lab's poison
    makes you harder to kill but hits softer

== TOX INVERSION MECHANIC (Reference) ==

Normal tox formula (all entities):
  incoming_dmg_multiplier = 1.0 + (tox / 100) ^ 0.6
  At tox 25:  ~1.16x damage taken
  At tox 50:  ~1.26x damage taken
  At tox 100: ~1.50x damage taken
  At tox 200: ~2.00x damage taken (dangerous spiral)

White Power inverted formula (when tox_inversion_active == True):
  incoming_dmg_multiplier = 1.0 - 0.5 * (tox / 100) ^ 0.6
  outgoing_dmg_multiplier = 1.0 - 0.5 * (tox / 100) ^ 0.6

  At tox 0:   1.00x both (no change, normal play)
  At tox 25:  ~0.92x both (~8% reduction each way)
  At tox 50:  ~0.87x both (~13% reduction each way)
  At tox 100: ~0.75x both (~25% reduction each way)
  At tox 200: ~0.63x both (~37% reduction each way)

The cap on outgoing penalty is 0.63x (cannot reduce below 37% of damage).
The cap on damage reduction from inversion alone is also 0.63x (prevents
permanent near-invincibility). High tox: you're a fortress that hits like
a wet sock.

Engine flag: engine.player_stats.tox_inversion_active = False (new field)
When True, the damage_received formula in combat.py replaces the normal
tox multiplier with the inversion formula. The outgoing damage penalty
applies in _compute_player_attack_power().

NEW EFFECTS needed (this doc):
  ToxInversionEffect — passive; replaces tox multiplier sign; stacks NOT
    required (it is a mode, not a stackable buff). Applied as a permanent
    passive once unlocked, not a timed effect.
  ScabEffect — short-duration: first hit after taking damage dealt back
  FortressStanceEffect — stationary stacking DR
  IvoryAuraEffect — temp HP regen based on tox delta
  CalcifiedEffect — % of DR converts to counterattack damage
  KilnEffect — armor regenerates faster per floor segment
  SlakeEffect — tox consumed for healing in a burst
  RiotShieldEffect — blocks the next hit completely (full absorb)
  CrustEffect — each hit taken adds +1 SWG for 10 turns (capped)
  BunkerEffect — dodge chance scales inversely with current HP ratio

NEW ENGINE STATE needed (this doc):
  engine.player_stats.tox_inversion_active: bool
  engine.player_stats.wp_inversion_tox_threshold: int (default 0 = always)
  engine.wp_fortress_stacks: int (per-floor, reset on move; FortressStance)
  engine.wp_crust_stacks: int (per-floor, decaying per-turn)
  engine.wp_scab_charged: bool (per-hit, resets after firing)
  engine.wp_kiln_regen_bonus: int (armor regen per floor segment)
  engine.wp_riot_shield_charges: int (cross-floor, consumed on hit)
  engine.wp_last_armor_value: int (for Kiln tracking)
  engine.wp_bunker_mode: bool (flag for HP-scaling dodge)

== STAT REFERENCES ==
  Player HP: 30 + CON*10. At CON 10: 130 HP
  Swagger defense: int((effective_swagger - 8) / 2)
    SWG 8=0, 10=+1, 12=+2, 14=+3, 16=+4, 18=+5, 20=+6, 24=+8
  Temp HP absorbed BEFORE armor BEFORE HP
  Armor: base value refills each floor; blocked before HP
  Energy: 100/tick. +30 speed = ~23% faster.

== EXISTING WHITE POWER PERKS (in-code) ==
  L1: Bleached    — stat: +4 SWG, passive +20% tox resistance
  L2: White Out   — activated: 3/floor, gain 25 tox, +8 SWG, -25% dmg 50t
  L3: Pure        — stat: +4 SWG, double XP from tox resisted
  L4: Whitewash   — activated: +4 CON, 1/floor: consume tox/2 as Temp HP

== TREE TOPOLOGY SHAPES (same 10 as previous doc) ==
  Y-split (L3)     — L1, L2 trunk; split L3: L3a+L4a vs L3b+L4b
  Diamond          — L1; split L2; converge L4; split L5. Two capstones.
  Triple Fork      — L1, L2, L3 trunk; split L4 into three capstones
  W-shape          — L1/L2 trunk; split L3; converge L5; split L6
  Long Trunk Y     — L1-L4 trunk; split L5. Two capstones.
  Inverted Y       — Two L1 choices; two L2; converge L3; single L4 capstone
  Ladder/Zigzag    — L1 shared; L2 choose; L3 shared; L4 choose; L5 capstone
  Trident          — L1-L2 shared; triple split L3; two paths reconverge L5; one solo
  Double Diamond   — L1; split L2; converge L3; split L4; converge L5
  Asymmetric Y     — L1-L2 trunk; split L3: short cap vs long 3-node branch


==========================================================================
VARIATION 1: "THE REACTOR" — Tox Battery
==========================================================================
Shape: Y-split (L3)
Fantasy: Swallow the poison and become a walking nuclear flask. Tox
  inversion unlocked in the trunk. Branches split between pure turtle
  (keep tox high, stay inverted) and active reactor (burn tox for
  explosive defense moments).

TREE DIAGRAM:

    L1: Corroded Shell
          |
    L2: Lead Lining
          |
         / \
        /   \
  [BRANCH A]      [BRANCH B]
  L3a: Saturated     L3b: Controlled Burn
        |                    |
  L4a: Critical Mass    L4b: Meltdown Protocol
     (CAPSTONE)             (CAPSTONE)

TRUNK:

L1 — CORRODED SHELL (stat + passive)
  Perk type: stat + passive
  Stat bonus: swagger +4, tolerance +2
  Passive: TOX INVERSION unlocked (tox_inversion_active = True).
    Your toxicity no longer multiplies incoming damage — instead it reduces
    it (and your outgoing damage by the same proportion). The poison is
    working for you now.
  Engine: player_stats.tox_inversion_active = True at L1 unlock.
  Desc: "You didn't avoid the sludge. You became the sludge."
  Balance: This is the identity-defining perk. All White Power investment
    flows from here. Starting tox ~0 at game start = no immediate impact;
    full payoff arrives in the Meth Lab zone. Low risk in Crack Den.

L2 — LEAD LINING (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: +3 DR (permanent_dr on PlayerStats). Additionally, at the start
    of each floor gain Temp HP equal to your current toxicity / 5
    (round down, min 1, max 20). E.g., at 50 tox = 10 Temp HP.
  Desc: "Good insulation. Great for keeping things out."
  Balance: At mid-game tox ~25-50: 5-10 Temp HP per floor. Flat +3 DR
    meaningful against the typical 4-8 enemy damage on floor 1-2.
    Combined with L1 inversion, you're already significantly tankier.

BRANCH A — THE TURTLE: Stay Saturated

L3a — SATURATED (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: If your current toxicity is 40 or higher:
    - Gain an additional +2 DR (stacks with Lead Lining, total +5 DR)
    - Each hit you absorb generates a Scab charge (engine.wp_scab_charged).
      The next attack you make while scab-charged deals +swagger_defence
      bonus damage (then scab clears). One scab charge at a time.
  Desc: "The crust is load-bearing. Do not remove."
  Balance: Incentivizes staying toxic — synergy with Chemical Warfare
    tox-accumulation builds. Scab counterattack is modest (+2-6 dmg
    per swing depending on SWG investment) but gives the turtle a bite.

L4a — CRITICAL MASS (passive + activated) [CAPSTONE]
  Perk type: passive + activated
  Stat bonus: constitution +2, tolerance +2
  Passive: At 80+ tox, the tox inversion formula doubles in strength:
    incoming multiplier = 1.0 - 1.0 * (tox/100)^0.6
    At tox 80:  ~0.66x incoming (34% reduction)
    At tox 100: ~0.50x incoming (50% reduction — half damage at 100 tox!)
    Outgoing penalty: standard 0.75x at 100 tox (not doubled; asymmetric).
    You take much less damage than you deal at peak tox.
  Activated: 1/floor. Trigger REACTOR: instantly add 30 tox to yourself
    and activate 15-turn window where your Scab charge fires AUTOMATICALLY
    every hit (no manual attack needed; auto-fires on the same hit that
    charged it). Reactor window ends when scab fires 5 times or 15t elapses.
  Desc: "Go critical. You're a reactor, not a man."
  Balance: Strong capstone but very vulnerable to tox removal (CW
    synergy or purge accidentally destroys your defense). High tox
    also means VERY low outgoing damage. Pure survival investment.
  New ability: reactor_pulse (PER_FLOOR, 1 charge)

BRANCH B — ACTIVE REACTOR: Burn Tox for Moments

L3b — CONTROLLED BURN (passive + activated)
  Perk type: passive + activated
  Effect: Passive: +2 SWG (swagger stacks toward inversion payoff).
    Activated: 2/floor. Consume 25 tox. For 20 turns:
      - +4 SWG (stacks with stat SWG; inversion scales off total tox)
      - +15% dodge chance
      - Enemies that hit you during this window take 2 reflected tox
    After window expires: 10 tox returned (net cost: 15 tox per use).
  Desc: "Burn it clean. You'll cook it back up later."
  Balance: Tox churn loop: burn tox for a defensive window, re-accumulate
    between uses. Requires active play. Lower passive floor than Branch A.

L4b — MELTDOWN PROTOCOL (passive + activated) [CAPSTONE]
  Perk type: passive + activated
  Passive: Every 10 tox you lose (from any source — Controlled Burn,
    purges, item use) generates 3 Temp HP (up to 30 Temp HP per purge).
    Losing tox now heals you rather than exposing you.
  Activated: 1/floor. MELTDOWN: consume ALL current tox. For each 10 tox
    consumed: +2 DR for 30 turns, +3 Temp HP (immediate). Cap: 200 tox =
    +40 DR and 60 Temp HP for 30 turns. After 30 turns: -2 CON for 10
    turns (crash; recovery period). Outgoing damage -30% during DR window.
  Desc: "Every purge is a reload. Every flush is a fortress."
  Balance: Requires tox stockpiling to maximize burst. The crash means
    you need to plan exits or have tox sources ready to rebuild defense.
  New ability: meltdown_wp (PER_FLOOR, 1 charge)
  New effect: MeltdownCrashEffect (debuff: -2 CON, 10t)


==========================================================================
VARIATION 2: "THE SHELL GAME" — Armor Cycling
==========================================================================
Shape: Diamond (L1; split L2; converge L4; split L5)
Fantasy: Tox inversion in the trunk but both branches focus on ARMOR —
  generating it, refreshing it, layering it. Final split: permanent iron
  fortress vs. reactive riot shield moments.

TREE DIAGRAM:

        L1: Sheet Metal
           /       \
    [A]  /           \  [B]
  L2a: Galvanized  L2b: Corroded Hull
    L3a: Kiln          L3b: Tin Can
           \           /
        L4: Scrap Heap (converge)
           /       \
    [A]  /           \  [B]
  L5a: Iron Coffin   L5b: Riot Wall
    (CAPSTONE)         (CAPSTONE)

TRUNK:

L1 — SHEET METAL (stat + passive)
  Perk type: stat + passive
  Stat bonus: swagger +4
  Passive: TOX INVERSION unlocked. Additionally: armor capacity +3
    (base armor increased by 3 permanently; refills each floor).
  Desc: "You got more sheet metal on you than a double-wide."
  Balance: +3 armor is modest on top of inversion unlock. Armor increase
    means each floor you start with 3 more points of damage absorption.

BRANCH A — KILN PATH: Armor Regeneration

L2a — GALVANIZED (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Stat bonus: constitution +2
  Effect: Engine.wp_kiln_regen_bonus += 2. Each time your armor drops to 0,
    it begins regenerating at 2 points per 5 turns (instead of only
    refilling at floor start). This regen is active until armor is full,
    then pauses. Armor regen does NOT stack with itself.
  Desc: "The dents fill back in. Not fast, but they fill."
  Balance: Meaningful sustain in long fights or multi-room sequences.
    At +3 armor from L1 + base armor (~5): up to 8 armor cycling via regen.

L3a — KILN (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Armor regen rate (from Galvanized) doubled to 4/5t. Additionally:
    while armor is regenerating (above 0 but below max), gain +1 DR.
    Desc: "Firing at temperature."
  Balance: Doubles regen efficiency. The +1 DR while regenerating creates
    a "sweet spot" where partial armor is better than full armor.

BRANCH B — TIN CAN PATH: Armor Stacking

L2b — CORRODED HULL (passive)
  Perk type: stat + passive
  Stat bonus: swagger +2
  Effect: Your base armor capacity increases by an additional +5 (total +8
    from this tree so far). Additionally: enemies that reduce your armor
    below half its maximum take 1 reflected physical damage per hit
    (thorns-style; triggers off armor consumption, not HP damage).
  Desc: "Takes a lot of rounds to dent a tin can. They always find out."
  Balance: +5 armor is substantial. Thorns 1 is minor but chips over time.

L3b — TIN CAN (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Each floor, the first 3 hits you absorb deal 0 damage to your
    HP (armor absorbs it regardless of whether armor is full or depleted —
    essentially 3 bonus "armor buffer" hits per floor). After 3 hits are
    absorbed this way, normal damage rules resume. Counter resets on
    new floor. Engine: engine.wp_tincan_hits_remaining (per-floor int).
  Desc: "Three free. After that, everybody pays."
  Balance: Very strong on low floors (enemies deal 4-8 per hit; 3 free
    absorbs = up to 24 free damage). Weaker vs. high-damage single hits.

CONVERGENCE:

L4 — SCRAP HEAP (stat + passive) [shared by all paths]
  Perk type: stat + passive
  Stat bonus: constitution +2, tolerance +2
  Effect: TOX INVERSION now also grants +1 flat DR per 25 current tox
    (max +4 DR at 100 tox), in addition to the percentage reduction.
    This stacks with Lead Lining and other DR sources.
    E.g., at 100 tox: +4 DR + ~25% incoming reduction from inversion.
  Desc: "You're not just resistant. You're infrastructure."
  Balance: Convergence perk makes both paths equally powerful heading
    into the capstone split. The flat DR scaling is the payoff for
    maintaining tox through the midgame.

FINAL SPLIT:

L5a — IRON COFFIN (passive) [CAPSTONE]
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Your armor now has a FLOOR: it can never drop below
    swagger_defence value (e.g., at SWG 14 = 3 armor floor). Enemy
    attacks that would deplete armor below this floor are absorbed by
    the floor value. Additionally: while armor is at its floor value
    (depleted to minimum), you gain +25% dodge chance.
    Passive: +3 SWG, +2 CON.
  Desc: "There's always metal between you and them. Always."
  Balance: Dodge at low armor is a powerful safety net. Combined with
    Galvanized regen or Tin Can's buffer, armor management becomes a
    strategic resource. Outgoing damage heavily penalized by high tox.

L5b — RIOT WALL (activated) [CAPSTONE]
  Perk type: activated (2/floor)
  Effect: Passive: +4 SWG, +2 CON.
    Activated: 2/floor. RIOT SHIELD UP: for 3 turns, the NEXT hit
    you take is COMPLETELY negated (absorbed; 0 damage to Temp HP,
    armor, or HP). After the negation fires, you gain Temp HP equal
    to the damage that would have been dealt (min 5, max 40). The
    shield does not block multiple hits — after one negation the
    cooldown starts. Remaining charges are cross-floor.
  New ability: riot_shield_up (PER_FLOOR, 2 charges)
  Desc: "You don't dodge it. You stand there and let it not hurt you."
  Balance: 2/floor means 2 guaranteed "free" hits per floor, each
    potentially worth 10-25+ Temp HP (at high-floor enemy damage).
    Very strong. Outgoing -25% from high tox still applies.


==========================================================================
VARIATION 3: "THE FORTRESS" — Triple Fork Swagger
==========================================================================
Shape: Triple Fork (L1-L3 trunk; split L4 into three capstones)
Fantasy: Long trunk stacks swagger to absurd levels, then forks into
  three very different ways to USE that swagger. Tox inversion in trunk.

TREE DIAGRAM:

    L1: Chalk White
          |
    L2: Thick Skin
          |
    L3: Bunker Mentality
       /    |    \
      /     |     \
  L4a:    L4b:   L4c:
  Ivory   Scab   Riot
  Tower   House  Gear
 (CAPS)  (CAPS) (CAPS)

TRUNK:

L1 — CHALK WHITE (stat + passive)
  Perk type: stat + passive
  Stat bonus: swagger +4, constitution +1
  Passive: TOX INVERSION unlocked. Also: +15% tox resistance.
  Desc: "White as chalk. Hard as chalk. You eat hits like chalk."

L2 — THICK SKIN (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: +4 SWG, +3 DR. Each time you take a hit that reduces your
    HP (not just armor/Temp HP), gain a Crust stack (engine.wp_crust_stacks).
    Each Crust stack: +0.5 SWG (fractional; rounds down) for 10 turns,
    max 6 stacks = +3 effective SWG. Stacks reset on new floor start.
  Desc: "Every punch just adds another layer."
  Balance: Encourages absorbing hits rather than dodging them. Synergizes
    with the inversion mechanic: take hits, become harder to hit via SWG.

L3 — BUNKER MENTALITY (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Stat bonus: constitution +2, tolerance +2
  Effect: When you are STATIONARY (do not move for 2+ consecutive turns),
    gain Fortress Stacks (engine.wp_fortress_stacks, +1/stationary turn,
    max 5). Each Fortress Stack = +1 DR and +0.5 SWG.
    Moving clears all Fortress Stacks immediately.
    At 5 stacks: +5 DR, +2.5 SWG (rounds to +2) — combined with inversion
    at 100 tox: total damage reduction is substantial.
  Desc: "Plant your feet. Become the wall."
  Balance: Rewards corner-camping playstyle. Enemies come to you.
    Strong in narrow corridors; weaker in open-room fights.
    +5 DR stacked at max means most small hits are near-negated.

FORK CAPSTONES:

L4a — IVORY TOWER (passive) [CAPSTONE]
  Perk type: passive
  Stat bonus: swagger +6, constitution +2
  Effect: Fortress Stacks now persist for 3 turns after you move (grace
    period). Also: while at max Fortress Stacks (5), your tox inversion
    formula is doubled in strength (same as Critical Mass in V1).
    Additionally: the outgoing damage penalty from tox inversion is HALVED
    while at 4+ Fortress Stacks — you fight back harder while entrenched.
  Desc: "From up here, they all look small. Most things bounce off."
  Balance: Best of both worlds for stationary builds. Outgoing damage
    penalty reduction while fortified makes this the most balanced
    damage/defense capstone. Requires active position management.

L4b — SCAB HOUSE (passive) [CAPSTONE]
  Perk type: passive
  Stat bonus: constitution +4, tolerance +2
  Effect: Every hit you receive (to any layer: Temp HP, armor, or HP)
    generates a Scab Charge (engine.wp_scab_charged). Unlike V1's scab,
    this version accumulates: max 5 charges at once (cross-floor).
    Each charge: next melee attack deals +3 damage (flat, not %).
    At 5 charges (15 bonus damage): still low-output due to tox penalty,
    but it fires automatically without managing a window.
    Also: Crust stacks now cap at 12 (not 6), doubling their SWG bonus.
  Desc: "Every hit you eat is a hit stored. They're paying interest."
  Balance: High tox = penalty on outgoing, but Scab charges partially
    compensate. Net effect: you tank tons of damage and deal modest but
    reliable counterattack bonus. Very slow output but great survivability.

L4c — RIOT GEAR (activated) [CAPSTONE]
  Perk type: activated (1/floor)
  Stat bonus: swagger +4
  Effect: Passive: Fortress Stacks now also add +2 Temp HP each
    (5 stacks = +10 Temp HP per stationary turn, up to 50 Temp HP
    per entrenched period).
  Activated: 1/floor. FULL LOCKDOWN: 5-turn window.
    During Lockdown: all hits reduced by 50% (additive with inversion and
    DR; total reduction can exceed 80%). At end of Lockdown, release:
    deal 1 damage to all enemies in r=3 per Fortress Stack consumed
    (max 5 * 1 = 5 damage per enemy, AOE; not a huge burst but
    area-of-effect pressure). All Fortress Stacks are consumed on release.
  New ability: full_lockdown (PER_FLOOR, 1 charge)
  Desc: "They come. They bounce. You exhale. They bleed."
  Balance: 50% reduction for 5 turns with AOE exhale is strong burst
    defense. Low damage output maintained (5 damage AOE is modest).

VARIATION 4: "THE PILGRIM" — W-shape Swagger-to-HP
==========================================================================
Shape: W-shape (L1/L2 shared; split L3; converge L5; split L6)
Fantasy: Long Swagger accumulation trunk, tox inversion midway, branches
  explore two flavors of scaling, then converge on a final testament to
  endurance before one last tradeoff at the capstone.

TREE DIAGRAM:

    L1: White Habit
          |
    L2: Plaster
         / \
        /   \
  [A] L3a     L3b [B]
  Grout       Cement
     |           |
  [A] L4a     L4b [B]
  Wall Paper  Foundation
        \     /
    L5: Monolith (converge)
         / \
        /   \
  L6a: Tombstone   L6b: Mausoleum
     (CAPSTONE)       (CAPSTONE)

TRUNK:

L1 — WHITE HABIT (stat + passive)
  Perk type: stat + passive
  Stat bonus: swagger +4, tolerance +1
  Effect: +20% tox resistance. You begin accumulating White Power XP.
  Desc: "A habit, not a hobby. Worn like armor."

L2 — PLASTER (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Stat bonus: swagger +2
  Effect: TOX INVERSION unlocked. Additionally: whenever you would gain
    toxicity (from any source), gain Temp HP equal to 10% of the tox
    gained (round down, min 1). E.g., absorbing 20 tox = 2 Temp HP.
    This fires BEFORE the tox is applied, so even resisted tox generates
    Temp HP if at least 1 tox passes through.
  Desc: "The poison fills the cracks. That's how walls work."
  Balance: Steady Temp HP gain in the Meth Lab zone. Low value per
    tick but adds up over a floor. Rewards actively absorbing tox.

BRANCH A — GROUT PATH: Swagger stacking

L3a — GROUT (stat + passive)
  Perk type: stat + passive
  Stat bonus: swagger +4
  Effect: Your swagger defense formula improves: every 2 SWG above 8
    now grants +1.5 defense (rounds down), instead of +1 defense.
    At SWG 20: normally +6 def; Grout gives +9 def.
  Desc: "Fill the gaps. Every gap. Fill them all."
  Balance: Significant amplifier for high-SWG builds. Combined with
    tree's many +SWG perks, defense becomes substantial. Still no
    outgoing damage improvement.

L4a — WALL PAPER (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Once per floor, when an attack would reduce you below 25% HP,
    trigger PAPER WALL: that hit deals only 1 damage (minimum, not 0).
    After Paper Wall fires, gain +3 DR for 5 turns. Resets on new floor.
    Paper Wall does NOT fire again on the same floor after triggering.
  Engine: engine.wp_paper_wall_used (per-floor bool)
  Desc: "It looks like nothing. It holds like nothing. Then it saves you."
  Balance: One guaranteed near-death save per floor. Strong permadeath
    insurance. The +3 DR afterwards helps survive the aftermath.

BRANCH B — CEMENT PATH: HP pool stacking

L3b — CEMENT (stat + passive)
  Perk type: stat + passive
  Stat bonus: constitution +3
  Effect: Your maximum HP bonus from CON increases: each CON point above
    base (8) now grants +12 HP instead of +10. Retroactive on existing CON.
    At CON 10: normally 130 HP; Cement gives 130 + 4 bonus = 134 HP.
    At CON 12: normally 150 HP; Cement gives 150 + 8 bonus = 158 HP.
  Desc: "You're not wide. You're deep. There's more of you to hit through."
  Balance: +2 HP per CON above base. Modest but scales with future CON
    investment. Combined with +3 CON from this perk: net ~+30+ HP.

L4b — FOUNDATION (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Stat bonus: constitution +2
  Effect: At the start of each floor, if your current HP is at maximum,
    gain Temp HP equal to 15% of your max HP (round up). These bonus
    Temp HP persist until consumed. Also: the first hit you take on
    any floor deals 0 damage to your actual HP (only burns Temp HP
    and armor); if neither exists, that first hit is negated entirely.
  Desc: "Start clean. End standing."
  Balance: Rewards entering each floor at full HP (managed play). The
    free-first-hit and Temp HP incentivize careful floor exits.

CONVERGENCE:

L5 — MONOLITH (stat + passive) [shared by all paths]
  Perk type: stat + passive
  Stat bonus: swagger +4, constitution +3, tolerance +2
  Effect: TOX INVERSION strengthened: the outgoing damage penalty is
    removed entirely. At any tox level, your outgoing damage is NORMAL.
    Only the defensive benefit of inversion remains (tox still reduces
    incoming damage). You hit just as hard as before; you just take much
    less damage at high tox.
  Desc: "A monolith doesn't negotiate. It stands. You stand."
  Balance: This is the major payoff of the W-shape's long path.
    Removing the outgoing penalty is enormous — the tank now hits at
    full strength while being reduced-damage-incoming. This is the
    strongest single perk in this tree; gated behind 5 perks.
  IMPLEMENTATION NOTE: Set player_stats.wp_no_outgoing_penalty = True.
    In _compute_player_attack_power(), skip the tox inversion
    outgoing multiplier when this flag is True.

FINAL SPLIT:

L6a — TOMBSTONE (passive) [CAPSTONE]
  Perk type: passive
  Stat bonus: constitution +4, swagger +2
  Effect: Death saves (if implemented via existing death_save mechanics)
    fire TWICE before true death (once per floor, same as base). While
    below 30% HP, the inversion formula is doubled in strength (incoming
    only; since Monolith removed outgoing penalty, the doubling only
    applies defensively). At 30% HP + 100 tox: ~50% incoming reduction.
    You become truly unkillable at low health.
  Desc: "They chiseled your name in. They were a little early."
  Balance: Encourages playing at low HP. Combined with Monolith's
    no-outgoing-penalty, this is a genuinely terrifying low-HP tank:
    full damage output + massive incoming reduction + death save.

L6b — MAUSOLEUM (passive + activated) [CAPSTONE]

== NEW PLAYERSTATS FIELDS (cumulative across all 10 variations) ==

  tox_inversion_active: bool = False
    Set True at L1 of any tree. When True, replaces normal tox damage
    multiplier with inversion formula in combat.py.

  wp_no_outgoing_penalty: bool = False
    Set True by Monolith (V4) and Grinding Stone (V10). Skips outgoing

== TOX INVERSION FORMULA (REFERENCE IMPLEMENTATION) ==

  In combat.py, in the damage resolution block:
    if engine.player_stats.tox_inversion_active and entity == engine.player:
        coeff = engine.player_stats.wp_inversion_coefficient  # default 0.5
        tox_ratio = min(entity.toxicity / 100.0, 2.0)         # cap at 200 tox
        reduction = coeff * (tox_ratio ** 0.6)
        tox_mult = max(0.10, 1.0 - reduction)                  # floor at 10% (never 0)
    else:
        tox_mult = 1.0 + (entity.toxicity / 100) ** 0.6       # normal formula

  In _compute_player_attack_power():
    if engine.player_stats.tox_inversion_active and not engine.player_stats.wp_no_outgoing_penalty:
        coeff = engine.player_stats.wp_inversion_outgoing_coefficient  # default 0.5
        tox_ratio = min(engine.player.toxicity / 100.0, 2.0)
        outgoing_mult = max(0.40, 1.0 - coeff * (tox_ratio ** 0.6))  # floor 40%
        atk_power = int(atk_power * outgoing_mult)

== BALANCE BENCHMARKS ==

  Tox inversion at 0.5 coefficient vs. normal tox formula:
    0 tox:   1.0x both formulas — no difference
    25 tox:  normal 1.16x; inverted 0.92x — delta: 0.24x at low tox
    50 tox:  normal 1.26x; inverted 0.87x — delta: 0.39x at mid tox
    100 tox: normal 1.50x; inverted 0.75x — delta: 0.75x at standard tox
    200 tox: normal 2.00x; inverted 0.63x — delta: 1.37x at max tox

  At 0.75 coefficient (V9 The Word capstone, strongest):
    100 tox: inverted 0.25x incoming — taking 25% of normal damage
    200 tox: inverted ~0.11x incoming — taking ~11% of normal damage (floor applied)

  At 0.5 coefficient (standard), combined with +8 DR and 10 Temp HP/floor:
    Floor 1-2 enemies deal ~4-8 damage. After -8 DR: many hits deal 0 HP damage.
    White Power tank becomes near-unkillable at floor 1-2 at moderate tox.
    This is intentional — the tradeoff is dealt via outgoing penalty.

  Outgoing penalty at standard 0.5 coefficient:
    100 tox: deal 75% of normal damage
    200 tox: deal ~63% of normal damage
    Fights take longer to resolve but player does not die.
    Synergy with Grinding Stone capstone (V10) removes this penalty.

== CROSS-TREE SYNERGIES ==

  Chemical Warfare (CW): Primary tox-accumulation partner. CW provides
    the tox needed to maximize inversion; WP converts that tox to defense.
    Together: maximum tox build (80-100+ tox maintained) with strong
    incoming reduction and CW's offensive capabilities.

  Meth-Head: Speed + tox. WP slows fight pacing (lower damage output);
    Meth-Head's speed bonus compensates by landing more hits per time unit.

  Mutation: Rad/tox dual-resistance. Tox resistance perks from Mutation
    feed into V5's Pure Immunity (tox resistance → inversion coefficient bonus).

  L Farming / Swagger items: Any external SWG bonus amplifies WP's
    Swagger defense formula. External SWG from items stacks directly
    with tree's SWG bonuses. +Swagger ring = more armor floor in V2 Iron Coffin.

  Drinking / Alco-Seltzer: The purge-50-tox item interacts with inversion:
    V1 Branch B (Meltdown) benefits from periodic tox purges. V6 Tallow
    benefits from any tox loss. Careful: excessive purging can lower inversion.

  Stabbing: With Grinding Stone (V10) removing outgoing penalty,
    a stabbing build with high tox can maintain near-full attack power
    while being damage-reduced incoming. Strong hybrid potential.
