CHEMICAL WARFARE — 10 Skill Tree Variations (L1-L4)
=====================================================
Design doc for the NIGRL roguelike (Meth Lab zone skill).
All variations replace the existing placeholder L4 perk and may optionally
revise L1-L3 to serve the variation's identity. Each stands as a complete,
self-contained tree proposal.

Toxicity system reference:
  - Player tox multiplier:  1 + (tox/200)^0.6    (200 tox = 2x damage taken)
  - Monster tox multiplier: 1 + (tox/50)^0.6     (50 tox = 2x, monsters more sensitive)
  - tox_resistance: int on PlayerStats (also temporary_tox_resistance)
  - Hazard types: toxic_creep (~, green-yellow), acid_pool (~, bright green),
    venom_pool (~, mid-green)
  - ChargeType options: INFINITE+cooldown, PER_FLOOR, TOTAL, ONCE, FLOOR_ONLY
  - Stat names: constitution, strength, street_smarts, book_smarts, tolerance, swagger

XP source for Chemical Warfare: dealing toxicity to enemies, kills on
high-tox enemies, using tox abilities.


=======================================================================
VARIATION 1 — "Toxic Tank"
Fantasy: Soak as much poison as possible. High personal tox becomes a
weapon: enemies who touch you get burned, your damage scales with your
own suffering. Walk into rooms already halfway dead and still win.
=======================================================================

L1 — TAINTED BLOOD (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Melee attacks apply 8 toxicity to enemies. If your own toxicity
          is >= 50, the amount doubles to 16.
  Desc: "Your fists are basically biohazard waste. Every punch leaks."

L2 — TOXIC CONSTITUTION (+4 TOL, +3 CON)
  Perk type: stat
  Effect: { "tolerance": 4, "constitution": 3, "tox_resistance": -20 }
  Desc: "+4 Tolerance, +3 Constitution, -20% Toxicity Resistance. Your
        body stops fighting the poison and starts using it."

L3 — CONTACT HIGH (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Enemies adjacent to you at the start of your turn gain 3
          toxicity. Scales with your own tox: +1 extra per 25 personal
          toxicity (max +4 extra at 100 tox). Stacks with Tainted Blood.
  Desc: "You're a walking contamination zone. Get too close and you'll
        regret it."

L4 — LAST RESORT (activated)
  Perk type: activated
  Effect: { "ability": "last_resort" }
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (2/floor)
  Target type: SELF
  Desc: "2/floor. Instantly transfer 40 toxicity from yourself to every
        enemy in a 3-tile radius (split evenly, minimum 5 each). You lose
        the tox; they gain it. If you had 100+ tox before the transfer,
        also stun all affected enemies for 2 turns. At low personal HP, a
        warning: you still take the multiplier damage while tox is
        draining."
  Balance note: 40 tox to 4 enemies = 10 each (minor). To 2 enemies = 20
        each. Real payoff is the stun threshold and the resource dump that
        buys breathing room. 2/floor keeps it from being a permanent tox
        reset button.

VARIATION 2 — "Plague Spreader"
Fantasy: Infect one enemy, let the room kill itself. Focus on chaining
toxicity between enemies — the more tightly packed a room, the deadlier
your opening move. Minimal personal tox risk.
=======================================================================

L1 — PATIENT ZERO (activated)
  Perk type: activated
  Effect: { "ability": "patient_zero" }
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (3/floor)
  Target type: SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS
  Desc: "3/floor. Infect a visible enemy with Patient Zero (15 turns):
        each turn they gain 5 tox AND spread 2 tox to every adjacent
        enemy. Stacks with existing toxicity. The first kill under this
        effect triggers a 5-tox burst in a 2-tile radius."

L2 — VECTOR (+2 TOL, +2 STS)
  Perk type: stat
  Effect: { "tolerance": 2, "street_smarts": 2 }
  Desc: "+2 Tolerance, +2 Street-Smarts. You've learned to read the
        spread: who to hit first, who'll carry it furthest."

L3 — OUTBREAK (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: When an enemy dies with 30+ toxicity, they release a toxic
          burst: 3-tile radius, 10 tox to all enemies caught in it.
          Enemies that die from this burst also trigger Outbreak.
          Chain reaction possible. Does not affect the player.
  Desc: "Death is just another vector. One corpse can clear a whole room
        if you play it right."

L4 — EPIDEMIC (activated)
  Perk type: activated
  Effect: { "ability": "epidemic" }
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (1/floor)
  Target type: SELF (global effect, no target needed)
  Desc: "1/floor. For 20 turns: any time an enemy takes tox damage,
        there is a 40% chance a random OTHER visible enemy also gains 5
        tox (no line-of-sight required, just visible). Combined with
        Outbreak and Patient Zero, this can chain-poison an entire floor
        in seconds. Grants +25 Chemical Warfare XP per chain trigger."
  Implementation note: track triggering in the add_toxicity() call site
        in combat.py when engine has engine.epidemic_active flag set.

  Desc: "First contact is always the worst. You've learned to make it

L2 — FLUSH (+3 STS)
  Perk type: stat
  Effect: { "street_smarts": 3, "tox_resistance": 20 }
  Desc: "+3 Street-Smarts, +20% Toxicity Resistance. You know exactly

  Perk type: activated
  Effect: { "ability": "chemical_mugging" }
  Charge type: INFINITE (40t cooldown)
  Target type: ADJACENT
  Desc: "Slam a chemical cocktail into an adjacent enemy: deals STR

L4 — GHOST DOSE (activated)
  Perk type: activated
  Effect: { "ability": "ghost_dose" }
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (3/floor)
  Target type: SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS
  Desc: "3/floor. Mark a visible enemy with Ghost Dose. For 10 turns:

  Balance: 10 turns * up to 5 triggers/turn = 50 tox max from mark
        alone (= 5 HP recovered). The healing is modest unless combined
        with Outbreak or Epidemic from other sources. The real value is
        the passive tox amplification on a priority target.

VARIATION 5 — "Volatile Body"
Fantasy: Ride the edge of the toxicity multiplier. The closer you are to
death from your OWN tox, the more powerful your abilities. A high-risk
glass cannon that needs careful resource management. Pairs with White
Power L4 (Whitewash) for a tox-dump safety net.
=======================================================================

L1 — CHEMICAL RAGE (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: For every 20 toxicity you carry, gain +1 melee damage (no
          cap). At 100 tox: +5 melee damage. At 200 tox: +10 melee
          damage.
  Desc: "Pain makes you angry. Poison makes you angrier. Simple math."

L2 — VOLATILE (+2 CON, +2 STR)
  Perk type: stat
  Effect: { "constitution": 2, "strength": 2, "tox_resistance": -20 }
  Desc: "+2 Constitution, +2 Strength, -20% Toxicity Resistance. You've
        made yourself more dangerous by making yourself more fragile."

L3 — DETONATOR (activated)
  Perk type: activated
  Effect: { "ability": "detonator" }
  Charge type: INFINITE (60t cooldown)
  Target type: SELF
  Desc: "Instantly vent your toxicity as an explosive chemical burst.
        All enemies in a 3-tile radius take damage equal to your current
        toxicity / 5 (e.g. 80 tox = 16 damage) and gain 15 toxicity
        each. Your personal tox drops to 0 after the blast. 60t
        cooldown. Has no effect at 0 tox. Best used at 100+ for maximum
        AOE damage."

L4 — PRESSURE COOKER (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: While your toxicity is 75 or higher: gain +3 to all stats
          temporarily. The bonus disappears if you drop below 75 tox.
          Additionally, the Detonator cooldown is reduced by 20 turns
          (to 40t) and the AOE radius increases to 4 tiles.
  Desc: "+3 all stats at 75+ tox. Detonator: 40t cooldown, 4-tile
        radius. You're a chemical weapon with legs. The question is
        whether you explode before the enemies do."
  Implementation note: the +3 all-stats bonus is a temporary_stat_bonus
        (dict) applied/removed by a passive check in the engine tick,
        similar to how Swashbuckling stacks work. Check player.toxicity
        each turn and apply/expire accordingly.

VARIATION 6 — "Junkie Economist"
Fantasy: Toxicity as currency. Spend it for power, earn it back via
kills and consumables. The loop is about maintaining a healthy tox
'bankroll' — not too high (death), not too low (no fuel). Unique
economic rhythm distinct from all other skill trees.

  Effect: Consuming any item (food, drink, strain, joint) grants +5

          Warfare XP. This creates a flywheel: consume to earn tox,
          spend tox for power, kill high-tox enemies for XP.
  Desc: "Every hit, every sip, every bite adds up. The body keeps a
        ledger."

L2 — LIQUID ASSETS (+3 TOL, +2 CON)
  Perk type: stat
  Effect: { "tolerance": 3, "constitution": 2 }
  Desc: "+3 Tolerance, +2 Constitution. More buffer means more float.
        You can carry more before the bill comes due."

L3 — MARKET CORRECTION (activated)
  Perk type: activated
  Effect: { "ability": "market_correction" }
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (2/floor)
  Target type: SELF
  Desc: "2/floor. Spend 30 toxicity to gain a 'dividend': heal HP equal
        to half the spent tox (15 HP) and gain +30 speed for 15 turns.
        If you're below 30 tox, does nothing. Think of it as cashing out
        before the crash."

L4 — PUMP AND DUMP (activated)
  Perk type: activated
  Effect: { "ability": "pump_and_dump" }
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (1/floor)
  Target type: SELF
  Desc: "1/floor. Spike: for the next 5 turns, your toxicity is treated

  Balance: Max tox 200 = 20 crash damage and double effectiveness for

        keeps it from being a rotation button.

VARIATION 8 — "Junk Science"

L2 — FIELD LAB (+3 BKS, +2 TOL)
  Perk type: stat
  Effect: { "book_smarts": 3, "tolerance": 2 }
  Desc: "+3 Book-Smarts, +2 Tolerance. You've self-taught enough organic
        chemistry to be genuinely dangerous. And unpredictable."

L3 — SYNTHESIZE (activated)
  Perk type: activated
  Effect: { "ability": "synthesize" }
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (2/floor)
  Target type: SELF
  Desc: "2/floor. Spend 25 toxicity to synthesize a random consumable
        item that appears in your inventory: 40% chance of a healing
        item, 30% chance of a tox-based strain (e.g. Nigle Fart, Dank

  Perk type: passive
  Effect: Synthesize now has 3 uses per floor (up from 2). The unique

  Desc: "Your synthesis is getting more refined. The margin between
        medicine and poison is thinner than anyone admits."
  Implementation note: synthesized items need a flag (e.g. entity
        attribute is_synthesized=True) for the XP tracking hook in
        combat.py on_entity_death().

VARIATION 10 — "Condemned"
Fantasy: Embrace mutual destruction. You and your enemies share the
pain. Every point of tox you gain also hurts them; your own toxicity
becomes a shared curse. The ultimate high-tox build — designed to push
toxicity as high as possible on BOTH sides simultaneously, turning the
damage multiplier into a weapon for the enemy AND against them at once.
Requires careful HP management and pairing with survival tools.
=======================================================================

L1 — SHARED SUFFERING (passive)
  Perk type: passive
  Effect: When YOU gain toxicity (from any source), all enemies in a
          3-tile radius also gain 25% of the amount you gained (rounded
          down, minimum 1). E.g.: if you gain 20 tox, nearby enemies
          gain 5 each. Does not trigger from the reflected amount
          (no infinite loops).
  Desc: "If you're going down, you're taking the room with you."

L2 — CONDEMNED (+4 TOL, -20% tox resistance)
  Perk type: stat
  Effect: { "tolerance": 4, "tox_resistance": -20 }
  Desc: "+4 Tolerance, -20% Toxicity Resistance. The resistance nerf
        is the point — more tox flowing in means more flowing out to
        enemies via Shared Suffering."

L3 — MARTYRDOM (activated)
  Perk type: activated
  Effect: { "ability": "martyrdom" }
  Charge type: INFINITE (70t cooldown)
  Target type: SELF
  Desc: "70t cooldown. Voluntarily injure yourself: lose 15% of max HP
        (no death — minimum 1 HP remaining) and instantly gain 50
        toxicity. The Shared Suffering passive immediately fires from
        this gain, spreading 12 tox to all nearby enemies. If you are
        already at 100+ tox when you use this, gain a 3-turn 'Condemned'
        buff: +25% damage dealt, immune to stuns. Risky, but there's
        a reason it's called martyrdom."

L4 — PLAGUE FLAG (activated)
  Perk type: activated
  Effect: { "ability": "plague_flag" }
  Charge type: PER_FLOOR (1/floor)
  Target type: SELF
  Desc: "1/floor. Plant the flag. For 15 turns: your Shared Suffering
        passive fires at 75% transfer rate (up from 25%). Every point
        of tox you accumulate hammers everyone around you. Your own
        toxicity multiplier is ALSO applied to your outgoing melee
        damage as a damage bonus (not just incoming damage). At 150
        personal tox: you deal ~1.54x damage (the full player-tox
        formula applied to OUTPUT too). The ultimate condemned build
        turns your greatest weakness into a reason to walk into every
        room at 200 tox and a smile."
  Balance note: The outgoing melee tox bonus uses the player-tox
        multiplier formula (1+(tox/200)^0.6), same function already
        in combat.py. At 200 tox this is 2x outgoing + 2x incoming —
        a true glass cannon mode. 1/floor, 15 turns only. Flag
        ends early if player reaches 0 HP (which can happen fast).

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES (all variations)
=======================================================================

Ability IDs referenced (all new, need ABILITY_REGISTRY entries):
  last_resort, patient_zero, epidemic, corrosive_spit, acid_volley,
  dissolution, needle_hands_proc, chemical_mugging, ghost_dose,
  market_correction, pump_and_dump, contaminate, toxic_minefield,
  synthesize, corrosive_slam, martyrdom, plague_flag

New engine state flags needed per variation (prefix "chem_"):
  chem_epidemic_active (bool, 20-turn countdown) — V2
  chem_pump_and_dump_active (bool) — V6
  chem_plague_flag_active (bool) — V10
  chem_acid_knuckles_streak (dict: enemy_id -> int) — V9
  chem_ghost_dose_targets (set of entity ids) — V4
  chem_contaminate_tiles (list of (x,y)) — V7
  chem_needle_hands_hit_set (set of entity ids, reset/floor) — V4
  synthesized_item_count (int, for XP hook) — V8

Passive checks that need hooks in engine._run_energy_loop() or
combat.py:
  - Shared Suffering: hook in combat.add_toxicity() when entity is
    player. Check if engine.player has skill "Chemical Warfare" >= L1
    for whichever variation is active.
  - Contact High: hook in engine._run_energy_loop() per-tick, after
    player energy gain, before monster turns.
  - Chemical Rage: recalculate in combat.py player power calculation,
    reading player.toxicity.
  - Volatile Body L4 (+3 stats at 75+ tox): checked per tick;
    apply/remove temporary_stat_bonuses dict.

Dead Zone (V7 L3) damage modifier:
  10% increased damage from all sources while on player-created tox
  hazard. Implement as a check in combat.resolve_attack() looking at
  the tile under the defender for a hazard with created_by_player=True.

Synthesize (V8 L3) item generation:
  Use existing ITEM_DEFS + random selection from a filtered list.
  Tag the produced Entity with is_synthesized=True for XP tracking.

Acid Knuckles (V9) consecutive counter:
  Reset on: different target attacked, player stunned, floor change.
  Store as engine.chem_acid_knuckles_streak dict {entity_id: int}.

BALANCE SUMMARY TABLE
=======================================================================

Variation | Identity         | Tox Dir | Stat Focus        | Risk Level
----------|------------------|---------|-------------------|----------
1 Tank    | Sponge + burn    | High    | TOL+CON           | Medium
2 Spreader| Chain infection  | Low     | TOL+STS           | Low
3 AcidMage| Terrain caster   | Medium  | BKS               | Low-Med
4 Assassin| Burst+strip tox  | Zero    | STS               | Low
5 Volatile| Tox=power+risk   | High    | CON+STR           | Very High
6 Economist| Tox as currency | Medium  | TOL+CON           | Medium
7 Zone Ctrl| Terrain denial  | Low     | TOL+STS           | Low-Med
8 Junk Sci | Crafting hybrid | Medium  | BKS+TOL           | Low
9 Brawler | Melee amplifier  | Medium  | STR+CON           | Medium
10 Condemned| Shared curse   | Very Hi | TOL               | Extreme

Recommended for implementation first: V1 (Tank) — most direct
extension of existing tree identity. V9 (Brawler) — fewest new systems
needed. V2 (Spreader) — Outbreak is the most mechanically elegant
single passive in the set.

Least implementable standalone: V8 (Junk Science) needs the most
hooks. V10 (Condemned) has the highest balance testing burden.
