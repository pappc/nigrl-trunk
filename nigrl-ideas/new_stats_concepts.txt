NIGRL — New Stat Concepts
=========================
Brainstormed in two batches. Mix of creative/thematic and traditional ARPG-style stats.
All stats use range [5, 12] like existing stats unless noted.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
BATCH 1 — CREATIVE / THEMATIC STATS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

HEAT
-----
How much attention you are drawing. High Heat = police/rival ambushes more likely,
enemies call for backup more aggressively, vendors charge more. Resets on floor change,
but major crimes spike it.

CLOUT
------
Social capital. Passive intimidation aura: enemies below certain morale thresholds might
flee. Affects dialogue/event outcomes if those get added. Opposed to Heat — high Clout
with low Heat = you are feared but not hunted.

CONNECTIONS
------------
Who you know. Determines what drops vendors carry, what rumors appear in the log,
unlock conditions for certain items. A passive "access" stat.

PARANOIA
---------
Accumulates from being stalked, hit from behind, staying in the dark. High Paranoia =
hallucinated enemies on the map (waste your attacks), shorter perceived FOV. Calmed by
food, sleep, certain consumables.

COMPOSURE
----------
Resistance to fear effects, stun duration reduction, chance to act during "skip turn"
debuffs. Decays when you flee; grows when you stand your ground.

PAIN THRESHOLD
---------------
Separate from defense. Reduces crit multiplier and bleed/DOT damage specifically.
High Pain Threshold lets you stay functional at low HP (no "staggered" penalties).
Scales with Constitution but is leveled separately.

HUNGER
-------
A stat that passively degrades. Not just a counter — when full, small bonuses to STR
and CON. When starving, penalties accumulate. Munching XP gains are already tied to
eating; making this a visible stat creates more decisions around food.

RHYTHM
-------
Volatile combat stat, 0-100. Builds with consecutive hits/kills, decays when you miss,
take damage, or wait. At high Rhythm: bonus crit chance, reduced ability cooldowns,
extra energy. At 0: small penalty. Creates a "keep moving, keep attacking" pressure.

REACH
------
(First appearance — creative framing)
Determines melee range (default 1 tile). Some weapons/perks extend it to 2 tiles.
Makes positioning and corridors more tactical.

NOTORIETY
----------
Persistent across floors (unlike Heat). Enemies that escape a fight can "spread word."
High Notoriety = harder floor enemies but also better loot (bigger targets = bigger
rewards). Permanent until floor 5 reset.

BATCH 2 — TRADITIONAL ARPG STATS (NIGRL-FLAVORED)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

HUSTLE
-------
ARPG equivalent: Increased Attack Speed / Movement Speed
Energy gained per tick. Base player speed is 100. Every point of Hustle adds +5
energy/tick, letting you act more often than enemies.
Gap filled: no player speed stat exists (speed is only on enemy templates).

GRIT
-----
ARPG equivalent: Damage Mitigation / Armor %
Flat % damage reduction applied AFTER the (power - defense) formula. Every 2 points =
1% DR, capped ~10%. Layers multiplicatively with Swagger's flat defense.
Gap filled: only flat defense exists, no % mitigation layer.

FEROCITY
---------
ARPG equivalent: Critical Strike Damage
Crit damage multiplier: crit_multiplier = 1.5 + Ferocity * 0.1
At max (12) that's x2.7. Pairs with Street-Smarts — build one for frequency, one for
punch.
Gap filled: crits currently have no multiplier stat.

LUCK
-----
ARPG equivalent: Magic Find / Item Rarity
Affects: gold drop amounts (+/-Luck%), loot tier rolls (more chances at better
category), proc chances on strain/food special effects.
Gap filled: no loot quality modifier stat.

TENACITY
---------
ARPG equivalent: Crowd Control Resistance
Reduces duration of all debuffs applied to you on application. Every 2 Tenacity = 1
turn trimmed from incoming stun/fear/slow/burn duration.
Gap filled: status effects have zero player-side resistance.

PREDATOR
---------
ARPG equivalent: Life on Hit
Each melee hit restores (Predator / 3) HP (rounded down, min 0). High Constitution
keeps you alive through bulk; Predator keeps you alive through aggression.
Gap filled: no sustain-through-damage playstyle stat.

ARMOR (as base stat)
---------------------
ARPG equivalent: Shield HP / Armor Layer
entity.armor already exists on Entity but nothing drives it as a player stat. Armor
fills your armor bar, which absorbs damage before HP. Unlike Grit (% reduction), armor
is a consumable buffer that resets each floor or on rest.
Gap filled: no consumable buffer layer between full HP and damage.

REFLEXES
---------
ARPG equivalent: Evasion Rating
Directly feeds dodge_chance: dodge_chance = Reflexes * 2%
dodge_chance already exists as a flat int with no base stat driving it.
Gap filled: no base stat feeds dodge_chance.

REACH (ARPG framing)
---------------------
ARPG equivalent: Increased Area of Effect / Attack Range
Melee attack range in tiles. Base 1. At Reach 2, you attack from 2 tiles away. Affects
line-of-sight melee, weapon abilities, and AoE radius on close-range spells.
Gap filled: no melee range variation.

VENOM
------
ARPG equivalent: Poison On-Hit / Elemental Proc
On-hit poison application chance. Each melee attack has Venom% chance to apply a poison
stack (DOT). Higher Venom = higher proc chance AND higher stack cap. Mirrors the
existing on-hit effect system enemies already use — gives the player that same mechanic.
Gap filled: player has no proc-on-hit stat.

ARMOR SHRED
------------
ARPG equivalent: Armor Penetration / Defense Reduction
Reduces target flat defense on hit: armor_shred = floor(ArmorShred / 3) stripped from
enemy defense per hit (temporary, resets after combat). Counters high-defense enemies.
Gap filled: no way to bypass enemy flat defense.

RECOVERY
---------
ARPG equivalent: HP Regeneration
+1 HP/tick per 3 points of Recovery while out of combat (no enemy in FOV). In combat,
Recovery is halved. Encourages tactical retreating and room-clearing pace.
Gap filled: no passive healing stat.

INTIMIDATION
-------------
ARPG equivalent: Aura / Debuff Aura
Passive aura that reduces nearby enemies effective defense. Within (Intimidation / 2)
tiles, enemies take +1 damage from all sources. Scales conceptually with Swagger —
you swagger so hard enemies lose their nerve.
Gap filled: no passive enemy-weakening presence stat.

IMPULSE
--------
ARPG equivalent: Ambush / First Strike Damage
Attacks against enemies who have not acted yet this combat deal +Impulse flat damage.
Rewards ambushing, lining up shots, and using stuns to reset the bonus.
Gap filled: no opener / ambush damage bonus.

MOXIE
------
ARPG equivalent: Berserk / Low-HP Rage
Bonus damage when below 50% HP: bonus_damage = floor(Moxie * missing_hp_percent / 10)
The more you are hurting, the harder you hit. Pairs badly with Grit (safe play) but
rewards high-risk builds.
Gap filled: no low-HP scaling damage stat.

FOCUS
------
ARPG equivalent: Cooldown Reduction
Every 2 Focus = 1 fewer charge cost per floor on PER_FLOOR abilities. TOTAL charge
abilities get +1 max charge. Also reduces channel time on multi-turn items (eating,
crafting) by 1 turn per 4 Focus.
Gap filled: no ability charge/timing reduction stat.
