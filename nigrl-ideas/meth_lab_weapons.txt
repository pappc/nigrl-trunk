================================================================================
METH LAB ZONE — WEAPON DESIGNS
================================================================================

New mechanics referenced below (not yet implemented):
  expose      — enemy defense halved for N turns
  root        — enemy cannot move but can still attack
  silence     — enemy cannot use special attacks for N turns
  corrode     — permanently reduces enemy power by 1 per hit
  barbed      — enemy takes damage each time they move
  tether      — enemy cannot move more than 2 tiles from player
  magnetize   — metal enemies pulled 1 tile toward player; applies shocked
  doom        — 10-turn countdown; enemy dies instantly when it expires
  petrify     — complete immobilization + double damage taken for N turns
  mark        — target takes +3 damage from all sources per stack
  charm       — enemy fights for player, attacking nearest enemy
  warp        — teleports enemy to random open tile on the floor
  jinx        — enemy attacks guaranteed to miss player for N turns
  barbed      — enemy takes 2 damage per tile moved
  momentum    — damage builds per consecutive hit on same target
  vampiric    — heal % of damage dealt (already in Bone Club, reused here)
  reflect     — enemies take % of damage they deal to player back
  vengeance   — stores last N hits taken; releases on next swing

SECTION 1: GHETTO / STREET WEAPONS (Meth Lab Zone)

1. Rusty Stop Sign  [Blunt]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ripped from the ground outside the lab. The angular octagonal edge catches
limbs at wrong angles.
  Base DMG:   11
  STR req:     9
  Reach:       2
  Scaling:     STR tiered
  On-hit:      expose — enemy defense halved for 3 turns

2. Dog Chain  [Blunt]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ripped off a junkyard rottweiler. Heavy iron links, clasp still attached.
  Base DMG:    6
  STR req:     5
  Reach:       2
  Scaling:     STR ratio
  On-hit:      root — enemy cannot move for 3 turns (can still attack)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. Zip Gun  [Special]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Fires a single shot then becomes a weak club. Two weapons in one terrible
package.
  Base DMG:    2 (melee, post-shot)
  STR req:     2
  Reach:       1
  Scaling:     none
  Special:     First use fires one ranged shot for 18 damage (ignores defense),

================================================================================
SECTION 2: CLASSIC ROGUELIKE FANTASY WEAPONS
================================================================================
Note: no named fantasy races or franchise-specific terminology.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Enchanted Club  [Blunt]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A plain wooden club wrapped in something that hums. Swings with more intent
than wood should.
  Base DMG:    6
  STR req:     4
  Reach:       1
  Scaling:     BKSMT threshold (above 5) — enchantment amplifies intelligence
  On-kill:     random minor buff to player (absorbs residual energy)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. Cursed Flail  [Blunt]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Heavy iron ball on a rusted chain. The curse makes it unpredictable.
  Base DMG:   14
  STR req:     7
  Reach:       2
  Scaling:     STR tiered
  On-hit:      25% chance to deal damage to the player instead of the enemy

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. War Hammer  [Blunt]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
No magic. Just a very large hammer requiring a very large person.
  Base DMG:   14
  STR req:    13
  Reach:       1
  Scaling:     STR tiered (divisor 1)
  On-crit:     permanently destroys 2 enemy defense (sunder on crit only)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. Draining Staff  [Blunt]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A long carved rod that pulls something out of whatever it touches.
  Base DMG:    5
  STR req:     4
  Reach:       2
  Scaling:     BKSMT threshold (above 5)
  On-hit:      steals 2 enemy power, adds to player as a buff for 5 turns
               (stacks per hit, each stack expires independently)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. Witch's Athame  [Stab]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A black-handled ritual knife. Each strike invokes something different.
  Base DMG:    6
  STR req:     3
  Reach:       1
  Scaling:     BKSMT threshold (above 5)
  On-hit:      random debuff from pool — stun, fear, confusion, chill,
               bleeding, ignite, or disarm (equal weight per roll)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. Cursed Dagger  [Stab]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Exceptional balance. The edge is cursed to find flesh — sometimes yours.
  Base DMG:   12
  STR req:     3
  Reach:       1
  Scaling:     STR tiered
  On-hit:      15% chance the damage applies to the player instead of the enemy

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7. Bone Spear  [Stab]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Fused animal bones, somehow rigid. Smells wrong. Only reach-3 stab in the game.
  Base DMG:    7
  STR req:     5
  Reach:       3
  Scaling:     CON threshold (above 5)
  On-hit:      fear 3 turns

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8. Sacrificial Blade  [Stab]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The damage it deals grows the closer you are to death. The blade seems to want it.
  Base DMG:    3
  STR req:     3
  Reach:       1
  Scaling:     missing HP linear — +1 damage per 10% max HP missing (max +10 at 1 HP)
  Vampiric:    20%

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
9. Doom Mace  [Blunt]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A dull iron mace carved with a single number: 10. No other markings.
  Base DMG:    5
  STR req:     6
  Reach:       1
  Scaling:     BKSMT threshold (above 5)
  On-hit:      doom — 10-turn countdown applied to enemy; when it expires the
               enemy dies instantly regardless of remaining HP
               (exceptional vs high-HP enemies, wasted on weak ones)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
10. Petrifying Rod  [Blunt]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Smooth pale stone rod. Hits something fundamental when it connects right.
  Base DMG:    6
  STR req:     5
  Reach:       1
  Scaling:     STSMT threshold (above 5)
  On-hit:      20% petrify — complete immobilization for 2 turns; enemy takes
               double damage while petrified

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
11. Hex Knife  [Stab]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Covered in small carved symbols. Leaves an invisible mark on targets.
  Base DMG:    5
  STR req:     3
  Reach:       1
  Scaling:     BKSMT threshold (above 5)
  On-hit:      mark — target takes +3 damage from all sources for 6 turns;
               stacks per hit (no cap); each hit adds 3 more

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
12. Vengeance Club  [Blunt]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Absorbs hits taken and gives them back.
  Base DMG:    4
  STR req:     5
  Reach:       1
  Scaling:     none (stored damage is the scaling)
  Passive:     stores last 3 hits received from enemies; next swing releases
               all stored damage on top of base damage

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
13. Runic Club  [Blunt]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Each kill etches a new rune into the wood. The runes do something.
  Base DMG:    7
  STR req:     5
  Reach:       1
  Scaling:     STR tiered
  On-kill:     gain a random permanent minor buff (+1 random stat, or +5 max HP,
               or +1% crit chance)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
14. Spirit Lash  [Blunt]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Hits something other than the body. Always deals a percentage of current HP.
  Base DMG:    20% of enemy current HP (ignores defense entirely)
  STR req:     4
  Reach:       2
  Scaling:     none (HP% is the mechanic)
  Note:        never one-shots; never trivial. Each hit cuts them down by exactly
               20%. Exceptional vs tanky enemies, slow vs weak ones.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
15. Charm Rod  [Blunt]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
When it connects right the target forgets whose side they're on.
  Base DMG:    4
  STR req:     3
  Reach:       1
  Scaling:     SWAG linear
  On-hit:      20% charm — enemy fights for player for 4 turns, attacking
               nearest enemy

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
16. Warp Blade  [Stab]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
When it connects, the target isn't where they were.
  Base DMG:    6
  STR req:     4
  Reach:       1
  Scaling:     BKSMT threshold (above 5)
  On-hit:      35% warp — teleports enemy to a random open tile on the floor
               (scatters packs; can land enemies in fire/hazards)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
17. Hunger Blade  [Stab]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The edge sharpens as you bleed. A sustained damage curve the entire fight.
  Base DMG:    4
  STR req:     3
  Reach:       1
  Scaling:     STR tiered (base) + 1 per 5 missing HP (no cap, separate scaling)
  Vampiric:    10% (sustains the engine at low HP)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
18. Cursed Anchor  [Blunt]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A boat anchor on a length of chain. Impossible. Someone made it work.
  Base DMG:   18
  STR req:    14
  Reach:       1
  Break chance: 3%
  Scaling:     STR tiered
  Equip drawback: player movement energy cost +50% (you are dragging an anchor)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
19. Mirror Shard  [Stab]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A jagged piece of mirror mounted in leather wrapping.
  Base DMG:    4
  STR req:     2
  Reach:       1
  Scaling:     SWAG linear
  Passive:     while equipped, enemies take 15% of damage they deal to the
               player reflected back (pairs with high-SWAG defensive builds)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
20. Jinx Stick  [Blunt]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A carved stick that makes the hit feel wrong for a long time. Not stun —
they can move and use specials, they just cannot land a hit.
  Base DMG:    5
  STR req:     3
  Reach:       1
  Scaling:     STSMT threshold (above 5)
  On-hit:      jinx — enemy attacks guaranteed to miss the player for 2 turns
               (distinct from stun: enemy remains active but cannot connect)
