SPECIAL MECHANICS
-----------------

TWEAKER
- Weakest unit; spawns in groups of 1-3
- ROOM_GUARD: wanders spawn room, chases on player entry
- Speed 70 (30% slower than player)
- No special attacks or on-hit effects
- Cash drop: 0-3

CRACK ADDICT
- Short 3-tile sight radius (must get close to trigger)
- 4-8 damage — highest raw damage variance of any regular mob
- WANDER_AMBUSH: chases once player enters 3-tile range
- Cash drop: 0-5

DRUG DEALER
- Always spawns with 1-3 Tweaker bodyguards (ESCORT AI)
- Those tweakers follow and guard their room; transition to CHASING if player enters
- DEF 1, decent HP; not threatening alone
- High cash drop: 5-15

UGLY STRIPPER
- ALARM_CHASER: activates on first kill anywhere on floor
- Special: "High Heel Kick" (20% chance) — 2.0-3.0× damage multiplier + Bleeding (1 DOT/turn, 4 turns)
- On-hit: "Frightened" (20% chance) — Fear effect, 10-turn duration (forces flee)
- Most dangerous status applier in the game
- Cash drop: 0-8

BABY MOMMA
- Tankiest regular mob (25-45 HP)
- PASSIVE_UNTIL_HIT: completely ignores player until damaged; then chases forever
- On-hit: "Child Support" (30% chance, 20 turns) — drains 1 cash per player move (~$20 total)
- Always female; DEF 1; low damage (2-4)
- Cash drop: 0-4

NIGLET
- Fastest monster (120 speed, 20% faster than player)
- HIT_AND_RUN: attacks once then flees; resets if player gets far
- Special: "Pickpocket" (85% chance) — 0 damage, steals cash instead
- 10% dodge chance on melee attacks
- Cash drop: 1-3

FAT GOONER
- Heaviest tank (30-40 HP, DEF 3)
- FEMALE_ALARM: dormant until any female monster dies on floor; then chases forever
- Slowest (50 speed, half player speed)
- Always male; no special attacks
- DEF 3 makes him resistant to early-game weapons
- Cash drop: 0-5

THUG
- Fixed 5 damage (highest consistent damage of regular mobs)
- WANDER_AMBUSH: activates on sight
- On-hit: "Mogged" (50% chance, 10 turns) — stacks Swagger reduction, eroding melee defense
- Mostly male (80%)
- Cash drop: 5-12

JEROME (BOSS)
- Only monster with natural crit chance: 9% (Street-Smarts = 3)
- MEANDER: always chases, no state transitions
- Special: "Knockback Punch" (25% chance) — 1× damage + knockback
- HEAL MECHANIC: when HP < 25, eats fried chicken (up to 2 times)
    - Each meal: +10 HP + Well Fed buff (+2 power, 10 turns)
    - After 2 meals: effective power = 4 + 4 = 8 damage
    - Effective HP to kill = 50 + 20 heal = 70 total damage needed
- Always male; DEF 2; Cash drop: 20-50

STAT SCALING FORMULAS (stats.py)
----------------------------------

Max HP     = 30 + effective_constitution × 10    (range: 80-150)
Crit %     = effective_street_smarts × 3%        (range: 15%-36%)
Defense    = (effective_swagger - 5) // 2         (range: 0-3)
Unarmed dmg = BASE_POWER(4) + STR - UNARMED_STR_BASE(5)  → avg ~7

All stats start in range [5,12], 46 total points to distribute.

Damage formula: max(1, attacker_power - defender_defense)

KEY BALANCE OBSERVATIONS
--------------------------

1. NO FLOOR SCALING
   Same enemies on floor 1 and floor 4. No difficulty ramp exists.
   Future work: floor-scaled variants, stat multipliers, or new enemy tiers.

2. CRACK ADDICT SPIKE RISK
   4-8 damage with 0 defense. A pack of 2-3 at close range (3-tile sight) can
   delete a player before they can react. Highest burst ceiling of regular mobs.

3. FAT GOONER / BABY MOMMA ARE CHORE FIGHTS
   DEF 3 on Gooner means early weapons barely scratch him. Baby Momma's 25-45 HP
   with no offensive threat makes killing her feel like grinding. Consider giving
   both a reason to engage (unique drops, ability-gating, etc.).

4. JEROME'S HEAL MECHANIC CHANGES THE MATH
   Two 10 HP heals + Well Fed buff means the fight requires ~70 effective damage,
   not 50. Power spike to 8 after meals is a noticeable threat jump. The mechanic
   rewards burst damage (kill before he heals).

5. STATUS EFFECT STACKING
   Stripper Fear + Bleeding while Thug Mogged debuffs are active can cascade fast.
   These interactions are undesigned but can spiral. May warrant explicit synergy
   caps or diminishing returns in a future balance pass.

6. CHILD SUPPORT IS THE HARDEST ECONOMY DEBUFF
   $20 lost over 20 turns per proc. On early floors, this can deny multiple shop
   upgrades. Baby Momma's passive nature (she doesn't attack until hit) means
   players often provoke her accidentally.

7. NIGLET DESIGN GAP
   Pickpocket (85% chance) means Niglet almost never deals real damage. Combined
   with hit-and-run and 10% dodge, it's more of an economy drain than a threat.
   May benefit from an escalation condition (comes back angrier after fleeing).

8. DRUG DEALER + ESCORT SYNERGY UNDERUTILIZED
   The escort mechanic is implemented but Tweakers are so weak that the "bodyguard"
   fantasy doesn't land. Higher escort quality on later floors would sell the theme.
