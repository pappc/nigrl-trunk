DEEP-FRYING SKILL TREE — 10 COMPLETE VARIANT LAYOUTS
NIGRL Roguelike | Design Doc v2.0 | 2026-03-28

FIXED POINTS (all trees keep these):
  L1 — Fry Shot (activated, EXISTS): Hurl hot grease at target within 4 tiles.
       Dmg: max(1, CON+2-DEF). Applies 3 Greasy stacks. 15t cooldown.
       Deep-Frying XP = damage dealt.
  L2 — Extra Greasy (passive, EXISTS): Fried food items restore +20% HP when eaten.

CALIBRATION BASELINE
---------------------
  CON range: 6-12 base, typical tanky build = 10.
  Player HP at CON 10: 130 HP.
  Enemy HP floors 1-2: ~10-30 HP. Floors 3-4: ~25-60 HP.
  Fry Shot at CON 10 vs DEF 2: 10 dmg.
  Grease Fire reaction: stacks*2 burst + stacks*2 ignite stacks added. Greasy consumed.
  GreasyEffect on enemies: triggers Grease Fire when Ignite applied (auto, no extra code).
  GreasyEffect on player: +3% dodge per stack (MAX 10 stacks = 30% dodge).
  SlowEffect: ratio=0.5 halves energy gain. SlippedEffect: skip 1 turn + 50% more melee dmg.
  Ignite: 1 dmg/stack/turn, ~5t duration. Max stacks 9999.
  Temp HP absorption order: Temp HP > Armor > HP.


================================================================================
TREE 1: "THE GREASE FIRE SPECIALIST" — Pure Burst DPS
================================================================================

THEME
-----
Everything feeds the Grease Fire reaction. Stack Greasy fast, apply Ignite, repeat.
Low-cooldown Fry Shot upgrades cascade into explosive multi-target chain reactions.
The capstone converts the player's own burning body into a detonation weapon.

  L1  — Fry Shot (activated, EXISTS)
  L2  — Extra Greasy (passive, EXISTS)
  L3  — FLASH FRY (passive)
         Name: "Flash Fry"
         Type: passive (on_player_melee_hit hook)
         Desc: "+2 CON. On every successful melee hit, 30% chance to apply 1 Greasy
               stack to the target (duration 20t). Chance increases to 50% if
               target already has 3+ Greasy stacks."
         Scaling: CON +2 flat stat bonus. Proc rate fixed at 30%/50%.
         Impl: check in combat.py melee hit block after damage dealt.
               engine.skills.get("Deep-Frying").level >= 3 →
               chance = 0.30 if greasy_stacks < 3 else 0.50;
               apply_effect(defender, engine, "greasy", duration=20, stacks=1)

  L4  — SPLATTER (activated, cooldown)
         Name: "Splatter"
         Type: activated, target_type=AOE_CIRCLE (pseudo — uses SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS
               with Chebyshev-1 splash in execute_at)
         Desc: "Hurl the fryer basket in a wide arc. 3x3 AOE centered on target
               tile within 4 tiles. All entities hit take CON-2 dmg and receive
               2 Greasy stacks. Cooldown 20t."
         Scaling: Dmg = max(1, CON-2-DEF). CON 10 vs DEF 2 = 8 dmg per target.
         Impl: ability_id="splatter". execute_at gathers entities in Chebyshev(1)
               of (tx,ty), deals damage to each, applies greasy 2 stacks.
               get_affected_tiles returns 3x3. XP = total damage dealt.
               engine.ability_cooldowns["splatter"] = 20

  L5  — IGNITION POINT (passive)
         Name: "Ignition Point"
         Type: passive (modifies Grease Fire reaction globally)
         Desc: "Grease Fire reactions you cause deal +CON/3 bonus burst damage
               (rounds down). At CON 10: +3 per reaction. CON 12: +4."
         Scaling: bonus = CON // 3. Applied in _check_grease_fire_synergy().
         Impl: check engine.skills.get("Deep-Frying").level >= 5;
               damage += engine.player_stats.effective_constitution // 3
               New PlayerStats bool flag not needed — level check is sufficient.
         Note: Stacks with any other Grease Fire modifier (Full Render, etc.)

  L6  — BURNING LARD (activated, cooldown)
         Name: "Burning Lard"
         Type: activated, target_type=LINE_FROM_PLAYER
         Desc: "Fire a 6-tile stream of burning grease in a direction. Each entity
               in the line takes CON+TOL-DEF dmg and receives 3 Greasy stacks.
               Each tile in the line becomes an oil_slick hazard (10t). Cooldown 25t.
               TOL 10+: cooldown reduced to 20t."
         Scaling: Dmg = max(1, CON+TOL-DEF). CON 10+TOL 8 vs DEF 2 = 16 dmg.
         Impl: ability_id="burning_lard". execute_at traces 6-tile ray (Bresenham).
               For each tile: deal damage to entity if present, apply 3 Greasy stacks,
               spawn oil_slick hazard (hazard_type="oil_slick", char='~',
               color=(220,180,50), blocks_movement=False, hazard_duration=10).
               In _run_energy_loop: entities stepping on oil_slick tile receive
               2 Greasy stacks + 30% chance SlowEffect(ratio=0.7, duration=2).
               cooldown = 20 if tol >= 10 else 25.

  L7  — GREASE TRAP (activated, per-floor)
         Name: "Grease Trap"
         Type: activated, target_type=SELF (instant, applies to all visible enemies)
         Desc: "1/floor. Instantly apply (1 + CON//4) Greasy stacks to ALL visible
               enemies. CON 10: 3 stacks each. CON 12: 4 stacks each."
         Scaling: stacks = 1 + CON // 4. CON 6=2, CON 8=3, CON 10=3, CON 12=4.
         Impl: ability_id="grease_trap". charge_type=PER_FLOOR, max_charges=1.
               execute: for each entity in dungeon.entities that is a monster and
               alive and (entity.x, entity.y) in dungeon.visible: apply greasy stacks.

  L8  — FULL RENDER (passive)
         Name: "Full Render"
         Type: passive (modifies Grease Fire globally)
         Desc: "Grease Fire burst damage multiplier becomes (2 + CON//5) per stack
               instead of 2. CON 8: x3/stack. CON 10: x4/stack. CON 12: x4/stack.
               Additionally, half the Greasy stacks survive the reaction
               (floor(stacks/2) remain). Ignite stacks added = stacks*3 not stacks*2."
         Scaling: mult = 2 + CON // 5. CON 10: 4x per stack.
         Impl: New PlayerStats bool: full_render_active (default False). Set True
               at L8 unlock. In _check_grease_fire_synergy(): check full_render_active.
               damage = greasy_stacks * (2 + con // 5) instead of greasy_stacks * 2.
               bonus_stacks = greasy_stacks * 3.
               Remove only floor(greasy_stacks/2) stacks instead of all.

  L9  — THERMITE BASTE (activated, total charges)
         Name: "Thermite Baste"
         Type: activated, target_type=ADJACENT
         Desc: "3 total charges (run). Apply MAX Greasy stacks (10, duration 30t) and
               (1 + CON//3) Ignite stacks to an adjacent enemy simultaneously.
               Grease Fire triggers instantly. CON 10: 3 ignite stacks on top of burst."
         Scaling: ignite_stacks = 1 + CON // 3.
                  With Full Render (L8) at CON 10: 10*(4) = 40 burst dmg, 30 ignite stacks.
         Impl: ability_id="thermite_baste". charge_type=TOTAL, max_charges=3.
               target_type=ADJACENT. execute_at: apply 10 Greasy stacks, then apply
               ignite stacks — _check_grease_fire_synergy fires automatically.

  L10 — THE FORBIDDEN DEEP FRY (activated, ONCE per run)
         Name: "The Forbidden Deep Fry"
         Type: activated, target_type=SELF
         Desc: "ONCE per run. Every enemy on the floor: (5+CON//3) Greasy stacks +
               CON//2 Ignite stacks — Grease Fire on all simultaneously. Player gains
               CON*5 Temp HP and 3 Greasy stacks (dodge). Player also catches Ignite
               (3 stacks, 5t) — you're covered in grease too. All floor tiles become
               oil_slick for 20t. Permadeath note: self-ignite is non-negotiable."
         Scaling: Enemy greasy = 5+CON//3 (CON 10=8), enemy ignite = CON//2 (CON 10=5).
                  Player temp HP = CON * 5 (CON 10 = 50). Player ignite = 3 stacks.
         Impl: ability_id="forbidden_deep_fry". charge_type=ONCE.
               execute: iterate dungeon monsters; apply greasy+ignite stacks.
               Apply temp HP and greasy to player; apply ignite to player.
               New engine state: floor_oil_slick_turns (int, countdown 20 per tick).
               In movement resolution: if floor_oil_slick_turns > 0, all passable tiles
               behave as oil_slick (apply 2 Greasy + 30% slow on entity step).

KEY SYNERGIES
  Flash Fry (L3) → Splatter (L4): melee stacks greasy on one, AOE stacks on group.
  Ignition Point (L5) + Full Render (L8): stacks additively with multiplicative burst.
  Grease Trap (L7) + Burning Lard (L6): mass greasy then line of fire = chain reactions.
  Thermite Baste (L9): guaranteed Grease Fire setup even without Ignite source.
  Full Render (L8) is required to unlock the true ceiling of The Forbidden Deep Fry (L10).


================================================================================
TREE 2: "THE SKILLET GENERAL" — Control / Terrain Denial
================================================================================

THEME
-----
Oil slicks, hazard placement, and kiting. The player creates a killing field before
combat begins. Greasy stacks come from terrain, not direct hits. Tanky CON build
that controls the room geometry.

  L1  — Fry Shot (activated, EXISTS)
  L2  — Extra Greasy (passive, EXISTS)
  L3  — SPILL (activated, per-floor)
         Name: "Spill"
         Type: activated, target_type=ADJACENT_TILE
         Desc: "3/floor. Place an oil_slick hazard on an adjacent floor tile
               (duration = 10 + CON turns). Enemies stepping on it get 2 Greasy
               stacks and 30% chance to slow (ratio=0.7, 2t)."
         Scaling: duration = 10 + CON. CON 6=16t, CON 10=20t, CON 12=22t.
         Impl: ability_id="spill". PER_FLOOR, max_charges=3.
               ADJACENT_TILE target. Create oil_slick hazard entity at (tx,ty).
               In _run_energy_loop: entities entering oil_slick tile get greasy+slow.
               Player immune to their own oil_slick (check entity == engine.player).

  L4  — SLICK FLOOR (passive)
         Name: "Slick Floor"
         Type: passive
         Desc: "+2 CON. Oil slick hazards you place last 50% longer and apply 1
               additional Greasy stack. Enemies with 3+ Greasy stacks on an oil
               slick have a 20% chance each turn to fall (SlippedEffect: skip turn,
               +50% incoming melee dmg for 2t)."
         Scaling: CON +2 flat. Duration bonus = 0.5x multiplicative.
         Impl: Check engine.skills.get("Deep-Frying").level >= 4 when spawning
               oil_slick in Spill/Burning Lard. Multiply duration by 1.5.
               In _run_energy_loop oil_slick processing: check greasy stacks >= 3,
               roll 20% → apply_effect(entity, engine, "slipped").

  L5  — DEEP FRY ZONE (activated, per-floor)
         Name: "Deep Fry Zone"
         Type: activated, target_type=SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS (targets a tile, AOE radius 2)
         Desc: "2/floor. Create a 5-tile radius zone of oil_slick on the floor
               centered on the target tile. All enemies currently in the zone
               receive 2 Greasy stacks. Zone lasts 10 + CON//2 turns."
         Scaling: radius always 2 (5x5 Chebyshev). Duration = 10 + CON//2.
                  CON 10: 15t. CON 12: 16t.
         Impl: ability_id="deep_fry_zone". PER_FLOOR, max_charges=2.
               execute_at: iterate Chebyshev radius 2 around (tx,ty); for each
               passable floor tile spawn oil_slick entity. For each enemy in zone:
               apply 2 Greasy stacks immediately.

  L6  — OIL RIVER (activated, cooldown)
         Name: "Oil River"
         Type: activated, target_type=LINE_FROM_PLAYER
         Desc: "Fire a 7-tile stream of oil from the player outward. No damage —
               pure terrain denial. Each tile becomes oil_slick (15t). Enemies
               in the line's path receive 3 Greasy stacks. Cooldown 18t.
               The oil does not ignite automatically but is primed for Fry Shot."
         Scaling: Oil slick duration = 15t fixed. CON does not scale tiles.
         Impl: ability_id="oil_river". cooldown 18t.
               LINE_FROM_PLAYER. execute_at traces 7-tile ray. No damage formula —
               just spawn oil_slick hazards + apply 3 Greasy to entities in path.
               Deliberately cheaper to use than Burning Lard (no damage, lower CD).

  L7  — PORK RIND ARMOR (activated, cooldown)
         Name: "Pork Rind Armor"
         Type: activated, target_type=SELF
         Desc: "+2 CON. Coat yourself in crystallized fried fat. Gain CON*3 Temp HP.
               While Temp HP remains, melee attackers have 40% chance to receive 1
               Greasy stack (grease rubs off onto them). Cooldown 25t."
         Scaling: Temp HP = CON * 3. CON 10 = 30 Temp HP.
         Impl: ability_id="pork_rind_armor". SELF. cooldown 25t.
               execute: engine.player.temp_hp += CON * 3.
               Store engine.pork_rind_active = True. In combat.py when player is
               hit by melee: if engine.pork_rind_active and random() < 0.4:
               apply_effect(attacker, engine, "greasy", duration=20, stacks=1).
               Clear pork_rind_active when player temp_hp reaches 0.
               New engine state: pork_rind_active (bool, default False).

  L8  — QUAGMIRE (passive)
         Name: "Quagmire"
         Type: passive (modifies oil_slick behavior)
         Desc: "Oil slick tiles you created become Quagmire — enemies that step on
               them also receive the SlowEffect (ratio=0.5, 3t) in addition to Greasy.
               Enemies at 5+ Greasy stacks in Quagmire also lose 1 DR (armor shred)
               for 5 turns per step. The shred is permanent for the floor duration."
         Scaling: Slow ratio=0.5 fixed. DR shred = 1 per proc.
         Impl: In oil_slick step processing: check D-F level >= 8.
               Apply slow(ratio=0.5, dur=3) in addition to greasy.
               If entity's greasy stacks >= 5: entity.defense -= 1 (min 0),
               store engine.quagmire_shredded = {entity_id: stacks_of_shred}.

  L9  — FLASH FLOOD (activated, per-floor)
         Name: "Flash Flood"
         Type: activated, target_type=SELF (global, instant)
         Desc: "1/floor. Flood the entire floor with oil. ALL passable tiles become
               oil_slick (8t). All visible enemies receive 3 Greasy stacks instantly.
               Player is immune to their own Flash Flood oil tiles. Massive setup turn."
         Scaling: Duration = 8t fixed (short flood — use Quagmire slow to maximize it).
         Impl: ability_id="flash_flood". PER_FLOOR, max_charges=1.
               execute: set engine.floor_oil_slick_turns = 8 (player-immune variant).
               Apply 3 Greasy stacks to all visible enemies.
               Optionally: spawn individual oil_slick entities for visual consistency.

  L10 — GREASE MONOLITH (activated, ONCE per run)
         Name: "Grease Monolith"
         Type: activated, target_type=SELF
         Desc: "ONCE per run. The floor becomes permanent Quagmire for the rest of
               the run on this floor — oil_slick tiles never expire. Summon a
               Grease Monolith (immovable hazard entity) at the player's position:
               radius-3 aura that applies 2 Greasy stacks per turn to all enemies
               in range. Player gains +CON permanent armor bonus for this floor.
               The Monolith has HP = CON*10 and can be targeted/destroyed by enemies."
         Scaling: Monolith HP = CON * 10 (CON 10 = 100 HP). Armor bonus = CON//3.
                  Greasy aura: 2 stacks/turn to all enemies within 3 tiles.
         Impl: ability_id="grease_monolith". ONCE.
               execute: set engine.floor_oil_slick_permanent = True (new flag;
               oil_slick hazard_duration never decrements when flag is set).
               Spawn a "grease_monolith" entity (entity_type="hazard", char='O',
               color=(220,180,50), blocks_movement=True, max_hp=CON*10, hp=CON*10,
               alive=True). In _run_energy_loop: for each alive monster within 3 tiles
               of monolith: apply 2 Greasy stacks.
               New engine state: floor_oil_slick_permanent (bool), grease_monolith_entity.

KEY SYNERGIES
  Spill (L3) + Slick Floor (L4): the cornerstone — cheaply placed slicks that last longer.
  Deep Fry Zone (L5) + Quagmire (L8): zone placement + shred combo.
  Pork Rind Armor (L7) + Flash Flood (L9): survive the opening, then flood the floor.
  Grease Monolith (L10) + Quagmire (L8): monolith aura stacks Greasy until DR is shredded.
  Oil River (L6): the connector — cheaply links zones of oil_slick.


================================================================================
TREE 3: "THE DEEP FRYER FIST" — Melee Hybrid
================================================================================

THEME
-----
Deep-Frying power expressed through fists and melee weapons. Greasy stacks on the
player fuel dodge, grease rubs off onto enemies mid-combat, and melee kills with
Greasy enemies trigger a violent burst. CON + STR cross-scaling at higher levels.

  L1  — Fry Shot (activated, EXISTS)
  L2  — Extra Greasy (passive, EXISTS)
  L3  — OILY FISTS (passive)
         Name: "Oily Fists"
         Type: passive
         Desc: "+2 CON. On every successful player melee hit, apply 1 Greasy stack
               to the target (100% proc rate). If the player has 3+ Greasy stacks,
               apply 2 Greasy stacks instead."
         Scaling: CON +2. Always procs on hit — no random component.
         Impl: check D-F level >= 3 in combat.py melee hit block.
               stacks = 2 if player_greasy_stacks >= 3 else 1.
               apply_effect(defender, engine, "greasy", ...).
               player_greasy_stacks = count from player.status_effects.

  L4  — GREASE BATH (activated, cooldown)
         Name: "Grease Bath"
         Type: activated, target_type=SELF
         Desc: "Douse yourself in oil. Gain 3 Greasy stacks (+9% dodge) and a
               10t buff: all melee damage dealt gains +STR//2 bonus damage.
               While Greasy Bath is active, melee kills restore 3 HP. Cooldown 20t."
         Scaling: Melee bonus = STR // 2. CON does not scale this — STR synergy.
                  Healing = 3 HP per melee kill. Duration 10t.
         Impl: ability_id="grease_bath". SELF. cooldown 20t.
               apply_effect(player, engine, "greasy", duration=50, stacks=3).
               apply_effect(player, engine, "grease_bath_buff", duration=10).
               New effect: GreaseBathBuff — modify_outgoing_melee_damage adds STR//2;
               on_player_kill_while_active: heal 3 HP.
               New engine state: grease_bath_active (bool) checked on kill handler.

  L5  — TALLOW SKIN (passive)
         Name: "Tallow Skin"
         Type: passive
         Desc: "+2 CON, +2 SWG. Your grease stacks become structural. Each Greasy
               stack on the player provides +1 temporary armor (in addition to the
               existing +3% dodge). Armor disappears when the stack expires. Max
               contribution: 10 stacks = +10 temporary armor."
         Scaling: +1 armor per Greasy stack. CON +2, SWG +2.
         Impl: Track player Greasy stack count on change. engine.player_stats.
               temporary_armor_bonus += (new_stacks - old_stacks) when greasy
               apply/expire fires. This requires hooking into GreasyEffect.apply,
               on_reapply, and expire to fire a callback.
               Alternatively: recalculate temporary_armor_bonus = greasy_stacks in
               the _run_energy_loop tick (simpler, no hook changes needed).

  L6  — KNUCKLE CRUNCH (activated, cooldown)
         Name: "Knuckle Crunch"
         Type: activated, target_type=ADJACENT
         Desc: "Slam an adjacent enemy with your greased fists. Dmg: CON + STR - DEF.
               If target has 3+ Greasy stacks, the hit crits (2x damage multiplier).
               On crit: apply 3 Ignite stacks to target (triggers Grease Fire).
               Cooldown 15t."
         Scaling: Dmg = max(1, CON+STR-DEF). CON 10+STR 8 vs DEF 2 = 16 base.
                  Crit: x2 = 32 dmg. Grease Fire at 3 stacks = 6 burst + 6 ignite.
                  With Ignite Point from Tree 1 these synergize.
         Impl: ability_id="knuckle_crunch". ADJACENT. cooldown 15t.
               Check greasy stacks on target >= 3 → crit (multiply by engine.crit_multiplier).
               If crit: apply 3 ignite stacks (grease fire fires automatically).
               XP = damage dealt.

  L7  — FAT RENDER (passive)
         Name: "Fat Render"
         Type: passive
         Desc: "+2 CON. When you kill an enemy that has Greasy stacks, absorb their
               grease: gain Greasy stacks equal to floor(their_stacks/2) (min 1).
               Also gain temp HP = 1 + CON//5 per absorbed stack. Encourages
               maintaining Greasy stacks on self via kills."
         Scaling: Temp HP per absorbed stack = 1 + CON//5. CON 10: 3 HP/stack.
                  If enemy had 4 Greasy stacks: absorb 2, gain 6 temp HP.
         Impl: In entity_died event handler (killer == player check):
               check D-F level >= 7; get greasy stacks on dead entity;
               if stacks > 0: apply floor(stacks/2) to player greasy; add temp HP.

  L8  — DEEP FRYER STANCE (activated, per-floor)
         Name: "Deep Fryer Stance"
         Type: activated, target_type=SELF
         Desc: "3/floor. Enter Fryer Stance for 15 turns. In stance:
               — All Greasy stacks on the player double (up to cap 10).
               — Melee attacks cost 15 less energy (faster melee).
               — On melee kill: spawn a Grease Fire hazard on the dead enemy's tile
                 (fire entity, 5t duration, applies 2 Ignite stacks to any entity
                 that enters it — uses existing fire hazard entity from hazards.py)."
         Scaling: Energy cost reduction = 15 (flat). Duration 15t.
         Impl: ability_id="deep_fryer_stance". PER_FLOOR, max_charges=3.
               apply_effect(player, engine, "deep_fryer_stance_buff", duration=15).
               New effect: modifies melee energy cost (-15 via modify_energy_gain).
               On kill while stance active: call hazards.create_fire(entity.x, entity.y)
               and add to dungeon.entities. Check engine flag in entity_died handler.

  L9  — MASTER BUTCHER (passive)
         Name: "Master Butcher"
         Type: passive
         Desc: "+4 CON. Killing any enemy while you have Greasy stacks on yourself
               instantly resets the cooldown of Fry Shot. Killing an enemy with
               Greasy stacks on them grants Deep-Frying XP equal to 20 * their
               Greasy stack count."
         Scaling: CON +4. XP = 20 * enemy's greasy stacks.
         Impl: In entity_died handler: check D-F level >= 9.
               If player has greasy stacks: engine.ability_cooldowns["fry_shot"] = 0.
               If dead entity had greasy stacks: gain Deep-Frying XP = 20 * stacks.

  L10 — THE FLESH IS THE FRYER (activated, ONCE per run)
         Name: "The Flesh Is The Fryer"
         Type: activated, target_type=SELF
         Desc: "ONCE per run. For 30 turns: player becomes a living deep fryer.
               Every melee hit applies 5 Greasy stacks AND 3 Ignite stacks to the
               target (Grease Fire guaranteed every hit). Player cannot gain more
               than 10 Greasy stacks on self but those stacks never expire during
               the 30t duration. Player's temp HP is permanently equal to Greasy
               stacks * CON (refreshed each tick, disappears when buff ends)."
         Scaling: Temp HP = Greasy stacks * CON. At 10 stacks + CON 10 = 100 Temp HP.
         Impl: ability_id="flesh_is_fryer". ONCE. SELF.
               apply_effect(player, engine, "flesh_fryer_buff", duration=30).
               New effect: on_player_melee_hit fires — apply 5 greasy + 3 ignite to target.
               Before_turn or tick: recalculate player.temp_hp = greasy_stacks * CON
               (but only add delta if increasing, don't reduce mid-buff).
               On buff expire: engine.flesh_fryer_active = False; temp_hp normalizes.

KEY SYNERGIES
  Oily Fists (L3) → Tallow Skin (L5): constant greasy generation = constant armor.
  Grease Bath (L4) + Knuckle Crunch (L6): STR scaling on both; Grease Bath buffs crunch.
  Deep Fryer Stance (L8) + Master Butcher (L9): stance kills reset Fry Shot CD constantly.
  Fat Render (L7): sustain loop — kills give health AND dodge stacks.
  The Flesh Is The Fryer (L10): requires Oily Fists + Tallow Skin already running.


================================================================================
TREE 4: "THE SLOW BURN" — DOT / Attrition
================================================================================

THEME
-----
Long-lasting debuffs, stacking Ignite, and patience. Instead of burst Grease Fire,
this tree converts Greasy stacks into persistent Ignite ticks and slows enemy healing
(if any). The capstone is a Creeping Lard DOT that builds infinitely.

  L1  — Fry Shot (activated, EXISTS)
  L2  — Extra Greasy (passive, EXISTS)
  L3  — RENDERED FAT (passive)
         Name: "Rendered Fat"
         Type: passive
         Desc: "When you apply Greasy stacks to an enemy that already has 1+ Ignite
               stacks, deal bonus damage equal to their Ignite stack count. No extra
               Greasy stacks needed — just the overlap. This procs once per ability
               or melee hit, not per Greasy stack."
         Scaling: Bonus dmg = current Ignite stacks. At 3 Ignite: +3 bonus per hit.
         Impl: In _check_grease_fire_synergy or in apply_effect for "greasy":
               check if entity already has ignite; if so, deal bonus damage equal
               to ignite.stacks. Only procs when Greasy is newly applied.

  L4  — LOW SIMMER (passive)
         Name: "Low Simmer"
         Type: passive
         Desc: "+2 TOL. Ignite you apply lasts 3 turns longer (duration +3).
               Enemies with both Greasy and Ignite take 1 additional damage per
               Greasy stack per 3 turns (a slow accumulating DOT separate from Ignite)."
         Scaling: TOL +2. Ignite duration: +3t at all times.
         Impl: When apply_effect("ignite") is called and D-F level >= 4:
               incoming.duration += 3.
               The additional DOT: in tick_all_effects, check both greasy + ignite
               present; if so, entity takes floor(greasy_stacks/3) bonus damage per tick
               (1 dmg at 3 stacks, 2 at 6 stacks, 3 at 9 stacks).
               New attribute on IgniteEffect: df_simmer_active (bool); or a separate
               check in tick_all_effects.

  L5  — INFUSED BASTING (activated, cooldown)
         Name: "Infused Basting"
         Type: activated, target_type=SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS
         Desc: "Select a visible enemy within 5 tiles. Apply 5 Greasy stacks +
               3 Ignite stacks (Grease Fire triggers). Then: for the next 5 turns,
               each time you deal any damage to that enemy, they receive 1 additional
               Ignite stack. This DOT bonus stacks and persists through Grease Fire.
               Cooldown 22t."
         Scaling: Greasy = 5, Ignite = 3. Bonus ignite per hit (5t window): 1 stack.
         Impl: ability_id="infused_basting". CD 22t.
               Apply 5 greasy + 3 ignite to target.
               Store engine.infused_basting_target_id and engine.infused_basting_turns = 5.
               In combat.py deal_damage: if engine.infused_basting_turns > 0 and
               target is infused_basting_target_id: apply 1 ignite stack to target.

  L6  — DRIPPING (passive)
         Name: "Dripping"
         Type: passive
