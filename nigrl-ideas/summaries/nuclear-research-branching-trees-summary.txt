NUCLEAR RESEARCH — Branching Skill Trees Summary
=================================================
Source file: nigrl-ideas/nuclear-research-branching-skill-trees.txt
10 branching topology variations. All 10 shapes used (one each).

QUICK REFERENCE — All 10 Variations:

V1  "Ground Zero"           Y-split (L3)       Bomb Specialist vs. Fallout Controller
V2  "The Reactor"           Diamond            Aura Builder vs. Single-Target Nuke
V3  "Chain Reaction"        Triple Fork (L4)   Bomb Mastery / Cascade Killer / Precision Targeting
V4  "The Dead Hand"         W-shape            Containment vs. Berserker (with death insurance)
V5  "Decay Chain"           Long Trunk Y       Long investment → Ramp Damage vs. Fusion One-Shot
V6  "Fission/Fusion"        Inverted Y         Scholar (BKS/Contaminate) vs. Aggressor (Fissile/Reactor)
V7  "Critical Geometry"     Ladder/Zigzag      4 build combinations from 2 paired choices
V8  "Ground Zero Protocol"  Trident            3 arms: Contaminator / Bomb Engineer / Reactor (solo)
V9  "Isotope Farm"          Double Diamond     4 paths converging on Isotope Protocol capstone
V10 "Mad Scientist"         Asymmetric Y       Short safe branch (Critical Mass) vs. long mad branch

CONSTANT TRUNK ELEMENTS (present in every variation):
  - Irradiated Intellect: +3 BKS, rad gain bonus scaled by BKS%, mutation block <=150 rad
  - Rad Bomb: 3/floor, 5x5, 15+BKS/2 damage, 25 rad cost (refund at 100+ rad), 3t fuse, 50 XP/cast

SPELL INVENTORY (all new abilities defined in this doc):
  rad_nova_b         — instant 5x5 AOE around player; 1/floor; 50 rad cost
  meltdown_b         — 9x9 delayed nuke (5t fuse); 1/floor; 75 rad; 30+BKS*2 dmg
  contaminate_b      — single-target rad DOT; 3/floor; 15 rad; 3 rad/turn x 8t
  fallout_zone_b     — 3x3 radioactive terrain; 2/floor; 30 rad; 12t duration; 4 rad/turn
  critical_mass_b    — self-buff; 1/floor; 40 rad; next spell guaranteed crit x8
  fission_b          — 3 mini-bombs at 40% each; 1/floor; 50 rad
  fusion_detonation_b — drain all rad; single target; dmg = rad/2; FLOOR_ONLY
  half_life_b        — mark enemy; burst at <=50% HP; 2/floor; 20 rad
  isotope_b          — mark enemy; on kill -> 3x3 Fallout Zone; 3/floor; 20 rad
  chain_reaction_b   — passive: rad kills cascade 10+BKS/4 rad to adjacent; 1-hop max
  reactor_core_b     — toggle aura; 5 rad/turn drain; 3 rad/turn to adjacent enemies
  enrichment_b       — unlimited; 15 rad -> +1 stack; max 5 stacks; +5 dmg/stack on next bomb
  ground_zero_b      — redirect next bomb's epicenter to marked tile; 1/floor
  dead_hand_b        — 1/run death prevention; detonates all crystals; survive at 1 HP
  scram_b            — 30% dmg reduction for 3t; 1/floor; 20 rad
  prompt_criticality_b — passive; 5 turns at 200+ rad = next bomb MEGA_CRIT x4

BRANCH IDENTITY MATRIX (what each path specializes in):
  Bomb Specialist    — Short Fuse + Fission: faster bombs, triple-split, Enrichment stacking
  Fallout Controller — Half-Life + Fallout Zone: terrain denial + execute marks
  Aura Builder       — Reactor Core: passive ambient irradiation; pairs with Chain Reaction
  Single-Target Nuke — Fusion Detonation: spend all rad for 100+ dmg single-target
  Mad Bomber         — Meltdown: 9x9 once-per-floor screen clear
  Cascade Killer     — Chain Reaction: rad kills cascade; Isotope marks leave hazard zones
  Glass Cannon       — Prompt Criticality + Fusion: maximum burst, minimum safety margin
  Contaminator       — Contaminate + Fallout Zone: DOT + terrain; enemies die to the floor
  Experimenter       — Fissile Material + Decay Chain: builds power from taking damage

RECOMMENDED IMPLEMENTATION ORDER:
  1. V1 (Y-split)    — simplest; establishes two core Nuclear Research branch identities
  2. V5 (Long Trunk) — closest to existing linear tree; easiest transition
  3. V3 (Triple Fork) — adds Chain Reaction passive; opens cross-tree synergies
  4. V7 (Ladder)     — tests dual-choice-node mechanic
  5. Rest after those foundations are proven
