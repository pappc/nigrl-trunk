10 Unique Equippable Items That Grant Abilities

CATEGORY A — RESOURCE/CURRENCY SYSTEM ITEMS (4 items)

1. Crown of the Corner Boy [Hat, +2 SWG]
   Resource: REP — +1 per kill. Stored on engine.hat.rep_count.
   Abilities: Corner Intimidate (3 rep: frighten nearest enemy 6t), Corner Henchman (8 rep:
   summon ally for 20t via is_summon), Corner War Cry (all rep, min 12: stun all visible
   enemies 1t per 4 rep spent).
   New infrastructure: hat grants_abilities support, engine kill hook, rep_count display effect.

2. The Knuckle Diary [Weapon/beating, 4 power, +1 STS]
   Resource: ENTRIES — +1 per crit while equipped. Cap 10. Stored on weapon entity.
   Abilities: Diary Quick Hit (2 entries: bonus adjacent attack, free action), Diary Break
   Defense (3 entries: zeroes target defense 8t via new BrokenDefenseEffect), Diary
   Retrospective (all entries, min 3: strike target once per entry).
   New infrastructure: weapon grants_abilities list support, crit detection, BrokenDefenseEffect.

3. The Hustle Ring [Ring, +1 STS/+1 TOL]
   Resource: HUSTLE — +1 per tile moved. Cap 20. Stored on engine.hustle_ring_steps.
   Abilities: Hustle Rush (6: ADJACENT_TILE, move 3 tiles passing through enemies for STR dmg),
   Hustle Trail (10: paint last 5 walked tiles with +10 energy/tick spray for 8t), Hustle
   Recovery (all, min 8: HealOnMove effect — 2 HP per step for duration=hustle turns).
   New infrastructure: ring grants_abilities support, handle_move hook, move history deque,
   hustle spray type, HustleRecoveryFlowEffect (modify_movement heals 2).

4. Tap-In Talisman [Neck, 15 armor, +1 CON]
   Resource: PAGES — +1 per time player takes HP damage. Cap 15. Stored on engine.neck.page_count.
   Abilities: Tap Dodge Window (2 pages: 90 dodge for 2t), Tap Shield (all, min 5: temp HP = 3x
   pages spent, cap 60), Tap Trauma Response (6 pages + 3 HP cost: +50% outgoing dmg 5t via
   outgoing_damage_mults).
   New infrastructure: damage-taken hook for page accumulation, DodgeWindowEffect,
   TraumaResponseEffect (outgoing_damage_mults).

CATEGORY B — SINGLE ABILITY-GRANTING ITEMS (6 items)

5. Crackhead Rocket Shoes [Feet, +30 energy/tick]
   Ability: Rocket Blast — SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS (range 8): teleport adjacent to target, deal
   STR*2+6 collision dmg + stun 2t. 10% misfire: blast backward 3 tiles for STR self-dmg.
   12-turn cooldown.

6. Deadman's Gloves [Weapon/beating, 3 power, +1 CON]
   Ability: Dead Man's Grip — ADJACENT: execute enemy at <=25% HP, absorb their current HP
   as temp HP (cap 30/total 60). 8-turn cooldown. Requires Beating weapon.

7. The Borrowed Watch [Ring, +2 STS]
   Ability: Time Steal — SELF: drain 30 energy/tick from nearest enemy, add 30 to player
   for 6 turns. On expire: player energy halved. 20-turn cooldown.
   New infrastructure: ring grants_ability support, TimeStealActiveEffect (tick drains enemy,
   expire halves player energy).

8. Prophet's Jawbone [Weapon/beating, 5 power, +2 SWG]
   Ability: Speak The Word — ADJACENT_TILE direction: blast line 8t, all enemies in ray
   knocked back 2 tiles + deafened (sight_radius=0, forced WANDERING) for 6 turns.
   18-turn cooldown.
   New infrastructure: DeafenedEffect (stores/restores sight_radius).

9. Plague Doctor Mask [Hat, 10 armor, +3 TOL, -1 FOV]
   Ability: Miasma Cloud — SELF: apply miasma to 9 surrounding tiles (player + 8 adjacent),
   enemies in range gain 5 toxicity/tick for 4 turns. 15-turn cooldown.
   Meth Lab synergy: toxicity multiplies enemy incoming damage.
   New infrastructure: hat grants_ability support, MiasmaInfectedEffect, miasma spray visual.

10. Bail Bondsman's Briefcase [Weapon/beating, 4 power, 2 defense, +1 BKS/+1 SWG]
    Ability: The Bond — SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS, 1/floor: mark a BOLO target. Any damage dealt to
    any OTHER enemy sends 30% echo damage to the BOLO target. Lasts the floor.
    New infrastructure: bolo_target_id engine state, combat.py echo hook, BoloMarkEffect,
    floor-reset clearing of bolo state.

SHARED INFRASTRUCTURE ADDITIONS

  engine.py: kill hook (rep/entries), damage-taken hook (pages), floor reset (BOLO)
  combat.py: BOLO echo in _deal_damage
  effects.py: 9 new Effect subclasses
  engine state: 7 new state variables
  abilities.py: 13 new AbilityDef entries

DESIGN PRINCIPLES APPLIED
  - All resource costs create meaningful spend-vs-hoard decisions
  - All abilities reference real existing systems (soul_count pattern, existing effects,
    existing knockback loop, is_summon, outgoing_damage_mults)
  - Risk/reward present in every item (misfire, HP cost, energy halved on expire, etc.)
  - Thematic urban flavor throughout (corner royalty, blunt trauma, plague street medicine)
  - No new graphical tiles required — reuse 0xE00D/0xE00A/0xE00B with unique colors
