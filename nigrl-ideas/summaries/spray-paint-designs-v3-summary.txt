SUMMARY: spray-paint-designs-v3.txt
=====================================
5 replacement spray paint colors (replacing rejected V2 Yellow/Purple/Pink/Brown/White).
Full doc: nigrl-ideas/spray-paint-designs-v3.txt

PAINT ROSTER (V3 replacements, numbered as 11-15 in slot sequence):
  11 Crimson  (210,35,65)     Player melee: heal 15+5*GrafLvl% of damage dealt. Leech tile.
  12 Copper   (195,115,45)    Player: +20+5*GrafLvl energy/tick. Enemy: -(15+5*GrafLvl) energy/tick.
  13 Cobalt   (35,75,210)     Player: +(3+GrafLvl) spell damage. Ability refund 50% (vs Blue's 25%).
  14 Violet   (150,45,195)    Player: +(3+GrafLvl) melee dmg. Risk: adjacent enemies +3 dmg back. 8 charges.
  15 Rust     (170,75,30)     Enemy on tile: -1 defense/turn (stacks to -10, floor_duration). Player immune.

KEY DESIGN PATTERNS:
  - Crimson = vampiric healing (inline in melee hit path; no new Effect)
  - Copper = energy-system manipulation (added in energy distribution phase; no new Effect)
  - Cobalt = spell build tile (tile_spell_damage_bonus PlayerStats field + refund extends Blue check)
  - Violet = explicit risk/reward (player +dmg, adjacent enemies +3 back; 8 charges only)
  - Rust = stacking armor erosion (new RustedEffect: floor_duration=True, stacks field, max 10)

NEW EFFECT:
  RustedEffect (id="rusted", floor_duration=True, stacks max 10)
  — pure defense-reduction holder; read in combat.py effective_defense calc

NEW PLAYERSTATS FIELDS:
  tile_spell_damage_bonus: int = 0  (cobalt; reset on step-off)
  tile_melee_damage_bonus: int = 0  (violet; optional, can be inline instead)
