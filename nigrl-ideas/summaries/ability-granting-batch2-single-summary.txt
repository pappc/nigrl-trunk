ITEM ROSTER
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. THE HOODOO NEEDLE [Weapon/stabbing, base_dmg=5, str_scaling /3]
   Grants: Hoodoo Pin (SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS, range 6, cooldown 20)
   Effect: "hoodoo_proxy" (dur=12, floor_duration). While active: every hit player TAKES
   echoes as equal damage on the pinned enemy (mirror damage, ignores defense).
   Tradeoff: you must tank hits to deal mirror damage. Weak raw stats.
   New state: engine.hoodoo_proxy_target_id (str|None).
   New effect: HoodooProxyEffect; mirror trigger in engine.py player-damage path.

2. LAST RITES CLEAVER [Weapon/slashing, base_dmg=9, str_scaling /1 above 8]
   Grants: Last Rites (SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS, range 5, cooldown 18 clean / 30 on miss)
   Effect: Leap to target, deal 2.5x damage. TARGET SURVIVES: take self_harm=dmg//2,
   apply -2 STR for 4t, cooldown 30. TARGET DIES: clean kill, cooldown 18.
   Tradeoff: enormous damage but catastrophic punishment for not finishing the kill.
   Uses existing stat_mod effect. No new effects needed.

3. PAROLE OFFICER'S NOTEPAD [Neck, +10 armor, +1 BKS]
   Grants: Mandatory Check-In (SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS, PER_FLOOR 1 charge)
   Effect: Mark one enemy as parolee; it enters passive_until_hit behavior. Compliance
   check: if parolee comes within 2 tiles it's stunned 1 turn. On parolee death: +$25.
   New state: engine.parolee_id. New effect: ParoleeEffect (saves/restores ai_type).
   Cash payout in kill handler.

4. THE THIN BLUE LINE RING [Ring, +2 defense, +1 SWG]
   Grants: Officer Down (SELF, cooldown 22)
   Effect: AOE confuse all enemies within 3 tiles (4 turns). Summon "Phantom Cop" entity
   (speed=130, hp=20, power=8, is_summon=False, ai_type="meander") for 8 turns — will
   attack nearest entity including player if no enemies in range.
   Tradeoff: indiscriminate phantom cop; may turn on you.
   Reuses existing ConfuseEffect. Phantom cop spawned inline.

5. DEAD LETTERS CHAIN [Neck, +5 armor, +1 TOL]
   Grants: Return to Sender (SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS, cooldown 15, costs 8 HP)
   Effect: Copy ALL player's active debuffs (whitelist: dot/bleeding/ignite/slow/confuse/
   child_support/mogged/rabies/stat_mod) onto the target with remaining duration.
   Tradeoff: only valuable when YOU are suffering debuffs. 8 HP cost always applies.
   No new effects needed — uses whitelist of existing effect ids.

6. GAS STATION SHADES [Hat, +2 STS]
   Grants: Read the Room (SELF, cooldown 25)
   Effect: "read_the_room_active" (dur=8). ALL attacks guaranteed crit for 8 turns.
   While active: -3 FOV tiles. FOV restored on expire.
   Tradeoff: massive DPS window but fighting nearly blind (5-7 tile vision).
   New effect: ReadTheRoomEffect (modify engine._read_the_room_fov_penalty, fov recompute).
   Integration: combat.py + gun_system.py force is_crit=True if effect active.

7. THE LOTTO SCRATCHERS [Ring, +1 TOL]
   Grants: Lucky Numbers (SELF, PER_FLOOR 3 charges)
   Weighted RNG table (100 pts): JACKPOT 15% (full heal +$30), SMALL WIN 35% (heal 10%
   + HoT 10t), SCRATCH FEVER 25% (+3 random stat 10t), LOSING TICKET 15% (-2 STR/STS 8t),
   ALREADY A WINNER 10% (cash drop + dot 3/turn 5t).
   Tradeoff: pure RNG; worst case 3 LOSING TICKETs in a row. No cooldown, charges limited.
   Uses existing StatModEffect, DotEffect, HotEffect.

8. STASH HOUSE SNEAKERS [Feet, +10 energy/tick, +1 STS]
   Grants: Ghost Stash (SELF, INFINITE, no cooldown)
   Effect: Two-mode ability. STORE (out of combat): pick a consumable from inventory,
   lock it in the heel. DEPLOY (in combat): use stored item as FREE ACTION (no turn cost).
   Stash state persists across floors. On unequip: stored item drops on floor.
   Tradeoff: requires pre-planning before fights. High skill ceiling.
   New state: engine.stash_sneakers_item_id, engine.stash_sneakers_pending_store.

9. THE UNDERTAKER'S BELT [Neck, +8 armor, +2 defense, +1 CON]
   Grants: Last Wishes (SELF, PER_FLOOR 1 charge, is_passive=True — fires automatically)
   Effect: PASSIVE DEATH SAVE. When player would reach 0 HP: survive at 1 HP, all enemies
   in Chebyshev 2 take overkill dmg, +15 temp HP, 1 turn invulnerable.
   Tradeoff: once/floor. Fires automatically, so second near-death kill you.
   New AbilityDef field: is_passive=True (UI shows "(passive)", no targeting mode).
   Integration: engine.py player-lethal-damage check before death event fires.

10. CORONER'S BADGE [Hat, +2 BKS]
    Grants: Cause of Death (SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS, cooldown 10)
    Effect: Analyze target. Apply "cause_of_death_mark" (dur=8): 20 + BKS//4 % bonus
    damage on all attacks against that target (via modify_incoming_damage amplification).
    One study active at a time; new target clears old mark. Range: full FOV.
    At BKS 8: +22% dmg. At BKS 12: +23% dmg. Low variance, high consistency.
    New state: engine.cod_study_target_id. New effect: CauseOfDeathMarkEffect.

11. THE RAT KING'S SCEPTER [Weapon/beating, base_dmg=7, +2 SWG, -1 STS]
    Grants: Swarm Call (SELF, PER_FLOOR 2 charges)
    Effect: Summon 3 Scurry Rats (speed=150, hp=5, power=4, is_summon=True, lifetime=25).
    On rat death: spawn "blood_patch" hazard (hazard_type="blood_patch", dur=10) that
    applies bleeding (2t, 2dmg) to any enemy standing on it.
    Tradeoff: -1 STS passive cost. Rats are fragile. Real value in blood patch zoning.
    New enemy template: "scurry_rat" (spawn_weight=0 — never spawns naturally).
    New hazard: "blood_patch" (char='.', red, tick in energy loop).

12. THE WARRANT [Gun/sidearm, base_dmg=14-20, mag=2, medium ammo]
    Grants: Served (SINGLE_ENEMY_LOS, cooldown 12)
    Effect: Mark enemy as "wanted" (WantedEffect, floor_duration). NEXT HIT on that target:
    2x damage, defense completely ignored. KILL SHOT: recover 2 ammo. Misses don't consume.
    One wanted target per floor. Cooldown applies from cast, not hit.
    Synergy peak: Sniping L3 mega-crit on a wanted shot = raw_dmg × 2 (wanted) × 4 (mega) = 8x.
    New state: engine.warrant_target_id. New effect: WantedEffect (floor_duration marker).
    Integration: gun_system.py post-hit check for wanted effect + defense bypass.
