----------------------------------------------------------------------
Six perk options for Decontamination L3-L6, replacing "Fallout" placeholder.

RECOMMENDED ARC:
  L3 — Consecrate (activated, 2/floor): spend 50 rad to place 3x3 irradiated
       ground zone (20t). Enemies on tiles gain +20 rad/turn. New dungeon system:
       dungeon.rad_tiles dict (mirrors grease_tiles). Rendered as "+" yellow-green.
       Alt: Purge Surge (passive): every 50 rad lost -> next melee hit +N dmg (cap 30);
       above 100 rad: melee hits apply 10 rad to target.
  L4 — Half-Life Aura (activated, 3/floor, 15t): enemies within 2 tiles gain +15
       rad/turn; you self-drain 3 rad/turn (fires Force Sensitive). Implemented as
       HalfLifeAuraEffect subclass.
  L5 — Sponge (activated, 2/floor, radius 3 AOE): absorb 50% of each irradiated
       enemy's rad, gain half yourself. If total pushes you above 150 rad: Rad Pulse
       (rad//10 dmg, radius 2). Requires enemies be irradiated first.
  L6 — Radiant Wake (passive): while above 75 rad, every tile left behind becomes
       rad tile (6t, +10 rad/turn). +2 STR, +10% rad resist.

CORE LOOP (full build):
  Build rad -> Emission (share to enemies) -> Half-Life Aura (grow their rad,
  drain yours) -> Consecrate (place zones, fires Force Sensitive) -> Sponge
  (harvest enemy rad, Rad Pulse) -> Skywalker OG (purge for Force Sensitive STR)
  -> Walk -> Radiant Wake trails irradiate pursuers.

IDENTITY: mobile zone-denial skirmisher; rad is a resource to cycle in/out, not
just build or spend. Distinct from White Power (tank), Nuclear Research (placed
explosives), Chemical Warfare (tox scaling burst).

NEW SYSTEMS:
  - dungeon.rad_tiles: dict[tuple, int] — (x,y) -> turns_remaining
  - Render: "+" in yellow-green (160,255,80)/(25,40,10) visible; dimmed when explored
  - HalfLifeAuraEffect(Effect) in effects.py
  - Engine state: purge_surge_accumulated, purge_surge_pending_dmg (if Purge Surge)
  - handle_move: 4-line Radiant Wake check (skill level >= 6, rad >= 75)

KEY SYNERGIES: Force Sensitive (remove_radiation fires on_rad_lost), Emission
(sets up Sponge), Skywalker OG (primary rad cycling tool).
