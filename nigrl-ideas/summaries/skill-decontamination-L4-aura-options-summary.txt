SUMMARY: skill-decontamination-L4-aura-options.txt
==================================================

6 toggle aura options for Decontamination L4 (replaces _PLACEHOLDER at SKILL_PERKS index 3).
Every even-numbered Decon perk = toggle aura (swap with Gamma Aura; one active at a time).

OPTION 1 — Sacred Ground Aura
  Aura: rad tiles in FOV within 5 tiles gain +2 turns/tick (cap 30t). Walking has 15% chance
  to spontaneously create a new rad tile (8t).
  Passive: stepping off a rad tile adds +4 turns to it (Holy Footprint).
  Requires Consecrate for most value. Vibe: zone-sustain paladin.

OPTION 2 — Divine Sight Aura
  Aura: visible enemies gain 1 Condemned stack/tick (cap 20). When enemy LEAVES FOV:
  burst for stacks*3 true damage, stacks reset.
  Passive: each visible enemy with 5+ stacks = +1 rad resist (cap +3).
  Uses FOV as a novel combat mechanic — unprecedented in NIGRL. Vibe: punishes fleeing enemies.

OPTION 3 — Corona of Judgment
  Aura (costs 15 rad to activate): when a VISIBLE enemy hits you, deal (10+rad//20) true damage
  to them AND gain 8 rad. Toggle cost = 15 rad (free to deactivate).
  Passive (Radiant Constitution): while your rad is 50-200, take 1 less damage from all sources.
  Force Sensitive synergy: every hit loads your rad pool. Vibe: immovable paladin.

OPTION 4 — Penitent Aura
  Aura: emit (3 + (1-HP%)*17) rad/tick to enemies within 3 tiles. Full HP = 3/tick (weak).
  Near-death = up to 20/tick (strongest aura in game).
  Passive (Martyrdom): once per floor, survive a lethal hit at 1 HP; gain 50 rad instantly.
  Most exciting passive in the doc. Vibe: blazes brightest at the brink of death.

OPTION 5 — Absolution Aura
  Aura: enemies within 3 tiles at 100+ rad are cleansed: lose half their rad, take (stripped//4)
  true damage; player heals (stripped//8) HP.
  Passive: +25% Decontamination XP from all sources (engine.decon_xp_mult = 1.25).
  Completion of Gamma->Absolution->Gamma loop. Vibe: healer-purifier.

OPTION 6 — Ironsoul Aura
  Aura: gain 1 flat DR per enemy in FOV (cap 5 DR). Each hit from a visible enemy grants 10 rad.
  Passive (Line of Sight Awareness): FOV radius +1. New enemies entering sight are announced.
  New: sight_radius_bonus passive (no other perk extends FOV). Vibe: awareness-as-armor.

AUTHOR RECOMMENDATION:
  Corona of Judgment (3) — tightest Force Sensitive synergy, creates first cost-to-purging
  tension in the game, new combat hook.
  Divine Sight (2) — most unique to NIGRL specifically; uses FOV in completely new way.
  Penitent (4) — most exciting perk text; Martyrdom is memorable roguelike design.

NEW FIELDS NEEDED BY OPTION:
  Option 2: entity.condemned_stacks: int = 0 (must serialize)
  Option 4: engine.martyrdom_used_this_floor: bool = False (reset on floor transition)
  Option 5: engine.decon_xp_mult: float = 1.0 (set to 1.25 on unlock)
  Option 6: engine.first_seen_entity_ids: set = set() (reset on floor transition)
  Option 6: player_stats.sight_radius_bonus += 1 (or inline in compute_fov)
