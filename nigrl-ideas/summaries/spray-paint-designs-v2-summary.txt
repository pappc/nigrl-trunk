10 new spray paint colors (Paints 6-15). Full doc: nigrl-ideas/spray-paint-designs-v2.txt

PAINT ROSTER (new):
  6  Yellow     (230,220,40)   Player: +3 STS +3 BKS simultaneously. Hybrid stat tile.
  7  Purple     (160,40,210)   Enemy on tile: 30% per turn -> confused (1t, random move).
  8  Pink       (255,100,160)  Player: +15 max HP while standing (non-healing).
  9  Gold       (255,190,30)   Kill enemy on tile: +$5 cash drop. Economy tile. 8 charges.
  10 Teal       (40,200,185)   Player: +20% skill XP from all sources while standing.
  11 Brown      (120,75,20)    Enemy on tile: +50 move cost (33% slower movement, no skip).
  12 Lime       (100,240,50)   All entities: enemy +6+2*GrafLvl tox/turn, player +3 tox/turn.
  13 Grey       (160,160,160)  Enemy on tile: -(1+GrafLvl) to their outgoing melee damage.
  14 Rad        (180,255,60)   All entities: enemy +10+2*GrafLvl rad/turn, player +6 rad/turn. Meth Lab.
  15 White      (245,245,245)  Player: sense enemy direction within (2+GrafLvl) tiles per turn.

KEY DESIGN PATTERNS:
  - Lime/Rad = Meth Lab tox/rad delivery (enemy-focused; self-damage risk)
  - Grey = attacker-tile modifier (new in combat.py; attacker.tile -> reduce dmg dealt)
  - Gold = economy kill reward (entity_died event bus, gold tile check)
  - White = pure information (no combat math; directional sensing only)
  - Pink = tile_max_hp_bonus (new PlayerStats field; clamp HP on step-off)
  - Teal = tile_xp_bonus (new PlayerStats field; multiplies all skill XP gains)
  - Brown = move-cost modifier (inline in monster move-cost block, no new Effect)
  - Purple = new ConfusedPaintEffect (id="confused_paint", dur=1, random move before_turn)

NEW PLAYERSTATS FIELDS:
  tile_max_hp_bonus: int = 0
  tile_xp_bonus: float = 0.0

NEW EFFECT:
  confused_paint (debuff, dur=1, before_turn: random walkable adjacent move)
