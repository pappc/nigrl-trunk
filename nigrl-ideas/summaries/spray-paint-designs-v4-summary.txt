SPRAY PAINT DESIGNS V4 — SUMMARY
=================================
Source: nigrl-ideas/spray-paint-designs-v4.txt
10 new paints (Paints 16-25). Total roster now 25.

PAINT QUICK REFERENCE:

16. Midnight (25,20,90)
    Player: +(10+5*Graffiti)% dodge. Total dodge capped at 60%.
    New field: tile_dodge_bonus: float = 0.0 on PlayerStats.
    Implementation: _update_tile_stat_bonuses; dodge check in combat.py.
    10 charges | Value 40 | both zones

17. Magenta (240,40,160)
    Player: +(8+2*Graffiti) Temp HP per kill (any damage source).
    Hook: kill event handler (alongside gold/graffiti-heal checks).
    No new fields. Temp HP capped at max_hp.
    10 charges | Value 40 | both zones

18. Navy (20,55,140)
    Player: +(2+Graffiti) gun damage/shot. 25% ammo-not-consumed per shot.
    Implementation: gun_system.py damage calc + ammo decrement path.
    No new fields needed.
    10 charges | Value 45 | both zones

19. Amber (245,190,20) [adjusted from 255,175,0 for distinctiveness]
    Enemy: if HP <= (45+5*Graffiti)% max HP, 50% fear check/turn (2t).
    Conditional fear CC — only active below bloodied threshold.
    Implementation: per-tick entity loop; reuses "feared" effect.
    10 charges | Value 35 | both zones

20. Bone (235,220,185)
    Player: damage reduction = (missing HP ratio) * (20+5*Graffiti) %.
    Reactive DR — 0 bonus at full HP, up to 35% at very low HP.
    Implementation: incoming-damage-to-player branch in combat.py.
    No new fields.
    10 charges | Value 35 | both zones

21. Slate (90,110,135)
    Player: Swagger defense doubled (mult = 2 + graffiti//2 * 0.5).
    Build-gated: useless at SWG 8, strong at SWG 12+.
    New field: tile_swagger_mult: float = 1.0 on PlayerStats.
    Implementation: _update_tile_stat_bonuses; swagger_def calc.
    10 charges | Value 40 | both zones

22. Chartreuse (170,255,40)
    Player: (10+5*Graffiti)% chance per XP event to gain +1 free SP.
    Bypasses potential/real XP conversion — direct SP spike.
    Implementation: hook in xp_progression.py at gain_potential_exp.
    No new fields.
    10 charges | Value 40 | both zones

23. Umber (110,50,15)
    Player: Grit = nearby enemy count (Chebyshev 3). 2=+1STR, 3=+2STR+1DEF, 4+=+3STR+2DEF.
    Group-scaling offense — stronger when more surrounded.
    Implementation: _update_tile_stat_bonuses counts living enemies in radius.
    No new fields (uses tile_stat_bonuses dict).
    10 charges | Value 35 | both zones

24. Tawny (210,150,60) [adjusted from 195,130,40 for distinctiveness vs Copper]
    Meth Lab: +1 Meth resource per action. Crack Den fallback: +(2+Graffiti) TOL.
    Zone-dependent dual behavior.
    Implementation: post-action hook (ML) / _update_tile_stat_bonuses (CD).
    No new fields.
    10 charges | Value 45 | meth_lab primary, crack_den

25. Indigo (65,25,180)
    Player: auto-stun 1 random adjacent enemy each player turn (1-2t).
    At Graffiti L2+: radius extends to 2 tiles. L3+: stun = 2 turns.
    Implementation: player-turn-start hook; reuses "stunned" effect.
    No new fields. 8 charges only.
    8 charges | Value 50 | both zones
