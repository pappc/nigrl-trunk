BRAINSTORM SUMMARY: PENTHOUSE 10TH NPC
Source: nigrl-ideas/brainstorm-penthouse-npc-tenth.txt
Date: 2026-04-02

6 concepts for the 10th and final one-time service NPC in Tyrone's Penthouse.
All concepts avoid overlapping the existing 9 NPCs (Gun Dealer, Chef, Plug, La'Quisha,
Precious, Rico, Soloman, Sage/Mixologist, Dice/Craps Dealer).

RANKED RECOMMENDATIONS:

1. DARNELL "The Memory Man" (A+) — RECOMMENDED
   Tallies three Crack Den metrics (kills, items used, cash spent), displays counts,
   player picks ONE to cash in for a reward. Other two tallies are wiped.
   Kill tally → max HP + ability charges. Item tally → random permanent stat points.
   Cash tally → permanent defense.
   Weird factor: high. Mechanically original. Retroactively rewards playstyle.
   Engine state: 3 counters tracked from run start + darnell_used bool.

2. SANDMAN "The Dream Broker" (A)
   Voluntary sleep: pay ability charges (blind — which ability unknown until after),
   receive temp HP + cleanse. Light nap (1 charge, 1 debuff cleanse, +20 temp HP) or
   Heavy sleep (all charges from 1 ability, all debuffs cleansed, +30 HP, +40 temp HP).
   Weird factor: high (blind cost revealed AFTER you commit).

3. LOLA "The Sprint Coach" (B+)
   Pick one of 3 permanent movement techniques:
   Burst Step (free double-tile move once per floor), Shadow Step (idle enemies
   don't notice you 1 tick longer), Iron Stride (immune to all movement slowing effects).
   Cleanly fills the movement/speed design space. Orthogonal to all 9 existing NPCs.

4. BIG PETE "The Contractor" (B+)
   Bounty contract: player picks a Meth Lab enemy type to hunt; Pete gives a deferred
   reward (random from HP/temp HP/defense/speed/ability charges) for achieving 3-7 kills
   of that type. One re-roll on contract terms allowed.
   Implementation is heaviest of the 6.

5. NEEDLE "The Body Mod Artist" (B)
   Pick one of 5 permanent defensive/offensive body modifications:
   Subdermal Plates (+20 def), Bone Bracing (-2 melee dmg taken per hit),
   Nerve Deadening (DOT ticks -1, min 1), Hollow Knuckles (+1 melee dmg, +5% crit),
   Scar Tissue (first hit per combat -50%).
   Clean mechanic filling armor/DR space.

6. MAMA EZRA "The Medium" (C)
   Cryptic psychic reading of one room on Meth Lab floor 1 (real data, abstract
   language). Optional compass marker toward that room. Pure information, thin payoff.
   Suggested as flavor text attached to a different NPC rather than a standalone slot.
