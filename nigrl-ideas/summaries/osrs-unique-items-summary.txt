================================================================================
OSRS UNIQUE ITEMS — SUMMARY
Source: nigrl-ideas/osrs-unique-items.txt
================================================================================

25 unique equippable items. Design lens: Old School RuneScape's special attacks,
degradation, set effects, slayer bonuses, and niche zone-specific weapons.
Every item references a specific OSRS counterpart and translates its philosophy
to NIGRL's urban street setting.

SLOT BREAKDOWN:
  Weapons stabbing (4): Dragon Double Dagger, Smite Shank, Abyssal Dagger of Debt, Ghrazi Stiletto
  Weapons slashing (2): Armadillo Godsword, Scythe of Vitur
  Weapons beating (5):  Granite Jawn, Barrows Beater, Verac's Veil, Toxic Staff Dead, Elder Maul
  Sidearms guns (2):    Craw's Web Shooter, Toxic Blowpipe
  Weapon guns (1):      Twisted Bow of the Block
  Rings (3):            Berserker Coil, Ring of the Witness, Wilderness Chrome
  Neck (3):             Void Chain, Blowpipe Bullet Chain, Spectral Shield Chain
  Feet (2):             Void Kicks, Bandos Kicks
  Hats (3):             Void Hood, Seer's Spectacles, Slayer Helm of the Snitch

KEY NEW MECHANICS INTRODUCED:
  - Spec energy system (engine.spec_energy: float; +25/turn; 100 max; 6 spec weapons draw from it)
  - Void set system (3-piece: +10% outgoing damage total; count-based outgoing_damage_mults)
  - Dharok's HP-inverse scalar (damage bonus = int((1 - hp_ratio) * 20); passive field)
  - Zone-specific gun passive (Craw's: bonus only in crack_den zone)
  - Attack cost override (Elder Maul: 150 energy instead of 100 per attack)
  - stat_req above player base max (Elder Maul: str_req 15; needs ring STR to equip)
  - Slayer task system (random enemy_type assigned per floor; engine.snitch_task_enemy_type)
  - Skull ring activation (3-kill threshold; mult added; reset on unequip)
  - Ignore dodge field (ignore_dodge: True on item defn; skip dodge roll in combat.py)
  - Per-entity ring charge resource (witness_charges; decrement on hit; recharge per floor)
  - Smite energy drain (smite_drain: True; reduce target.energy by damage//2 per hit)
  - Vitur sweep (vitur_sweep: True; side-tiles hit for 50% of primary damage)
  - Tox-scaling gun damage (tox_scaling_gun: dict; bonus_per_100_tox applied in gun_system)
  - Gun tox-on-hit (on_gun_hit_tox: int — new field, applied after confirmed gun hit)
  - Reduced gun energy cost field (reduced_energy_cost_gun: int)
  - One-hit kill heal trigger (Bandos Kicks: detect fresh-HP kill, heal 10)
  - Spectral passive decay (50% chance per turn: -2 tox AND -2 rad independently)
  - Berserker post-kill STR stacking (BerserkerMadnessEffect, max +8 cap)
  - Seers spell-kill BKS stacking (SeersBksKillEffect, max +3 cap)
  - Calamity Shield tox immunity + tox aura (ToxStaffCalamityEffect, 5 turns)
  - Free action attack (Granite Jawn: player_free_action = True; granite_free_used_this_turn flag)

NEW ENGINE/ENTITY STATE:
  engine.spec_energy: float = 100.0
  engine.granite_free_used_this_turn: bool
  engine.berserker_coil_bonus: int
  engine.calamity_shield_active: bool
  engine.snitch_task_enemy_type: str | None
  engine.skull_kill_count: int
  engine.skull_ring_active: bool
  engine.skull_ring_equipped: bool
  engine.seers_bks_kill_stacks: int
  engine.void_item_count: int
  entity.witness_charges: int = 50  (ring entity)
  entity.witness_depleted: bool = False  (ring entity)

NEW EFFECTS REQUIRED:
  BerserkerMadnessEffect (id="berserker_madness"; dur=3; +4 STR on apply; cap 8 total)
  SeersBksKillEffect (id="seers_bks_kill"; dur=8; +1 BKS on apply; cap +3)
  ToxStaffCalamityEffect (id="calamity_shield"; dur=5; tox immunity + tox aura)
