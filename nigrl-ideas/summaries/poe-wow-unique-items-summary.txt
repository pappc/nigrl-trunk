================================================================================
POE/WOW UNIQUE ITEMS — SUMMARY
Source: nigrl-ideas/poe-wow-unique-items.txt
================================================================================

25 unique equippable items. Design lens: PoE rule-breaking uniques with real
"Cannot do X" tradeoffs + WoW proc/stacking legendaries. Every item has a
build-defining mechanic that does not duplicate any existing unique.

SLOT BREAKDOWN:
  Weapons melee (5): 2 stabbing, 2 beating, 1 slashing
  Weapons guns (1):  1 large gun (weapon slot)
  Sidearms (3):      3 small guns
  Rings (7):         diverse builds (energy, charges, cleave, cash, DoT, speed, procs)
  Neck (4):          tox offense, melee-disable, dodge retaliation, grudge burst
  Feet (2):          tox dodge, HP-threshold speed swap
  Hats (3):          random debuff tank, kill-dodge charges, gun-crit amplifier

QUICK REFERENCE:
  glass_cannon         Weapon (stabbing)  Forces armor=0; bonus damage = suppressed armor / 4
  thinkin_mans_heater  Weapon (beating)   BKS-only scaling; crit = +1 temp BKS (max 4 stacks)
  deadweight_shank     Weapon (stabbing)  +3 dmg/kill (max +15); ALL stacks expire after 10 idle turns
  knucklehead          Weapon (beating)   15% Dazed proc; 3 stacks on same target = Haymaker (3x dmg)
  manifesto_machete    Weapon (slashing)  TOL scaling (+2/TOL); +10 tox/hit; tox-kill → perm tox resist
  decimators_oath      Weapon (large gun) d4 table per shot: 2x/normal/half+Shocked/miss+Fear
  last_resort          Sidearm            Deals min damage above 35% HP; 3x defense-ignoring below
  corner_store_chrome  Sidearm            base_damage = 1 + player.armor (current value)
  clean_sweep          Sidearm            Bullet chains to up to 3 more adjacent targets (-20% each)
  borrowed_time_ring   Ring               5 HP regen/turn; energy ONLY from kills
  accountability_mirror Ring              30hp budget/floor: absorb incoming damage → ability charges
  static_discharge_ring Ring              Melee crits arc lightning to 2 adjacents + self-shocked
  street_tax_ring      Ring               Cash wallet → +1 dmg/10$; 2x kill cash
  parasite_ring        Ring               DoT ticks also heal 50% of damage dealt
  stash_house_ring     Ring               +3 TOL/BKS/STS + 2x cash; -20 energy/tick
  vicars_signet        Ring               25% on ability use: refund charge + free random secondary
  preachers_belt       Neck               +1 melee/20 tox; tox thorns on hit; -20 tox resist
  cowards_coat         Neck               Melee DISABLED; +80 armor +8 def +30% tox/rad resist
  snitches_get_stitches Neck              Any dodge triggers free 150% power counter (once/turn)
  grudge_beads         Neck               Incoming damage stored; next attack spends (min(30, grudge/2))
  meth_lab_overalls    Feet               dodge_chance += min(40, tox // 5); +20 tox resist
  backpedal_boots      Feet               -10 energy/tick above 40% HP; +60 below 40% HP
  the_empties          Hat                +3 TOL -3 SWG; 30% DR + random debuff when hit for 10+
  prophets_poncho      Hat                Kill → Prophecy charge (max 3); charge → guaranteed melee dodge
  hollow_point_hat     Hat                Gun crits 3x dmg; melee crits 1.5x; +3 STS

KEY NEW MECHANICS INTRODUCED:
  - Armor forced to 0 by equipped weapon (glass_cannon_equipped flag)
  - Energy ONLY from kills — no passive accumulation (borrowed_time_equipped)
  - Execute gun: useless above threshold; defense-ignoring burst below
  - Damage = current armor (not base_damage range) for gun
  - Chain bullet arc (up to 4 targets, -20%/hop) in gun system
  - d4 chaos table per gun shot (each roll has distinct outcome)
  - Cash wallet as direct melee damage scalar
  - DoT-healing conversion (Parasite: DoTs heal 50% of tick damage)
  - Melee-disabled "Cannot" with massive compensation (Coward's Coat)
  - Tox-to-dodge scaling (min(40, tox//5) dodge bonus)
  - HP-threshold energy swap (Backpedal: slow above 40%, fast below)
  - Kill-generated dodge charge (persist, decay on empty floors)
  - Grudge accumulation (stored incoming damage → burst next attack)
  - Tox thorns on hit (enemy gets tox when it attacks you)
  - Dazed stacking on same target → Haymaker free attack
  - Ability proc chain (25% chance: refund + random second ability free)
  - Gun crit multiplier override per hat (gun 3x, melee 1.5x)
  - BKS crit feedback loop (crit → +1 temp BKS → bigger next crit damage)
  - TOL as primary melee scaling stat (Manifesto Machete)
  - Permanent tox resistance earned via tox kills (Manifesto)
