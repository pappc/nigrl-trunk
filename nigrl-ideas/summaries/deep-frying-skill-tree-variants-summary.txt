DEEP-FRYING SKILL TREE VARIANTS — SUMMARY
File: nigrl-ideas/deep-frying-skill-tree-variants.txt
================================================================================

10 complete L1-L10 Deep-Frying skill tree designs. All keep L1 (Fry Shot) and
L2 (Extra Greasy) as fixed points. Each has distinct playstyle and capstone.

FIXED PERKS (all trees):
  L1 — Fry Shot (activated): CON+2-DEF dmg, 3 Greasy stacks, 15t CD. [EXISTS]
  L2 — Extra Greasy (passive): Fried food restores +20% HP. [EXISTS]

TREE OVERVIEW
-------------

TREE 1 — "THE GREASE FIRE SPECIALIST" (Pure Burst DPS)
  Theme: Stack greasy fast → detonate via Grease Fire reaction.
  L3: Flash Fry (passive) — 30%/50% on-hit greasy proc
  L4: Splatter (activated) — 3x3 AOE greasy splash, CD 20t
  L5: Ignition Point (passive) — +CON/3 Grease Fire burst bonus
  L6: Burning Lard (activated) — 6-tile LINE, CON+TOL dmg + oil_slick trail
  L7: Grease Trap (activated) — 1/floor, mass greasy on all visible enemies
  L8: Full Render (passive) — Grease Fire: stacks*(2+CON/5) burst; half stacks survive
  L9: Thermite Baste (activated) — 3 total charges, 10 greasy + CON/3 ignite, ADJACENT
  L10: The Forbidden Deep Fry (ONCE) — floor-wide greasy+ignite all monsters, CON*5 TempHP, self-ignite

TREE 2 — "THE SKILLET GENERAL" (Control / Terrain Denial)
  Theme: Oil slick hazard placement, kiting, room geometry control.
  L3: Spill (activated) — 3/floor, ADJACENT_TILE oil_slick placement
  L4: Slick Floor (passive) — oil_slicks last 50% longer, slip proc on 3+ greasy
  L5: Deep Fry Zone (activated) — 2/floor, 5x5 oil_slick zone
  L6: Oil River (activated) — 7-tile line of oil, no damage, pure denial
  L7: Pork Rind Armor (activated) — CON*3 TempHP, attackers 40% get greasy
  L8: Quagmire (passive) — oil_slick now also slows, 5+ greasy = DR shred
  L9: Flash Flood (activated) — 1/floor, entire floor oil_slick for 8t
  L10: Grease Monolith (ONCE) — permanent floor oil, summoned Monolith with radius-3 greasy aura

TREE 3 — "THE DEEP FRYER FIST" (Melee Hybrid)
  Theme: Melee + greasy synergy; grease on player = armor; kills absorb grease.
  L3: Oily Fists (passive) — 100% on-hit greasy to enemy (2 stacks if self-greasy >= 3)
  L4: Grease Bath (activated) — 3 self-greasy + 10t STR/2 melee buff + on-kill heal
  L5: Tallow Skin (passive) — +1 armor per player Greasy stack (max +10)
  L6: Knuckle Crunch (activated) — CON+STR dmg, crit if target 3+ greasy → ignite
  L7: Fat Render (passive) — kills absorb half enemy greasy stacks + TempHP
  L8: Deep Fryer Stance (activated) — 3/floor, double greasy on self, -15 melee energy, kills spawn fire
  L9: Master Butcher (passive) — greasy-enemy kills reset Fry Shot CD; +20xp per stack
  L10: The Flesh Is The Fryer (ONCE) — 30t: every melee hit = 5 greasy+3 ignite on target; TempHP = stacks*CON

TREE 4 — "THE SLOW BURN" (DOT / Attrition)
  Theme: Persistent Ignite, Creeping Lard DOT, Greasy stacks never expire.
  L3: Rendered Fat (passive) — bonus dmg = ignite stacks when applying greasy
  L4: Low Simmer (passive) — Ignite +3t duration; greasy+ignite = bonus DOT per 3t
  L5: Infused Basting (activated) — 5 greasy+3 ignite; +1 ignite per hit for 5t
  L6: Dripping (passive) — 3 greasy stacks always survive Grease Fire
  L7: Keep It Warm (passive) — enemy greasy stacks never expire naturally
  L8: Creeping Lard (activated) — 2/floor, DOT starts 1dmg/t, +1 per 3 ticks, cap 20
  L9: Pressure Cooker (passive) — greasy+ignite+CL simultaneously = enemy DEF set to 0
  L10: Confit (ONCE) — 1 enemy: all DOTs tick twice/turn, perma-slow, death = distribute stacks

TREE 5 — "THE BIG-BONED BUILD" (Tank / Sustain)
  Theme: CON investment returns HP, armor, and healing from kills.
  L3: Lard Up (activated) — CON/3 self-greasy + CON/2 TempHP, CD 15t
  L4: Fat Tank (passive) — +1 armor per player Greasy stack
  L5: Grease Is The Word (passive) — kill Greasy enemy = heal 2+stacks+CON/4 HP
  L6: Pork Rind Armor (activated) — CON*4 TempHP, 50% attacker greasy proc, CD 20t
  L7: Blubber (passive) — big hits (>=10% max HP) give 1 player Greasy stack (3/turn)
  L8: Deep Fat Reserve (activated) — 2/floor, consume all player greasy: heal CON/3 per stack + DR
  L9: The Lard Is My Shepherd (passive) — +1 SWG/kill with own greasy, every 5 greasy-kills = CON heal
  L10: Grease Golem Form (ONCE) — 20t: greasy cap 20, 40% dmg reduction, infinite Fry Shot, expire = AOE

TREE 6 — "THE SPLATTER HOUSE" (AOE Specialist)
  Theme: Maximum coverage, multi-target Grease Fire chains, room clearing.
  L3: Splatter (activated) — 3x3 AOE greasy + dmg, CD 20t
  L4: Wide Splash (passive) — Fry Shot gains 3x3 splash (adjacent = CON-2 dmg + 1 greasy)
  L5: Napalm Drop (activated) — 2/floor, radius-2 AOE: raw CON dmg + 4 greasy + oil_slick
  L6: Chain Splatter (passive) — Grease Fire spreads 1 greasy to Chebyshev-2 neighbors; chains up to 4x
  L7: Pressure Burst (activated) — all floor Greasy enemies take CON/2 dmg; 5+ = 3 ignite stacks
  L8: Splatter Field (activated) — 1/floor, entire room: 3 greasy + 1 ignite all enemies
  L9: Deep Fryer Artillery (passive) — +1 charge to 3 abilities; kills with fryer = adjacent splash
  L10: The Big Cook (ONCE) — teleport all enemies within 5 tiles; CON*2 dmg + 5 greasy + 3 ignite each

TREE 7 — "THE FAT ASSASSIN" (Boss Killer / Single-Target)
  Theme: Maximum focus damage on one enemy; execute mechanics; Grease Fire guarantee.
  L3: Marked Meal (activated) — mark enemy: 2x greasy stack receive, +15% dmg taken, dur 20t
  L4: Crisped (passive) — 5+ greasy on enemy = double crit chance; crits add 2 ignite stacks
  L5: Deep Fry Focus (activated) — 2/floor, 10t: Fry Shot = CON*2-DEF dmg, 5 stacks
  L6: Baste and Broil (activated) — 4 greasy + CON dmg + 3 ignite in one action (guaranteed GF)
  L7: Over Done (passive) — <30% HP enemies take 2x dmg from frying abilities (execute)
  L8: Rendered Marrow (activated) — 4 total: 10 greasy + STR+CON unblocked dmg + reset Fry Shot CD
  L9: Connoisseur (passive) — cold-open Fry Shot = 3x dmg + 6 greasy; guaranteed GF maximum
  L10: The Chef's Kiss (ONCE) — 10 greasy + CON ignite (GF auto) + halve HP + 5t stun; unblockable

TREE 8 — "THE SPEED COOK" (Low Cooldowns / Combo Chains)
  Theme: Kill resets, rapid-fire abilities, momentum-based energy system.
  L3: Quick Shot (passive) — Fry Shot CD 10 (was 15); kills reset CD to 0; 2x XP
  L4: Hot Streak (passive) — 2 kills in 8t = Hot Streak; +5 speed/stack, max 5 stacks
  L5: Flash Pan (activated) — ADJACENT, CON/2 dmg, 2 greasy, CD 8t; kills = +20 energy
  L6: Turbo Baste (activated) — 5 rapid hits: CON/3 dmg + 1 greasy each; CD 20t
  L7: Chain Cook (passive) — consecutive frying abilities within 3t = +3% dmg/stack (max +9%)
  L8: Pressure Release (activated) — 3/floor: reset all frying CDs + +50 energy + 5t -30% energy cost
  L9: Adrenaline Grease (passive) — applying greasy to enemy = +5 player energy
  L10: Infinite Fryer (ONCE) — 15t: all frying CDs 0, no charges, no energy cost; +10 energy/hit

TREE 9 — "THE GREASE PIT PROFESSOR" (Debuff Specialist)
  Theme: Greasy stacks reduce enemy power, sight, actions, and healing.
  L3: Slip Hazard (passive) — 3+ greasy = 25% chance to slip each enemy turn
  L4: Oil Blind (activated) — enemy: halve sight_radius, +15% miss, block ALERTING; TOL/2 duration
  L5: Slippery Slope (passive) — -1 enemy attack power per Greasy stack on them
  L6: Dunk (activated) — ADJACENT; slow ratio=0.3 TOL turns + 4 greasy + blocks special attacks
  L7: Grease Leak (passive) — greasy enemies transfer 1 stack to adjacent enemy on move
  L8: Pressure Cook Them (activated) — 1/floor; debuffs +50% longer, -1 DEF per 3t (up to -6), 2x greasy stacks
  L9: Deep Fried Constitution (passive) — 8+ greasy = Saturated (no healing, -20% dmg)
  L10: Total Grease (ONCE) — 25t: all enemies 8 greasy/Saturated/-10 ATK; expire = all slip + CON dmg

TREE 10 — "THE CHEF OF FIRE" (Pyromancy Crossover)
  Theme: Greasy stacks count as Ignite ticks; Pyromania level boosts all mechanics.
  L3: Flammable (passive) — each Greasy stack = 0.5 ignite dmg/tick (1.0x if Pyro >= 3)
  L4: Grease Kindle (activated) — ADJACENT_TILE hazard: entity stepping on it = 3 greasy + 2 ignite (GF)
  L5: Inferno Drip (passive) — applying Ignite to Greasy enemy = bonus dmg = stacks*2 immediately
  L6: Deep Fire Harmony (passive) — GF deals +CON/2 bonus; Pyro 4+ = GF spreads 2 ignite to adjacent
  L7: Napalm Grease (passive) — Ignite on entity atop oil_slick = immediate GF (tile consumed)
  L8: Boiling Point (activated) — 2/floor, 10t: ignite stacks doubled, Flammable = 1.0x, +10 XP per GF
  L9: Eternal Flame (passive) — enemy Ignite duration = 999 (permanent until death/Wet)
  L10: The Burning Gospel (ONCE) — 10t: all enemies 5 greasy+5 ignite, fire immune, kills spawn fire tiles

  Confit effect (double DOT ticks) — Tree 4
