SPELL & ABILITY DAMAGE FORMULA REFERENCE
========================================
Last updated: 2026-03-30

Legend:
  BKS  = effective_book_smarts
  STS  = effective_street_smarts
  STR  = effective_strength
  CON  = effective_constitution
  TOL  = effective_tolerance
  SWG  = effective_swagger
  TSD  = total_spell_damage (permanent + temporary + equipment + WMB BKS bonus)
  CRY  = Cryomancy skill level
  ELEC = Electrodynamics skill level
  RAD  = player.radiation (capped at 200)
  DEF  = target.defense
  ATK  = player_attack_power (weapon + STR scaling)
  WMB  = Wizard Mind Bomb buff (adds BKS to TSD dynamically while active)

=== SOURCES OF total_spell_damage ===

Source              | Amount                    | Duration        | How applied
--------------------|---------------------------|-----------------|----------------------------
Arcane Intelligence | +1 per stack              | 20 turns        | temporary_spell_damage
Leftovers Well Fed  | +1 per stack              | varies          | temporary_spell_damage
Rune Scraper weapon | STR/2                     | while equipped  | equipment_spell_damage
Wizard Mind Bomb    | +BKS (dynamic)            | while buff up   | computed in TSD property
(future items)      | varies                    | permanent       | spell_damage (permanent)


=== NOTES ===

- total_spell_damage is a property on PlayerStats that dynamically sums all sources
- WMB bonus is computed live (reads current BKS each time), so BKS buffs apply correctly
- Spells = cast from ability menu, is_spell=True. Melee abilities are NOT spells.
- Staff Bolt is a weapon ranged attack, not a spell
- Graffiti Gun shock bolt is utility (applies Shocked only), not a damage ability
- Fart (formerly Lesser Cloudkill) is NOT a spell — does not benefit from TSD
- Discharge is channeled (6 ticks, 3 damage pulses) — excluded from Spell Echo
- Ray of Frost is channeled (3 ticks) — excluded from Spell Echo
