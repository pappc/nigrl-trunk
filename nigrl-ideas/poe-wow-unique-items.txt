================================================================================
POE / WOW INSPIRED UNIQUE ITEMS — BATCH 1 (25 items)
Design philosophy: Path of Exile rule-breaking uniques + WoW proc/stacking
legendaries. Every item has a TRULY build-defining mechanic with real tradeoffs.
================================================================================

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY NOTES
------------------------
Path of Exile inspiration used here:
  - Rule inversion: "your crits do not deal extra damage BUT..."
  - Conversion: one stat type feeds another completely
  - Cannot + powerful compensation ("Cannot be healed; gain 20% DR per turn")
  - Threshold bonuses kicking in at a breakpoint
  - Items that make previously-bad stats attractive
  - Build-enabling uniques that unlock a playstyle from scratch

World of Warcraft inspiration used here:
  - RPPM (real procs per minute): meaningful proc rates on specific triggers
  - Stacking "Azerite" style buffs that build to a threshold explosion
  - Execute phase bonuses (below 20%/25%/35% HP)
  - Cooldown transformation windows (e.g., combo point → execute window)
  - Set-bonus logic adapted to single items (conditional second effect)
  - Resource conversion (energy/meth/tox as alternate power sources)

ITEM 01 — GLASS CANNON
================================================================================
Slot:     weapon (stabbing)
Char:     / (ASCII)
Color:    (220, 240, 255) — pale ice-blue
Tags:     ["glass_cannon", "unique"]
Zones:    []
Item ID:  glass_cannon

POE Inspiration: Presence of Chayula — replaces a fundamental defensive layer with
an equally fundamental offensive one. "Cannot be stunned" + "20% of damage taken
gained as Energy Shield." Here: no physical armor, but attacks deal bonus damage
equal to your missing armor.

CONCEPT SUMMARY
A slender glass shiv ground from a lab flask. Zero armor of its own, and wearing
it REMOVES all armor from every other equipment source. Your total max_armor is
forced to 0 while this is equipped. In exchange: each melee hit deals bonus damage
equal to 1 + (armor_you_would_have_had / 4), where "would-have-had" is tracked as
a hidden field glass_cannon_suppressed_armor on the item entity. This converts your
entire defensive investment into raw offense, but strips you of the attrition buffer.

MECHANICAL SPECIFICATION
- base_damage: 12
- str_req: 8
- weapon_type: "stabbing"
- str_scaling: {"type": "tiered", "divisor": 2}
- reach: 1
- Tag: "glass_cannon"
- On equip: record engine.player_stats.permanent_armor_bonus + sum of armor_bonus from
  all currently worn neck/feet/hat as weapon.glass_cannon_suppressed_armor; set a
  glass_cannon_active flag on engine. While active, _compute_player_max_armor() returns
  0 (all armor_bonus contributions ignored). Deal bonus flat damage each melee hit =
  max(0, weapon.glass_cannon_suppressed_armor // 4).
- On unequip: clear flag, restore normal armor computation.

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES
- New engine flag: glass_cannon_equipped (bool). Set in inventory_mgr.py on equip.
- Modify _compute_player_max_armor() to return 0 if glass_cannon_equipped.
- In handle_attack(), after base damage roll, add glass_cannon_bonus if flag is set.
- Suppressed armor snapshot taken at equip time; updates if player equips/unequips
  other items while Glass Cannon is in weapon slot.

BALANCE CONSIDERATIONS
- Typical neck+hat armor contribution: 30-80 armor. At 60 armor: +15 flat damage.
  That is massive on a base-12 stabbing weapon at STR 10 (total ~20 base).
- The player completely forgoes the Temp HP → Armor → HP damage absorption chain.
  Any direct hit leaks straight to HP. High-risk, high-reward.
- Pairs with: healing sustain (Bone Club vampiric doesn't stack here), Iron Lung
  strain (tox armor conversion), status effect immunity builds.
- Anti-synergy: L Farming L4 "Armor Up" becomes useless (armor=0 disables it).

TRADEOFF SUMMARY
UPSIDE: potentially +10-25 flat damage per hit from armor conversion.
DOWNSIDE: zero armor, making even light hits pierce directly to HP.

ITEM 02 — BORROWED TIME
================================================================================
Slot:     ring
Char:     o (ASCII)
Color:    (80, 60, 140) — deep purple-violet
Tags:     ["borrowed_time", "unique"]
Zones:    []
Item ID:  borrowed_time_ring

POE Inspiration: Replica Kaom's Heart — "You have no mana regeneration; your life
regenerates at 2% per second." Converts an otherwise useless resource into something
meaningful. Here: your HP regenerates passively, but kills are the only source of
energy recovery.

CONCEPT SUMMARY
An amethyst ring carved with hourly ticks. While equipped, the player regenerates
5 HP per turn passively (via a permanent floor-duration HoT effect). However, the
player's energy no longer accumulates passively — their energy refills ONLY from
kills. Each kill grants a flat 120 energy (enough for 1.2 actions). This completely
restructures engagement: the player must keep killing to take actions.

MECHANICAL SPECIFICATION
- stat_bonus: {} (no direct stat changes)
- Tag: "borrowed_time"
- On equip: apply a permanent floor-duration "borrowed_time_hot" effect to the player
  (5 HP/turn). This effect also sets a flag engine.borrowed_time_equipped = True.
- Modify _run_energy_loop(): when borrowed_time_equipped is True, players do NOT gain
  energy from the base tick distribution. Instead, when an entity_died event fires
  with killer=engine.player, grant engine.player.energy += 120.
- On unequip: remove the "borrowed_time_hot" effect; clear the engine flag; restore
  normal energy accumulation.

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES
- New Effect subclass "BorrowedTimeHotEffect" (id="borrowed_time_hot"): heals 5/turn,
  floor_duration=True, never expires unless ring is unequipped.
- In _run_energy_loop(), gate the player energy tick behind `not borrowed_time_equipped`.
- In the entity_died event handler in engine.py: if killer == player and
  borrowed_time_equipped: player.energy += 120.
- If player is stunned and can't kill: they literally cannot act. Maximum tension.

BALANCE CONSIDERATIONS
- 5 HP/turn is significant (at 130 max HP: ~4% per turn). Replaces all healing
  consumable need over a long floor.
- 120 energy per kill = just over 1 action per kill. With melee this means
  kill → attack → need another kill to attack again. Forces efficient routing.
- On very sparse floors or boss rooms with no trash: catastrophic energy starve.
- Pairs well with: fast-moving Boots builds (spring/stride), area control.
- Anti-synergy: any "wait and heal" strategy is now energy-negative.

TRADEOFF SUMMARY
UPSIDE: 5 HP/turn passive regen (never need to use healing items).
DOWNSIDE: cannot accumulate energy without kills — standing still = frozen.

  If yes: damage = max(base_damage) * 3, no defense subtracted (deal_damage skips

  If no: damage = min(base_damage), no crit possible (is_crit forced False).

  Boss enemies with 200+ HP: still takes 3-4 shots. Not a complete trivialization.
- Pairs with: melee+sidearm combo builds, Sniping tree (L3 mega crit below threshold
  would be absurd at 48 * 4 = 192 dmg).
- Anti-synergy: pure gun builds that want consistent damage above threshold.

TRADEOFF SUMMARY
UPSIDE: massive execute burst below 35% HP, defense-ignoring.
DOWNSIDE: almost no damage against healthy targets — cannot open fights with it.

PoE Inspiration: Replica Atziri's Mirror — reflects a portion of damage back.

CONCEPT SUMMARY
A crown of crushed empty beer cans duct-taped together. Grants +3 TOL and -3 SWG.

MECHANICAL SPECIFICATION

  empties_equipped and damage >= 10, multiply damage by 0.70 (30% reduction), then
  apply a random debuff (1: slow dur 5; 2: confuse dur 4; 3: stun dur 2) to the
  attacker if it is a monster within melee range. The proc fires at most once per
  hit (not once per tick).

  but before deal_damage(), check for empties_equipped tag and damage >= 10.
  Apply the 30% reduction inline; then apply_effect to attacker.
- The -3 SWG means swagger defense drops by ~1-2 points (swagger 8→5: defense -2).

  tuned as above.

  The -3 SWG from 8 → 5 reduces defense from 0 to -2 (net -2 melee defense).
  The +15 armor partially covers this, but the swap is: swagger defense for TOL power.
- 30% damage reduction on big hits (10+): significant against hard-hitting bosses.
  Against weak trash (5-8 damage each): no proc, armor does most of the work.
- Pairs with: Iron Lung strain (tox armor conversion), Tolerance-scaling builds.

TRADEOFF SUMMARY
UPSIDE: +3 TOL, 30% damage reduction + debuff proc on heavy hits, +15% tox resist.
DOWNSIDE: -3 SWG means -1 to -2 melee defense depending on base.

================================================================================
Slot:     neck
Char:     " (same as chains)

Tags:     ["preachers_belt", "unique"]
Zones:    []
Item ID:  preachers_belt

PoE Inspiration: Coward's Trial — inverts a penalty into a mechanic. Here: the
player's toxicity, normally a harmful status, now deals reflected damage to attackers.

CONCEPT SUMMARY
A cracked leather belt worn as a necklace. The player gains +1 melee damage for
every 20 toxicity they carry (rounded down). Additionally, when a monster deals melee
damage to the player, the monster receives toxicity damage equal to 10% of the
player's current toxicity (minimum 1). Encourages players to deliberately carry high
toxicity as a weapon.

MECHANICAL SPECIFICATION
- armor_bonus: 0
- defense_bonus: 2
- stat_bonus: {}
- tox_resistance: -20  (takes 20% MORE toxicity — feeds the mechanic)
- Tag: "preachers_belt"
- On equip: set engine.preachers_belt_equipped = True.
- Passive damage bonus: in _compute_player_attack_power(), if preachers_belt_equipped:
  atk_power += engine.player.toxicity // 20
- Tox thorns: in handle_attack(), when defender == engine.player and preachers_belt_equipped
  and attacker is a monster: add_toxicity(engine, attacker, max(1, engine.player.toxicity // 10))

- add_toxicity already exists in combat.py. Call it from handle_attack() after
  deal_damage() to attacker (monster that just hit the player).
- Toxicity//20 damage bonus: at 100 tox = +5 damage, at 200 tox = +10 damage.
  This is meaningful scaling without being absurd.
- -20% tox resistance means tox sources hit harder (more gain = more bonus).
- The tox-thorns synergizes with Chemical Warfare XP gain.

  incoming damage multiplication already, so the tox thorns reflect ALSO more.
- Pairs with: Iron Lung (dump tox → convert to armor), Nigle Fart, Chemical Warfare tree.
- Anti-synergy: White Power tree (purging tox removes your damage bonus).

TRADEOFF SUMMARY
UPSIDE: tox-to-melee conversion + tox thorns on hit.
DOWNSIDE: -20 tox resistance (you take 20% more toxicity — feeding the engine).

- Pairs with: Kushenheimer (BKS spell synergy), Book-Smarts ring stacking.

TRADEOFF SUMMARY
UPSIDE: strong BKS-to-melee conversion + crit feedback loop.
DOWNSIDE: no STR scaling at all; low critters get mediocre damage.

ITEM 07 — ACCOUNTABILITY MIRROR
================================================================================
Slot:     ring
Char:     o (ASCII)
Color:    (200, 200, 200) — silver-white
Tags:     ["accountability_mirror", "unique"]
Zones:    []
Item ID:  accountability_mirror

PoE Inspiration: Replica Soul Tether — converts a damage type into a different
resource entirely. Here: the ring makes all DAMAGE YOU TAKE convert to ability
charges rather than HP loss, but only up to a cap before the HP damage resumes.

CONCEPT SUMMARY
A small hand mirror you wear as a ring. Once per floor, when you take damage, the
first 30 points of damage that turn are absorbed and converted into ability charges
(+1 charge for each 10 damage absorbed, distributed to your lowest-charge ability).
After the 30-point charge budget is spent, all further damage hits HP normally.
Resets each new floor. While the budget is available: incoming damage triggers the
charge conversion instead of the damage pipeline.

MECHANICAL SPECIFICATION
- stat_bonus: {}
- Tag: "accountability_mirror"
- New engine state: accountability_mirror_budget (int, reset to 30 each floor change)
- On equip: initialize budget to 30 if not set.
- In modify_incoming_damage (via a new AccountabilityEffect that is applied on equip):
  if budget > 0: absorb = min(damage, budget); budget -= absorb; damage -= absorb;
  find lowest-charge non-INFINITE ability in engine.abilities; grant charges = absorb//10.
  If no abilities with charges: no charge grant (budget still depletes).
- On floor change: reset accountability_mirror_budget = 30.

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES
- Cleanest as a permanent Effect subclass applied on equip. The budget tracking lives
  on the engine (not the effect itself) since it needs floor-reset logic in
  engine's floor-transition code.
- New effect: "AccountabilityMirrorEffect" (id="accountability_mirror_eff"):
  floor_duration=True; its modify_incoming_damage checks engine.accountability_mirror_budget.
- Edge case: at budget=0 this ring is purely decorative until next floor.
- Multiple rings: only one Accountability Mirror can be equipped (unique tag prevents
  duplication in ring replacement logic).

BALANCE CONSIDERATIONS
- 30 hp-to-charges per floor. If the player has a PER_FLOOR ability with max_charges=3
  (e.g., Burst from Gatting): at 30 dmg absorbed → 3 extra charges per floor free.
  Against abilities with large max_charges: meaningless (1-3 extra).
- Primarily useful for TOTAL charge abilities or expensive PER_FLOOR abilities.
- No stat bonus means you're trading a ring slot for a conditional charge mechanic.
  Worth it for ability-heavy builds; weak for melee-only builds with no abilities.
- Anti-synergy: builds with only INFINITE abilities (melee, smoking) gain zero.

TRADEOFF SUMMARY
UPSIDE: up to 30 damage absorbed into ability charges per floor.
DOWNSIDE: no stats; charges only generated once per floor budget; useless without charges.
