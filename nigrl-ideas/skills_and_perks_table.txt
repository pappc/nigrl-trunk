XP MECHANICS
  - Potential XP:  Earned passively by performing skill-related actions.
  - Real XP:       Converted from potential XP by spending Skill Points.
  - Skill Points:  Gained as a fraction of potential XP, scaled by Book Smarts.
  - SP gain rate:  min(0.5, 0.1 + 0.3 * sqrt(book_smarts / 80))
                   bksmt=0  → 0.10  |  bksmt=8  → ~0.19  |  bksmt=50 → ~0.34

PERK TYPES
  [PASSIVE]    — Always-on passive effect; no activation required.
  [STAT]       — Permanent stat bonus applied on unlock.
  [ACTIVATED]  — Grants or unlocks an active ability.
  [PLACEHOLDER]— Not yet designed; slot reserved for future content.
