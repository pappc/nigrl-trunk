- `nigrl-ideas/unique-item-research-v2.txt` (v2, 2026-03-26) — Deep PoE (ascendancies, leagues, gems, auras, Watcher's Eye, cluster jewels), deep WoW (tier sets, trinket RPPM, Dragonflight, classic items, enchants, PvP, M+ affixes), full LoL exploration (mythics, legendaries, ADC/assassin/mage items, champion passives, summoner spells, runes)

Five categories of interesting unique effects:
A. STACKING — do X repeatedly for permanent/temp power (Veigar, Nasus, Conqueror)
B. CONDITIONAL — while condition X, gain buff (Watcher's Eye, Crown, Banshee's)
C. KILL CHAINS — on kill, gain benefit that helps next kill (Duskblade, Tailwind)
D. ACTIVE ABILITIES — use item for powerful one-time effect (Zhonya's, Galeforce)
E. SYSTEM TRANSFORMATION — change how a fundamental mechanic works (Rabadon's, Reservation)

Turn-based conversion: attack speed = energy cost/gain, dash = teleport tiles, cooldown seconds = cooldown turns, RPPM = % chance per hit, shield = temp armor/HP.

Top v2 proposals (Tier 1):
- Ghostblade (assassin chain-kill stealth weapon)
- Bounty Hunter's Badge (quest mark on every floor, permanent stacking)
- Shape-Shifter's Ring (transforms based on playstyle at 50 kills)
- Executioner's Machete (bleed stacks to execute loop with reset)
- Blood Oath Ring (missing HP heal, clutch moments)

Slot balance note: rings are over-represented (25+ proposals for 5 slots). Future design should prioritize hats and feet.
