Full research covering CDDA's mutation system for NIGRL's mutation overhaul expansion from 80 to 100 mutations.

## Key Findings

### CDDA System Mechanics
- **Mutation Conflicts**: Explicit conflict pairs (Claws vs Tentacles, Fur vs Scales vs Bark). New mutation REPLACES conflicting old one.
- **Mutation Chains**: Claws -> Talons -> Retractable Talons. Each step replaces previous. Progressive evolution along a theme.
- **Purifier**: Removes one random mutation. Cannot remove post-threshold mutations. Chimera mutations are purification-immune.
- **Serum System**: Category-specific mutagens (Feline Mutagen/Serum). Serum = targeted, Mutagen = weighted random. Consumption adds to threshold counter.
- **Threshold**: 5-7 doses of same category crosses threshold. Post-threshold grants unique powerful bonus. Permanent and unpurifiable.
- **Instability**: Each mutation has instability score (1-5). High total instability causes spontaneous mutations, pain events, hallucinations.

### 10 Design Gaps Identified in NIGRL's 80 Mutations
1. Terrain creation/modification (no mutation creates tiles)
2. Enemy behavior manipulation beyond aggro
3. Item transformation/interaction beyond consumption
4. Sound/noise mechanics
5. Corpse/death event interaction
6. Player repositioning/forced movement
7. Vision changes beyond FOV (+/-)
8. Conditional mode-switching/stances
9. Projectile/gun modification
10. Semi-autonomous appendages

### Most NIGRL-Applicable CDDA Patterns
- **Web Weaver/Slime Trail**: Leave functional terrain behind you (adapt as grease trail mutation)
- **Spore Cloud death-spawn**: Enemies killed spawn temporary allies (adapt as Parasitic Spores)
- **Roots immobilization tradeoff**: Anchor self for power boost but can't move
- **Ink Cloud**: Area blindness that works both ways
- **Chromatophores**: Stealth toggle requiring stillness
- **Slime Hands**: Dissolve items for nutrition (adapt as Metabolic Furnace)
- **Tentacle grab at range**: Grapple mechanics for adjacent-tile immobilization
- **Chimera instability**: Random mutations from all categories, can't be purified

### 20 New Mutation Proposals (Summarized)
- Viscous Trail (Weak Good): Deliberate grease trail, immune to own grease
- Detonation Glands (Strong Good): Melee kills create corpse bombs
- Pheromone Confusion (Weak Good): Redirect enemies to attack each other
- Parasitic Spores (Strong Good): Kills spawn temporary Spore Husk allies
- Kinetic Recoil (Weak Good): Bounce backward after melee, wall-brace bonus
- Anchor Growth (Strong Bad): Involuntary immobilization cycle with DR bonus
- Corrosive Touch (Weak Bad): Items dissolve on pickup
- Metabolic Furnace (Strong Good): Destroy inventory items for HP
- Berserker Cortex (Strong Good): Involuntary berserker mode with forced movement
- Split Consciousness (Strong Bad): Reversed movement controls periodically
- Bioelectric Rounds (Weak Good): Gun attacks apply shocked stacks
- Tremor Rounds (Weak Bad): Gun recoil knockback and noise alert
- Autonomous Tendril (Strong Good): Auto-attacking appendage
- Plague Carrier (Strong Bad): Walking disease aura, contagion on death
- Sonic Screech (Weak Good): Stun+pushback ability, but alerts distant enemies
- Carrion Magnetism (Weak Bad): Corpses attract and buff enemies
- Temporal Stutter (Strong Good): 8-turn tactical rewind ability
- Infrared Bloom (Weak Bad): See enemies through walls but items are unidentifiable
- Hive Resonance (Strong Bad): Same-type enemies share damage pool
- Root Grip (Strong Good): Immobilize adjacent enemy, root network damage sharing
