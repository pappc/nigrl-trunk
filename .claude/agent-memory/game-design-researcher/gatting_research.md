Gatting skill tree research completed. 15 games surveyed for rapid-fire/spray mechanics: Borderlands, Enter the Gungeon, DOOM Eternal, Tarkov, CoD Zombies, Warframe, Deep Rock Galactic, Risk of Rain 2, Remnant, Fallout, XCOM, Synthetik, Vampire Survivors, Nuclear Throne, Hades.

**Why:** Gatting is NIGRL's fast-fire gun skill (spray and pray). Existing L1-L3 trunk: Locked In (+1 dmg/consecutive hit), Doin' It Sideways (-10% acc, -10 energy), Gun Crit. Need 10 branching tree designs at L4+ for build diversity.

**How to apply:** Research document at `nigrl-ideas/gatting-rapid-fire-research.txt`. Summary at `nigrl-ideas/summaries/gatting-rapid-fire-research-summary.txt`. 12 branch archetypes identified (Furnace/Suppressor/Bullet Storm/Ricochet/Status Hose/Turret/Shredder/Chain Killer/Ammo Alchemist/Detonator/Panic Shooter/Iron Curtain). Key design insight: in turn-based, rapid fire = rolling more dice per action, not holding a button. Suppression and miss conversion are uniquely powerful in NIGRL because fast mode has inherently low accuracy. Companion branching tree designs document is the next step.
